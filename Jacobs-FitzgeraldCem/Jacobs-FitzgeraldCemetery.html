JACOBS-FITZGERALD (B. H.) CEMETERY, SANTA FE

(Also called JACOBS CEMETERY.) Go 1/4 miles west of Wesley's Chapel on Santa F. Pike, turn north on Tom Fitzgerald Rd. Go 1 mile, to where T. V. A. transmission line Crosses road. Cemetery is located behind the Steve Williams house on left.

(They Passed This Way, Page 8-47, 1968.); (Maury County Tennessee Cemeteries, P 221, 1987)
