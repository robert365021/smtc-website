ROACH CEMETERY, JACKSON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Roach Cemetery. Coordinates in the center are 34.81488 X -85.93058.

From Hwy 72 going NW toward South Pittsburg go thru Stevenson and over the Backwaters of the Tennessee River to County Road 42 and turn left. Follow that to Fackler and cross the RR tracks continuing on CR 42 about another 1 and 1/2 miles. Turn right or north on CR 55 and go about 1/4 mile and look right and you will see the cemetery as a clump of trees out in the field. Go to the field/Cemetery Road and turn right and back to the cemetery. That is public access.
