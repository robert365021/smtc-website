CROSS (Maclin) CEMETERY, Stevenson, JACKSON COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Clark Cross Cemetery. Coordinates in the center are 34.829532 X -85.861418.

From Scottsboro Hwy 72 going NW toward South Pittsburg/I-24 go thru Hollywood and cross over the Backwaters of the Tennessee River to about a mile before the second crossing of the backwaters of the Tennessee River and look to your right at the cemetery on the south bank of Hwy 72 where you will see the white marble markers behind a wrought iron fence.
