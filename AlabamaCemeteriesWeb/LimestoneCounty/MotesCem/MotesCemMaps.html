MOTES CEMETERY (Coggins Road), N.E. LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Motes Cemetery GPS is a common term for the Latitude and Longitude. Location: SE quarter of SE quarter, Sect. 14, T1S, R3W. 

From Athens Alabama travel east on Hwy Hwy 251 (old hwy 31) to Bethel Road. Turn right there and go about a mile to Old School House Road and travel that for about a mile to Coggins Road and turn left (or West) and go 200 yards. The cemetery is on the bank of the north side of the road almost in the back yard of a residence. 
This aerial view above uses a yellow pointer to show the cemetery located inside a fence next to Coggins Road.
