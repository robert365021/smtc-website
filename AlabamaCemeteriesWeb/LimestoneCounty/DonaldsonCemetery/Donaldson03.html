DONALDSON CEMETERY, (north bank of Hwy. 72 W.), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

Nancy Donaldson memorial. We are facing east. This is a bit cluttered looking, with a large cedar stump being in the middle of the grave along with a broken box tomb top and broken upright stone in front of the cedar stump. Believe the round upright stone is the footstone of William M. Donaldson. It has the initials W.M.D on it. This stone is the first one in the row of three slabs facing east/west in a north/south row.

The broken memorial for Nancy Donaldson. This is part of the top of the boxtomb of Nancy Donaldson.


DONALDSON, Nancy, 31 May 1795 - 21 Jun 1859. (wife of William M. Donaldson.)
Inscribed as: "Nancey Donaldson -  Born 31 May 175 - Died 21 Jun 1859"
