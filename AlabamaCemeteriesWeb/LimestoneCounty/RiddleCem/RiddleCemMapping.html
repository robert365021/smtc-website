RIDDLE CEMETERY, Ripley Rd., LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Riddle Cemetery aka Henderson- Riddle Cemetery as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location.
ACME Mapper 2.1: N 34.75927 W -87.09614

From Athens go west on Hwy 72 to 7 Mile Post Road and turn south. Follow that to the Ripley Rd and turn right and go about 1/4 mile and look for the road back into the cemetery on the north (right) side of the road.

This aerial view uses a yellow line to outline the general perimeters of the cemetery.
 Maps from ACME Mapper 2.1, MS Streets & Trips, & Google modified for this site by Wayne Austin 16 Nov 2015.
