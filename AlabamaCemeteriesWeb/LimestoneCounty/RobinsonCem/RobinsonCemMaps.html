ROBINSON CEMETERY, (Robinson Road, northern area), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This aerial view shows the GPS of the Robinson Cemetery. 

This road map show the cemetery on Robinson Rd. It can be accessed traveling out of Elkmont on Hwy 49 to Robinson Road nd turn right. Go until the road gets to its furtherest point northward and the cemetery is out in the field north up the hedgerow about 400 yards across the creek. You will get your feet wet unless you go into the house lane just east of there for permission.

With this aerial view we can see the hedge row where the cemetery is located.
