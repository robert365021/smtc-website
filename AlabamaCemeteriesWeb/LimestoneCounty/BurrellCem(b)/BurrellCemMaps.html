BURRELL CEMETERY (Lucas Ferry Rd.), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Burrell Cemetery. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location.

From Hwy 72 in the western part of Athens turn south on Lucas Ferry Road (County Road #45) & go about 2 & 3/4 miles and the cemetery is on the left next to the road in a grove of trees.
Alternate Location:SW quarter of NW quarter, Sect. 30, T3S, R4W on Lucas Ferry Rd.   

This aerial view uses a red check to point out the cemetery located under the trees next to the road on the east side of Lucas Ferry Road.
