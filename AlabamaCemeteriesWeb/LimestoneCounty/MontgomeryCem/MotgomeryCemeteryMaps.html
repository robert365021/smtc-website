MONTGOMERY CEMETERY (down the road from New Garden Cem.), LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS location of the Montgomery Cemetery in Limestone County Alabama. Alternate Location: Township 2 South, Range 4 West, Section 4, Northeast Quarter.

 Directions to the Montgomery Cemetery Take I-65 to Exit no 361 and go west on Sandlin Road to Hayes Mill Road. Go more than a mile and turn left on Hayes Mill or New Garden Road. At the New Garden Cemetery go down New Garden Road east until the woodland clears and the cemetery is about 200 yards south of the road into the woodland. This cemetery is in the edge of the woodland facing an open field looking east.

With this Aerial View we see the New Garden Road which becomes County Road 98 going east. You can park in the church parking lot, scale the bank across the road and follow the opening in the woodland to the cemetery. You must keep the eastern edge of the woods in your view as you scan for the two stones and one box tomb to the east (or your left).
