LUCAS CEMETERY, (Backwaters of Tennessee River west of Hwy 31) LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Lucas Cemetery as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper: N 34.67486 X W 86.00906. Alternate Location:Center of Sect. 23, T45, R5W on the backwaters of the Tennessee River.

From Athens go sout on Hwy 31 almost to Calhoun Community College and turn right on Laughmiller Rd and go about 3 miles to Lucas Ferry Road and turn left (south) and follow that until it get to be a gravel road and look to your right out into a field at a clump of trees and there will be a road that goes to your right and beside the cemetery as long as it is dry and not subject to bogging down.
This aerial view uses a red check to show the cemetery located under a few trees out in a field. On either side of you is the backwater lake of the Tennessee River.
