NEW OAKLAND BAPTIST CHURCH CEMETERY (Old Elkmont Rd.), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the New Oakland Baptist Church Cemetery. GPS is common terminology for the Latitude and Longitude N 34.86172 W 86.98254.

From Athens Alabama travel North on North Jefferson St and turn left (west) on Cross Key Road and go about 1/4 miles and turn (right) or north on Old Elkmont Road. Travel about a 1/2 miles and the cemetery is on your left marked by a large oval sign and tall black metal ornamental fence. This cemetery was named the Malone Cemetery on this map. Why? I am not sure.  
This aerial view above shows the cemetery marked with a red check and well laid out with internal road. It is inside a metal fence and set up for expansion.
