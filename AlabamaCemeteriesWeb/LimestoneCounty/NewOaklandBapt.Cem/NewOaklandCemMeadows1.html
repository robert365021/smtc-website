MEADOWS, William "Bill", Nov 21, 1954 - Oct 13, 2015,  h/o Vickie Meadows; s/o Marvin and Mildred Meadows, Sr., Retired from Athens Utilities.

McConnell Funeral Home, Athens, AL, obituary: William "Bill" Meadows

The Funeral Service for William (Bill) Meadows age 60, of Elkmont, AL; will be Thursday, October 15, 2015 at 2:00 p.m. at McConnell Memorial Chapel with Anthony Anderson and Dwight Rayburn officiating.
Burial will be New Oakland Cemetery.
Visitation will be Thursday, October 15, 2015: 12-1:45 p.m. at McConnell Funeral Home.
Mr. Meadows passed away Tuesday October 13, 2015 at his residence.
He was born November 21, 1954 to Marvin and Mildred Meadows, Sr.
Mr. Meadows is preceded in death by his parents, and one brother. He retired from Athens Utilities.
He is survived by his loving wife Vickie Meadows of 40 years of Elkmont, AL; one son Dusty Meadows, one brother Donald Meadows, two sisters Becky Adams, and Dianne Hall & husband Glenn, one grandson Luke Cooper Meadows.
Pallbearers will be David Jones, Lynn Haynes, Blair Davis, Danny Wallace, Bradley Young, and Richard Johnson.
