SYLONE CEMETERY (Upper Fort Hampton Road), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Sylone Cemetery. GPS is also known as the Latitude and Longitude. It is misspelled as Cylone Cem

From Elkmont travel north and then east out 120 Upper Fort Hampton Road for about 5 miles and find the cemetery adjacent to the road on the right across a white board fence. 
This aerial view above shows the cemetery located in the white outline. You will climb a small rise and the road begins to curve right and there on the right is the cemetery. There is a circular road into it as can be seen here.
