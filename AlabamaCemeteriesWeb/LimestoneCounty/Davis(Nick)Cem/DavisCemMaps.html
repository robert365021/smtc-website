NICK DAVIS MONUMENT &  CEMETERY, (Nick Davis Road), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Nick Davis Monument & Cemetery.

Go west from Huntsville Alabama and turn north on Old Rail Road Bed Road. Go about 1 mile and turn left or east on Capshaw Road and go thru Capshaw and turn back north or right on Sanderson Road. Follow that for 2+ miles and turn west or left on Nick Davis Road. After 1/2 mile look for the black historical marker on the left bank of the road just in front of the Limestone Correctional facility. The monument is nestled in a pine tree grove.

This aerial image shows the cemetery back from the road under the trees on the left going west.
