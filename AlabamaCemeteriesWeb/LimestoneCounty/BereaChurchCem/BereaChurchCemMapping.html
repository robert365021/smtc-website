BEREA CEMETERY, SW junction of Elk River Mills & Tillman Mills Rds., LIMESTONE COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Berea Church Cemetery as plotted by C. Wayne Austin using ACME Mapper 2.1. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. N 34.85397 W -87.05668, Alternate location: SE quarter, Sect. 21, T2S, R5W

From Athens go northwest on Hwy 99 aka Buck Island Road several miles to Elk River Mills Road and turn left. Follow that for 1/4 mile and turn into the cemetery road. There is a cemetery sign at that road. It is a field road but plays out before it gets to the cemetery. Go toward the grove of trees that contain the cemetery about 400 yards from the road.

This aerial view shows the cemetery inside a clump of trees. I have made an attempt to divide it up by heritage but I do not know the heritage of the area surveyed on the left or 3rd but think it is Caucasian even if it is outside the fence. The above map is solely an attempt to display the prominent clusters of graves and not any other type of division of the graveyard perimeters.
