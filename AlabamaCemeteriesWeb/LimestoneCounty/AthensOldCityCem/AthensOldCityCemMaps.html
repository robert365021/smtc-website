ATHENS - OLD CITY CEMETERY. (Athens Alabama) LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA.

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of 34.8025 X 86.9664 W for the Athens - Old City Cemetery commonly known as the Latitude and Longitude.

Athens - Old City Cemetery is found on E. Washington Street on the north side of the street in the 300 block, just west of the Athens Board of Education. Look for the historical markers.   
This aerial view zeros in on the location (with a red check) south of Spring Park - next south of the Tennis courts.
