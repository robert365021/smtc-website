JONES-DONNELL aka HUNDLEY CEMETERY (Greenbrier, Old Hwy 20), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Jones-Donnell aka Hundley Cemetery. GPS is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location.

From Athens travel I-65 south to I-565 and take that several miles east. Leave that at Exit 3 and go about 2 miles north or left on Greenbrier Road to Greenbrier. Just as you come into town look left and during the winter, at least, you can see the tall tombstones behind the fence.

This aerial view above shows the cemetery located in the small fenced area over next to a machinery storage area.
