HARRISON CEMETERY, (Behind Burgreen's Gin, Browns Ferry Rd) LIMESTONE, COUNTY ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Harrison Cemetery as plotted by Topozone/C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper #2:N 34.72828 W 86.79876 behind Burgreen's Gin on Browns Ferry Road. ALTERNATIVE LOCATION: NE quarter of NW quarter, Sect. 1, T4S, R3W

From Athens go south on Hwy 31 to Browns Ferry Road and turn (left) east. Travel that past Mooresville Road to Burgreen's Gin several miles nearly to the intersection of Burgreen & Browns Ferry Rds. The cemetery is behind the long equipment building on your right in a small tree thicket. The cemetery usually hidden from the road by an old pile of cotton hulls, a trailer and the equipment building.

This aerial view shows the cemetery inside a clump of Hackberry trees south of Browns-Ferry Road marked here with a red check. It is likewise west of Burgreen Rd CR 87.
