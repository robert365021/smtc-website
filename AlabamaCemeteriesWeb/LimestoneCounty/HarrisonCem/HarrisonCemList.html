This is what Linda Smith in her book said about this cemetery: "On Brown's Ferry Rd. at Burgreen's Gin. At one time, there was a low concrete block wall around this cemetery but it is partially knocked down and surrounded by mounds of cotton hulls from the gin. All names appear on one stone which was placed there by family members some years ago. Copied Mar. 21, 1991, Linda Smith."

This Cemetery presentation is based on the photography of Wayne Austin on 28 Nov 2012. Uploaded here by C. Wayne Austin of Madison Alabama on 4 Dec 2012. This cemetery was also presented in the book Limestone County Alabama Cemeteries by Linda H. Smith, 1991. It is now presented also on findagrave.com, but the map is about 200 feet off.
