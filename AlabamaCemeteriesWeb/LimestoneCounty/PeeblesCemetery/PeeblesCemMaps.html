PEEBLES CEMETERY (Mooresville, Pryor Rd), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Peebles Cemetery. GPS is also known as the Latitude and Longitude.

From Athens travel east on Hwy 72 and turn south on Mooresville Road. Go to Mooresville and take old highway 20 eastward to Pryor Road and turn south and go about 1 mile and you will see the grain silos on the left and the cemetery across the road sprawled back to your right behind a chain link fence. It is overgrown in Brush so be prepared for that. I went there a year ago and cleared some if it but it still needs work. 
This aerial view above shows the cemetery located in the small clump of trees across the road from the Grain Silos. A long span of fencing leads up to the road with a gate that could be locked if the owners so desired. The cemetery has been cared for in the past with ornamental plants that have been overrun by massive dense privy hedge which I cleared much of it but it remains a problem.
