DELAP-CLEMENT CEMETERY, (Ardmore, Cedar Hill Rd), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA


This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Delap-Clement Cemetery. GPS is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location.

From Ardmore go south on Hwy 53 toward Huntsville and turn right on Cedar Hill Road. Travel 3 tenths of a mile and  turn left into a residential road with houses along it. Go past all the houses about 400 yards and into the gate. The cemetery is in a field. The grass is allowed to grow up in the summer time making the stones not so easily found. The above map wrongly labels Cedar Hill Road as Elkwood Section Road. This road is Elkwood Section Road on the east side of Hwy 53.

This aerial view above shows the area of the cemetery highlighted in yellow. The graves are back in the northeast corner of that square shaped separately fenced field.
