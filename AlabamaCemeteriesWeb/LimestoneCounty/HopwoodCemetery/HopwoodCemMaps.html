HOPWOOD CEMETERY, (east of Ardmore), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map combined with the TOPOZONE mapping data shows the GPS of the Hopwood Cemetery.

Go east on Hwy 53 Ardmore Highway and merge left on State Line Road and look for the neighborhood sign shown in the photos on the right.

The yellow pointer denotes the location of the cemetery on the south side of State Line Road in the front yard of a condominium complex.

This map zooms in on the approximate area of the cemetery outlined in yellow. Though I may have it drawn larger than it is the cemetery is in that area across from the junction of Slaughter Pen Road.
