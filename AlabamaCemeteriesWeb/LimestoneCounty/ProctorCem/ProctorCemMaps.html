PROCTOR CEMETERY, Wales Road, LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates using ACME Mapper 2.0 for the Proctor Cemetery as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. The GPS is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location: N 34.73041 W -87.05786.
Alternate Location: NE quarter of section 4 and NE quarter of that square - Section 4, T3S, R5W.

On Interstate Highway 65 going north Exit off on #351 into Athens Alabama,
Turn left and go west on Highway 72. Go several miles to Browns Ferry Road and turn left there (southwest). (not Huntsville-Browns Ferry Rd.) Go several miles. Pass Round Island Baptist church and go approximately 1/2 mile further, turn left (south) on McCormick Road. Go to junction of McCormick Rd and Wales Road (438) and turn right into the driveway to Jerry Goode's house (2013). Turn left (south) in front of house into field road and go through the field approximately 1/4 mile south of the house passing a large chicken brooding (two house) operation. Immediately after passing the end of the last Chicken house turn right and the cemetery is on your left out in a field in a thicket of trees due south of you in a separate fenced area. Park south of the brooding operation and walk the final 75+ yards. This is approximately 8 miles south west of Athens Alabama.
