HARRIS CEMETERY, (Hwy 31 at Pryor House) LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Harris Cemetery as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper #2:N 34.67935 W 86.94535.

From Athens go south on Hwy 31 to the old Pryor Home and the cemetery is across the road east of that.

This aerial view shows the cemetery inside a clump of trees east of Hwy 31. Follow the road Flower Hill Way east until it turns north then in dry weather and with permission veer north east toward the clump of trees where the cemetery is located.
