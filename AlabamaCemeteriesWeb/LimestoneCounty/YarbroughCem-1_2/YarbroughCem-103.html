YARBROUGH CEMETERY #1, (NE of Athens 10 miles) , LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

South side of the Yarbrough Monument, but we are focusing on the north side now. This is what you will see if your eyes are quick as you blaze by on Hwy 72 going to Florence, Alabama from Huntsville, Alabama.

G. Fenner, & Mary Williamson & Archie N. Yarbrough, on the north side of the Yarbrough monument. That is a very busy highway Hwy 72 in the background.

YARBROUGH, Archie N./(or M.), died 1862. Military Marker says Archie M.
YARBROUGH, G. Fenner, died 1870.
YARBROUGH, Mary Williamson, died 5 Mar 1872.
 All on the north side of a large Yarbrough common memorial.
