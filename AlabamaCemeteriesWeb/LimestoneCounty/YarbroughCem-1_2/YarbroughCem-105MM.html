YARBROUGH CEMETERY #1, (NE of Athens 10 miles) , LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

David A. Yarbrough military marker beside a monument inscribed only as "MOTHER". Presumably it is for the wife of David A. Yarbrough who was known to be Martha A. Yarbrough.

David A Yarbrough military marker in front of a footstone inscribed only as David A. Yarbrough.

YARBROUGH, David A., 14 Aug 1840 - 21 Dec 1912. Military Marker: David A. Yarbrough, Pvt Co E., 9th Alabama Calvary, Confederates States Army, born 14 Aug 1840 - died 21 Dec 1912.
