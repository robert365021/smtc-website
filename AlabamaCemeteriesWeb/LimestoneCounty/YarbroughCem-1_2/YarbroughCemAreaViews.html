YARBROUGH CEMETERY #1, (NE of Athens 10 miles) , LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This is the main monument that has all the Yarbrough family inscriptions. We are looking east and Hwy 72 is on our left.

This photos shows the rows of graves running north/south. The vines are deep and visibility is difficult though.

We are looking northeast at the eastern row also covered by vines. In the distance is an old barn, in front of that next to the road is an old historic house that likely had connections to the folks that inhabit this graveyard.
 Photos by Wayne Austin added here 8 Dec 2009.
