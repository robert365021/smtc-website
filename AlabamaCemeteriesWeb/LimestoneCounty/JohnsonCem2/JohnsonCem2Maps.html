JOHNSON CEMETERY #2, (Mooresville Road), (East) LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Johnson Cemetery #2. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location.

Go east on Nick Davis Road to Mooresville Road and turn south (or right). Go several hundred yards and find the Johnson Cemetery #2 on the left on a rise above the road.  
This aerial view uses a red check to show the Johnson cemetery #2 located under a stand of trees on the west side of the road. It was kept up when I was there last.
