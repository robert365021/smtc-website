ANDERSON (Frances) CEMETERY (N. side Browns Ferry Rd) LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This map shows the general layout of this cemetery. With this map one should be able to dig up the tombs on the future if they become covered again as I found them. I estimate the length of this cemetery to be about 35 yards N/S and 10 yards E/W wide. That means the Anderson tomb is about 15 yards from the south end and the Robert Tomb is about 4 yards from the north end. The Jones Tomb is adjacent and just south of the Roberts Tomb with the Tinnin Tomb being about 15 yards south of that.
