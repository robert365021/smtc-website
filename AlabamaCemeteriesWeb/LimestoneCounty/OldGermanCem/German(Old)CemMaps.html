OLD GERMAN CEMETERY, Clement Gin Road, LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Old German Cemetery as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper: N 34.86762, W -86.85304. Alternate Location: Northeast quarter of South West quarter of Section 16, Township 2S, Range 3W on Clement Gin Road .3 miles west of Old Hwy. 31 N and west of Panther Branch.

From Athens go out old Hwy 31 aka Hwy 251 and just past Harvest Road on your right turn left (west) into Clement Gin Road. Turn into that and go 3 tenths of a mile and the cemetery is visible on your right next to the road.
Maps from ACME Mapper 2.0 and MS Streets & trips. modified for this site by Wayne Austin 21 Mar 2015.
