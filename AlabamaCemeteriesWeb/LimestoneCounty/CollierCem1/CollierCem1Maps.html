COLLIER CEMETERY #1, (Robinson Lane), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA


This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Collier Cemetery #1 . GPS is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location.

From Athens go out Buck Island Road (Hwy 99)  about 8 miles and turn right on Robinson Lane. Go about 1/2 mile until the road turns back north. About 50 yards back from that point turn north into the almost abandoned cemetery road going up the slope. Follow that road about 100 yards and the cemetery will be before you inside a chain link fence.
This aerial view above shows the cemetery pointed out with a red check under the tree line north and west of Robinson Road.
