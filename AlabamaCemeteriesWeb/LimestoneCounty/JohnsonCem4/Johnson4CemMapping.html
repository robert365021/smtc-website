JOHNSON CEMETERY # 4,  Williams Rd., LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Johnson Cemetery #4, as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper #2.1: N 34.84690 W 87.06633 Alternate location: NW quarter, Sect. 28. T2S. R5W, off Elk River Mills Rd. This cemetery is well-maintained and fenced.

From Athens go northwest out Buck Island Road (Hwy 99) several miles to Elk River Mills Road and turn left (west). Go about 1 1/2 miles and turn left again on Williams Road follow that straight into Johnson Cemetery Road and the cemetery is on the left.
