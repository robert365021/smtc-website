CAMBRIDGE CEMETERY, Cambridge Lane, LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates using ACME Mapper 2.1 for the Cambridge Cemetery as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location:  N 34.75771 W -86.89567.

From Athens take Hwy 72 and go east slightly more than two miles and turn right on Cambridge Lane. The cemetery is about a 1/2 mile down Cambridge Lane on the right.

This Google view shows the cemetery in more detail. There are some very old stones in this cemetery so exercise care in reading and cleaning them. They are soft limestone and will break and wear away. Some are already broken beyond reading.
Maps from ACME Mapper 2.1, MS Streets & Google modified for this site by Wayne Austin 10 Oct 2016.
