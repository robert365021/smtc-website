SHERBERT CEMETERY, New Cut Road, LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Sherbert Cemetery as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper 2.1: N 34.81650 W -87.12811. Alternate Location: NE quarter, Sect. 2, T3S, R6W. A 1915 TVA survey map showed this cemetery contained about 4 graves.

From Athens take Hwy 72 West less than 10 miles to the New Cut Road and turn right. Go about 1 mile and the road bends back to the right (east). After the bend look (left) far out into the field for the rock wall enclosure for the cemetery as seen in the below photo. Walk to the cemetery about 300 yards in the distance, but take care not to disturb the farmer's crop

This aerial view from Google shows (labeled with a yellow check) the cemetery in front of the woodland.

Here we see the rock wall in the distance.
