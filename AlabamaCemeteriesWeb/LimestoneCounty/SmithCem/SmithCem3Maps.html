SMITH CEMETERY#3 (Hwy 127 east side of Road), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Smith Cemetery #3. GPS is also known as the Latitude and Longitude.

From Athens travel north out Hwy 127  11 + miles. Just before the road bends sharply northwest there is a long residential road called Bucks Hollow Way to a house that is back into the area where the cemetery is found. You will be in the trees and traveling downhill on Hwy 127 at the turn off to the right into this long driveway. The driveway is under the trees, long and steep in places so travel with caution & keep to the left. Stop at the house for permission and the cemetery then can be found by going down into the creek valley past the house and across the creek. The cemetery is on your left as you are climbing a hill on the field road up from the creek. There is a gate into the field past the creek. You must go thru that with permission from the folks back at the mentioned house on the rise in the clearing. 
This aerial view above shows the cemetery located in the small clump of trees on a knoll up above the creek and cattle pond.

The house is shown just to the right of the Richard Martin Trail.
