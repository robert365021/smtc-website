GORDON CEMETERY, Batts Rd., LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Gordon Cemetery as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper 2.0: N 34.74808 W 87.14540. 
From Athens go west on Hwy 72 about 8 miles and turn left (south) on 24C (Hardy Rd.). Follow that for about 2 miles and turn right on Batts Road. Follow that for about 1.5 miles to Cates Road on the left and turn left. Basically it is a residential road. The cemetery is on that road on the left in clear view.
Maps from ACME Mapper 2.0, MS Streets & Google modified for this site by C. Wayne Austin 12 Jun 2014.
