HUDSON CEMETERY, (Holt-Spring Road - S of Hwy 72), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Hudson Cemetery. GPS is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location.

From Athens go out Hwy 72 west about 8 miles and turn south on Ezell Road. Go a couple of miles and the road terminates into Holt Spring Road. The cemetery is there across the road from that junction on the south side of the road.
This aerial view above shows the cemetery pointed out with a red check under the few trees , south of Holt Springer Road.
