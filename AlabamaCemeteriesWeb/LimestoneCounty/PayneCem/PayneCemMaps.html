PAYNE CEMETERY (north middle edge of county), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Payne Cemetery. GPS is also known as the Latitude and Longitude.

From Athens go out N. Jefferson St and veer right onto Hays Mill Road. Follow that across Thatch Road where it becomes Pettusville Road and follow that and you find the left turn onto Spence Cave Road. Spence Cave Rd winds and twist around, but just before the State Line look to the left under a grove of trees for the sign to the Payne Cemetery which is African-American. Along that road to the left is the Harlow Cemetery which is  a white cemetery and much smaller and older, but veer right and you will find the entrance into the Payne Cemetery.
This aerial view above shows the cemetery south and much bigger than the Harlow Cemetery. Both are pointed out with a red check along with a rough white outline of the cemetery premises.
