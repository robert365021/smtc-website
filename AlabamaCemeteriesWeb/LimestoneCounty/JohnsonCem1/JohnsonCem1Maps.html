JOHNSON CEMETERY #1, (Off of Hwy 251 near Ardmore, Al.), (NorthEast) LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Johnson Cemetery #1. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. Another location method is: Section 23 Township 1 Range 3, Northeast Quarter of the Northwest Quarter. Athens/Limestone County Archives can issue you a map for $4 that makes this useful to find Cemetery & other assets.

Go east to north on Hwy 251 (old Hwy 31) several miles to Johnson Road. Turn east (right) there and go about 1/2 mile and merge onto Johnson Cemetery Road. Follow that 1/4 mile and before the road bends southeast the cemetery is in front of you.  
This aerial view uses a yellow pointer to show the cemetery located under a stand of trees. It is inside a gated chain link fence, and well groomed as a community cemetery.
