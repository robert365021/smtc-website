 William Edmond Nethery was born near Pocahontas, McNairy County, Tennessee, February 24, 1863. He died August 15, 1910 in the Douglas Infirmary, Nashville, Davidson County, Tennessee, at 46 (47) years of age. He was buried in the Family Cemetery at "Oak Mount," the Gamble Family Farm just west of Belle Mina and north of Mooresville, Limestone County, Alabama. He married Mary GAMBLE in Mooresville, Limestone County, Alabama, February 13, 1896. She was born at "Oak Mount," the Gamble Family Farm just west of Belle Mina and north of Mooresville, Limestone County, Alabama December 30, 1866
