ISOM CHAPEL CEMETERY, (Mooresville Road, east side), (N.E.) LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This aerial view shows the GPS of the Isom Chapel Cemetery located on the Mooresville Road north of the Isom Chapel United Methodist Church. 
From Athens go east on Hwy 72 to Mooresville Road and turn north (or left). Go past Pepper Road 3 tenths of a mile and the cemetery will be on your right along the road, fenced from the road and well maintained.

This cemetery is located on the north side of the Isom Chapel United Methodist Church and the east side of Mooresville Road in a clearing.
