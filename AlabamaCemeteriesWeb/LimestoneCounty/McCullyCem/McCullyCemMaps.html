McCULLY CEMETERY, Myers Rd., N. of Buck Island Rd. LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the McCully Cemetery. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. N 34.850822 W -87.025780 -- Alternate location: Township 2S Range 5W, Section 23 SE quarter, on Myers Rd. N of Buck Island Road. After visiting there the center of the cemetery is actually at N 34.850822 W 87.02578 The location is on the southwest side of the thicket of trees jutting into the field.

From Athens go northwest on Buck Island Road to Myers Road and turn right (north) hen the road bends to the left the cemetery is in a thicket along a hedge row that runs east t west north of the road. You may have difficulty crossing the two branches that cross between you and the cemetery.

This aerial view shows the cemetery inside a clump of trees south of a hedge of trees northeast of Myers Road. The home in the northwest corner of the map is probably where the permission/notification lies.
