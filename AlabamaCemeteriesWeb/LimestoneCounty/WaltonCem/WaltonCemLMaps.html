WALTON CEMETERY, Barlow Road, LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Walton Cemetery as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper: N 34.62113 W -86.81340. Alternate Location: NE quarter, Sect. 11, Township 5S, Range 3W in the middle of a field.  The cemetery sits on a concrete slab around all the stones. One small stone either had no writing or the inscription was covered by the concrete. It sits on the northeast corner of the cemetery and is a concrete marker dated about 1900. Two of the stones are box-tombs. King Walton has an age on the tombstone but it is buried deeply and cannot be read with any degree of certainty though I think I see the top of a 4 and 7 meaning he was 47 years of age in 1905.

From County Line Road in Madison go south past I-565 another 5+ miles and turn right into SW Wall St and go about 1/4 mile and turn north (right) into Swanscott Road follow that until it turns back west and then go about 2/3 of a mile and turn right (north) into Bilow Rd SW. Follow that until it dead ends on Barlow road. Turn left (west) and go to the end of Barlow Road and if the weather is dry turn off the road and follow a field road for 250 yards out to the cemetery on your right sitting on a concrete pad.
