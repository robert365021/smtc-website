ADAMS CEMETERY # 1, Shoals Creek Rd, LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Adams Monuments as plotted by C. Wayne Austin. This is also known as the Latitude and Longitude location. ACME Mapper 2.0: N 34.95616 W -87.07764.
Alternate Location: SE quarter, Section 17, Township 1S, Range 5W approximately 200 yards off the road on the east side in a pasture or cultivated field.  

From Athens take Hines Street North which becomes Edgewood Road which becomes Easter Ferry Road. Branch right off of Easter Ferry Rd onto Legg Town Road and after about a mile turn left on Shoals Creek Road. You can see the cemetery in a large field on the right about 200 yards from the road.

This photo view from Google as I have it labeled shows the cemetery in front of the small grove of trees. In this 2008 photo view one can see at least one cedar tree. Looks to be wheat growing here. I was there once and tall Corn was growing.
