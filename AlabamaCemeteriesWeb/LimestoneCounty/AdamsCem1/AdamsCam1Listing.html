This Cemetery presentation is based on the photography of Donnie Stanford, 2011 & C Wayne Austin 24 Jan 2017 #9382-#9405. It was also presented in the book Tombstone Inscriptions of Limestone County Alabama by Sheryl B. Smith 6 Dec 1999.  Findagrave has the cemetery and genealogy listed with Donnie Stanford being the photographer. When I first attempted to visit this cemetery years ago the corn was growing taller than my head. I decided not to negotiate that itchy environment and by-passed the cemetery for another more favorable time.
