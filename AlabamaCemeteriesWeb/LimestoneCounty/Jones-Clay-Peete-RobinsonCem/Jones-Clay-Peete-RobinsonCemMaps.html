BAILEY-CRUTCHER CEMETERY, (Subdivision south of Mill Road, Madison Al.), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Jones-Clay-Peete-Robinson Cemetery in the green box lower right corner of the map. Alternate Location: NW quarter, Sect. 32, T3S, R3W. This cemetery was not found by the Smith in their book but was included from another source.

 At the intersection of Mooresville Road and Hwy 72 go south to Newby Road and turn east (Left) and go to Gray Road. There turn south or right and go to Iron Gate Dr. follow that to the cemetery which is in between houses on the right.

Notice the red check indicating the cemetery location occupying a private open lot in this subdivision. The owner of this lot values their privacy. If you visit here advanced permission is required.
