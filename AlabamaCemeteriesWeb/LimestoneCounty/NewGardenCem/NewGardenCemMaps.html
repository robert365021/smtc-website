NEW GARDEN CEMETERY- (Main Section), LIMESTONE COUNTY ALABAMA

This map shows the GPS location of the New Garden Cemetery in Limestone County Alabama.

Directions to the New Garden Cemetery: Take I-65 to Exit no 361 and go west on Sandlin Road to Hayes Mill Road. Go about 1 mile and bear left on New Garden Road. Cemetery is about 400 yards down New Garden Road when it junctions to the east/west. The cemetery is on the left and right of the road junction.

With this Aerial View we see the New Garden Road which branches from Hays Mill Road. This Google Map labels this the Montgomery Cemetery but that cemetery is down the road & back into the woods and includes the James G. (1781 - 1854) & Mary (1797 - 1831) Montgomery Family cemetery.
