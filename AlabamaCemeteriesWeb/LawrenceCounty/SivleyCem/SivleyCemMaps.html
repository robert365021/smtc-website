SIVLEY CEMETERY, (near Hwy 24) LAWRENCE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map from Topozone shows the GPS of the Sivley Cemetery. This is just south off of Hwy 24.

 This road map from MS Streets & trips shows the directions as: From Decatur, travel west on Highway 24 for 5.6 miles.  After turning right (south) on county road 322 and traveling about 1/2 mile, watch for a narrow dirt road on the right (west side of the road). The cemetery sits off the road and is normally not visible, but there was a sign on the road when I was there, because the road also serves residential property in the area.
The cemetery is seen here with this map as in a clearing under a few trees. It is marked with a red check here. The access road is seen as a gray line leading west off of CR 322 to the cemetery.
