HOLLAND CEMETERY, Hillsboro, LAWRENCE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Holland Cemetery as N 34.62339 X W -87.15180.

This road map shows the directions: Go west from Decatur on Highway 20 toward Muscle Shoals to Hillsboro and turn south on County Road 53/217 which goes thru Hillsboro.  Follow that thru Hillsboro and when you come to County Road 377 turn right (or west). That road goes west then southwest then bends directly south. After bending back south go several hundred yards further and look left at the cemetery behind a chain link fence and included in a grove of trees and thick shrubs.

Notice the cemetery in the midst of trees and shrub, but behind a chain link fence only visible on one side.
