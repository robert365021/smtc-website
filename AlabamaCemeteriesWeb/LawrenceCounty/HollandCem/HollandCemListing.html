Mr. Horace Jerry Holland and others provided a wealth of information in this cemetery about their ancestors on headstones which look to be set largely about 1950-1960 on flat style granite markers. While it is less clear which of these ancestors were buried in this cemetery, findagrave.com has that pegged fairly well. I suspect that several of the tombstones represent what is termed a "Cenotaph" the term for a memorial stone set not over the remains of the one being memorialized, but just there to remember the loved one when it is not known where the body is located.
This cemetery is covered in Vinca Minor and shrub that are overrunning the graveyard. I slang bladed some of of the excess away but more needs to be done. Some gorgeous old Box Elders are present here along with other shrubs. These grow so slow they are hardly ever a problem. [CWA 29 Nov 2014]
