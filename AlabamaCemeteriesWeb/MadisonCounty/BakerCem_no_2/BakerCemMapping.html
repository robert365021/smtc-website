BAKER (Samuel) CEMETERY, Hurricane Road, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
 The GPS coordinates of the Samuel Baker Cemetery are shown here as N 34.83808 X W -86.41358. Alternate Location: Section 28-Range 2 Township 2E. Just north of the corner of Hurricane Road and County Lake Road and about 5 1/2 miles south-south east of New Market.
 At the intersection of Winchester Rd (Hwy 65) and County Lake Road go east about 2 1/2 miles to the Hurricane Creek Road and turn north (left). Go about 300 yards and look to your right upon a bank of the road at the cemetery. Park your vehicle just back on the right before the giant Oak Tree.

This street view from Google shows the cemetery clearly on the east side of Hurricane Creek Road. Please do not go here unless you have honor for the deceased in your heart.
