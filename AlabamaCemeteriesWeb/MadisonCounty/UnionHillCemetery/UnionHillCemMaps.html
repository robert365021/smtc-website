UNION HILL CEMETERY, Winchester Rd., MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Union Hill Cemetery. This is at GPS coordinates of N 34.79333 W -86.58369.

 At the intersection of Hwy 431/231 go West on Winchester Rd. about 1/3 of a mile and look to your right at the Church Building. The cemetery is in the lot just past the Church building, but has nothing to do with the Cemetery. This is an old Pioneer Cemetery. It sits between the Home and Church.

This close up view of the Clump of trees whereby the cemetery resides. Vandals have been picking away at this cemetery for years and while some of the tombstones are standing they appear to be out of place or misplaced and broken up.
