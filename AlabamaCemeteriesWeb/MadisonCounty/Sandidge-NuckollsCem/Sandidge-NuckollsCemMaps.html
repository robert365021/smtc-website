SANDIDGE-NUCKOLLS CEMETERY (Steger Rd.) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Sandidge-Nuckolls Cemetery. The location is indicated under the red check in the center of the map as N 34.88387 X W -86.53998.

Take Hwy 234/431 north from Huntsville and turn (east) right on Steger Rd. Follow that for about 2 miles and the cemetery is on private property behind a locked gate on your left. It is directly behind a metal building and under Hackberry trees. Since this is private land all around get permission to visit here.

Notice the red check indicating the cemetery location behind a metal building and in front of a two story home. It is under a stand of Hackberry trees.
