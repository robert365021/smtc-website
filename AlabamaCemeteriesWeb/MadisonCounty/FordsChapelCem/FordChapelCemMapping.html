FORDS CHAPEL CEMETERY, Stovall/Yarbrough Rd., MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This ACME Mapper Map 2.1 originally from Google Shows the GPS of the Fords Chapel Cemetery. This is at GPS coordinates of N 34.8382, W -86.73223, measured in the middle of the road between the two sections.

 On Hwy 72 Go north on Wall Triana Hwy to Stovall Road and turn right (east). Follow that about a 1/2 mile until it becomes Yarbrough Road and another 1/2 mile. The cemetery will be found just at the bend of the road before & after that road bends back north (left).
