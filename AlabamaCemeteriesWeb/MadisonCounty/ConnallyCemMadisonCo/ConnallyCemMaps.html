CONNALLY CEMETERY, St Clair Ln., MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Connally Cemetery. The GPS coordinates on this Cemetery is:  34.79868 N X -86.45810 W. Alternate Location: Section 7, Township 3, Range 2E, N/2, N/2, NE, NW. On St. Clair Lane off Bell Factory Road in a grove of trees on the south side of the road on a Christmas Tree farm.
 At the intersection of Winchester Road and Bell Factory Road go south (right turn going east on Winchester Road) on Bell Factory Road. Follow that and turn right or back south again on St Clair Lane and the road then bends back left to the east. Go another 4 tenths of a mile and look to your right at the Evergreen thicket as shown on the next below image. Vandals/Devil Worshippers beware. The neighbors & Madison Co Sheriff are watching for your actions.
