DEDMAN CEMETERY, (left side of Wall-Triana Highway) MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Dedman Cemetery of Madison County Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map.
Out Wall-Triana Highway just past Stovall Road look for the grove of trees among residential property adjacent to the highway where the cemetery once resided. This is a cemetery ideal for excavation and removal to a more suitable site.

Notice the red check indicating the cemetery location. It is likely the trees all were a part of the cemetery at one time. A utility easement cuts thru the cemetery property and that explains the tree elimination in a swath north and south along the highway.
