PALMER / CARTWRIGHT-CRUTCHER CEMETERY, (Palmer Road, Madison, Al.), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Palmer / Cartwright-Crutcher Cemetery of Madison Alabama.

From the intersection of County Line & Palmer Roads go past one set of buildings on Palmer Road and there will be a tree covered area on the right next to Palmer Road where the cemetery is located. It has historically been abandoned and grown up in brush. This cemetery is known as the Palmer Cemetery in USGS maps.

Notice the red check indicating the cemetery location under the trees between an office complex and Palmer Road on the south side of the road.
