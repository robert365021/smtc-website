HARRIS FAMILY HISTORY MONUMENT, (Zeirdt Road, south of Madison Al.), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

This very public monument on very private land along the west side of Zeirdt Road cemetery as a tribute to the William Harris Family b. abt 1828 d. between 1870-1874, who lived at one time on what is now Red Stone Arsenal.
