HARRIS FAMILY MONUMENT, 570 Zierdt Rd, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA


This aerial map shows the GPS of the Harris Family Monument. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked at GPS N 34.5907 X W -86.7373.

From Huntsville go out I-565 and merge into Old highway 20 in Madison and go to Zeirdt Rd and turn left. Go nearly down to Triana at the junction of Record St and Zierdt Road and look left at the monument in a front yard of a private home near the road and behind the fence.

This close up aerial view with a red check to show where the monument is located under the trees. This is across from the Record St junction of Zierdt Rd.
