BAILES-BROYLES CEMETERY (Hurricane Creek Valley) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Bailes-Broyles Cemetery. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked at GPS N 34.84835 X W -86.39856. Alternate Locator: Section 22, Township 2S, Range 2E, NW SE.

From Huntsville go out Winchester Rd several miles past Bell Factory to County Lake Road and turn (east) right. Go less than a half mile and turn left on Fussy Hill Rd. Follow that a mile or so until it dead ends into Hurricane Rd. Turn north (left) on Hurricane Rd and go about a mile and turn right on Ray Road. Follow Ray road until just before it turns northward (leftward) and turn right (south) on Hearthstone Rd. Then that road turns back east and dead ends then turn back south (right) then continue to circle the cemetery until you are on the north side and you can park and walk down the gentle slope. The Bragg Cemetery shown above may not exist or it may exist in a different place from the exact location above.

This close up aerial view shows a yellow highlighted outline of the cemetery premises. There is a fence today with access from the south side. The large object seen in the bottom area is a piece of farm machinery unrelated to the cemetery.
