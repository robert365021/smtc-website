NORRIS CEMETERY, (New Market), MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Norris Cemetery. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked at GPS N 34.90544 X W -86.42577. Alternate Locator: Section 33-Township 1S, Range 2E - SW SW. Located on north side of Cemetery Road in New Market, Alabama.

From Huntsville go out Winchester Rd several miles to New Market. Turn right (east) on Hurricane Road and go several blocks to Davis Road and turn right. Follow that until it dead ends into Cemetery Road and turn right (west) and the cemetery is there on the right (north) of the road.

This close up aerial view shows a yellow highlighted outline of the cemetery premises. There is no fence today.
