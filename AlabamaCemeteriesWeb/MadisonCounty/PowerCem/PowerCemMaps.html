POWER CEMETERY, Frank Hereford Rd., MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Power Cemetery as N 34.8781 X W -86.42078. Alternate Location: Section 9, Range 2E, Township 2S, N/2 N/2 N/2 NW/4. Located in a clump of brush by the side of Frank Hereford Rd.

 Go out Winchester (Hwy 65) Highway to Frank Hereford Road and turn right (east). Follow Frank Hereford Rd until you pass Lewis Mountain Road on your right. Immediately look to your the right (south) bank of the road now under a clump of Sassafras and black Cherry trees. Vandals beware - the neighbors & Madison County Sheriff are watching you.

Notice the red label indicating the cemetery location occupying an area under a clump of trees 1/2 way between Lewis Mt Rd and Jacks Road on Frank Hereford Rd.
