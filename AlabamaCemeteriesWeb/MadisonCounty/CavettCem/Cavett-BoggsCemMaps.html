CAVETT-BOGGS CEMETERY, (1/4 mile east of hwy 31), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Cavett-Boggs Cemetery of Madison Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked as Cem.. Specific Location: Section 18-2-1E - NE corner of the SE NW NW - Approx. 1/4 mi. east - off Memorial Parkway and 1/2 mile south of Steger Road inside a subdivision in a dense woodland thicket. ACME Mapper says a more central location is 35.87099 N X -86.56711 W

At the intersection of Hwy 231 N and Winchester Road travel about 6 miles to Crossing Blvd and turn right there. Follow that to Royal Court Rd and turn north (left) and go less than 100 yards and the woodland thicket on your left above the road contains the cemetery.

Notice the marker Cavett Cemetery indicator showing the cemetery location. be prepared to fight the dense brush to get into & around in the cemetery.
