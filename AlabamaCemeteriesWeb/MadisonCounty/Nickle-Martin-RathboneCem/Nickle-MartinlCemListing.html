This cemetery contains a large area with few areas appearing to have graves. With the passage of time identifying unmarked graves becomes more and more difficult as the evidence is destroyed.
I have used the spelling of Nickle (Robert) on the tombstone even though the signage says Nicholas is Robert's surname. Nicholas is probably correct with a tombstone error being possible due to the low level of spelling literacy in the past, but who knows.
