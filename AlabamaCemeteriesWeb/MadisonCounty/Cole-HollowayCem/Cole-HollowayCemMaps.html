COLE-HOLLOWAY CEMETERY, (Miler McCann Dr-S. of Ready Section Rd), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Cole-Holloway Cemetery. The location is indicated in the center of the map as marked at GPS N 34.93065 X W -86.64201 Specific Location: Section 28-Township 1S Range 1W. W/2 W/2 NW NW. South off Ready Section Road and approximately one-half mile due northwest of Briar Fork Church & Cemetery.

On Hwy 231 go several miles north and turn left on Grimwood Rd. Go several miles and turn north (right) on Murphy Hill Rd. The road will become Briar Fork Rd after more than a mile. Keep right on Briar Fork Rd until it comes to Ready Section Road. Turn left on Ready Section Road and go to the first crossing which is Butter & Egg Road on right and Miler McCann Rd on left. Take the left Miler McCann Road and go to the first house on left and the cemetery is in the left front area of the front yard. Permission to enter the yard is required.

This aerial view is provided to show the roads better.
