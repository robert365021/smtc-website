COLE-HOLLOWAY CEMETERY, (Miler McCann Dr. S. of Ready Section Rd), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

Area overview of the cemetery among the giant oaks: 7034

COLE, Geles D., wife of William Cole, 9 Nov 1810 - 23 May 1846. (William Cole married Geles Clark Sept. 2, 1829.) 7035/7026/7027/7029/footstone G.D.C.:7028
 In Memory of GELES D. COLE, wife of Wm. COLE, Born Novr. 9th, 1810 and died May 23rd, 1846.

HOLLOWAY, Sally, d/o Stephen and Judith Holland, w/o Joseph H. Holloway, 19 Feb 1794 - 27 Jul 1861, Aged 67 years, 5 Mo. and 8 days. 7031R/7030/7031/7032/7033
IN MEMORY OF SALLY. DAUGHTER OF STEPHEN AND JUDITH HOLLAND, CONSORT OF JOSEPH H. HOLLOWAY. BORN FEB 19TH, 1794 DIED JULY 27TH, 1861, AGED 67 YEARS, 5 MO., & 8 DAYS. BLESSED ARE THE DEAD THAT DIE IN THE LORD.

These 3 stones show up among massive Red Oak trees as in the front yard of a private home. Please show privacy and respect for the folks living here. My photos were directed away from their home.

William Cole is listed in the Madison County 1830 Census, but does not show up in the 1850 Census indicating that he had died by then. This may mean he is here in an unmarked grave beside his wife.
