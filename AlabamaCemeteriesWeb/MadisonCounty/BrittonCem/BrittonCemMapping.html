BRITTON CEMETERY, Isbell Road NE, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS of the Britton Cemetery as N 34.97900 W -86.45502. Alternate Location: Section 31-Range 1S, LOCATION: Section 6-1-2E - NW NW SW SE on Isbell Road approximately 1/4 mile east of the Isbell Road Intersection.
 From Hazel Green go east on Joe Quick Road. After more than 2 miles you will dead end into Butler Road. Turn left (north) and go over 2 miles and turn right (east) Phillips Rd. Go less than a mile and turn left on Isbell Road. Follow that for less than 1/2 mile
and the cemetery is on the right (east side).
