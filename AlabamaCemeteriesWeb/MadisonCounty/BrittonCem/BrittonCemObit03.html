BRITTON CEMETERY, Isbell Road NE, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA

Name: Larry R. Moore
Birth: Mar. 15, 1950; Madison Co.,AL
Death: Aug 22, 2014, Madison Co.,AL
 Parents: Rufus J Moore (1925 - 1999) and Jerrie Baeder Moore (1928 - 2011)
 Spouse: Sheila Pitcock Moore (1953 - 2015)
  Sibling: Linda Carol Moore White (1944 - 2004)
 
Mr. Larry R. Moore, 64, of New Market, passed away on Friday.

He was a well-known member of the Plevna Community. His passion was his lifelong commitment and dedication to farming and cattle. His joy was spending time with his kids and grandkids.
He was preceded in death by his parents: Rufus and Jerrie Moore; His sister: Linda Moore White.
He is survived by his son and daughter-in-law, Kory and Jennifer Moore; his daughter and son-in-law, Kim and Andy Boseck; His grandchildren: Kendall, Jackson, Clayton, Cade, Brooke, Luke, and Aubrey; His sisters: Judy Dale Moore and Janice Harbin.
The family will receive friends on Sunday from 1 to 2 pm at Hazel Green Funeral Home. A graveside service will follow at 2:30pm on Sunday at Britton Cemetery.
Hazel Green Funeral Home is honored to serve the family.
