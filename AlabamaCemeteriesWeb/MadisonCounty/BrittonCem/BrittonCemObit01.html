Allison Wayne "A. W." Britton Birth: Apr 28, 1939 Madison Co. AL, Death: Aug 24, 2015 Hazel Green Madison Co. AL

Allison Wayne �A.W.� Britton, age 76, of Hazel Green, AL passed away on Monday, August 24, 2015 at his residence. He was born in Madison County, AL on April 28, 1939, son of the late Russell
and Oma Clark Britton. Mr. Briton was a tile installer and was of the Baptist faith. In addition to his parents, he is preceded in death by his brothers, Lavoyd Britton and Thomas Britton,
and one great grandchild. Mr. Britton is survived by his wife, Anita Britton of Hazel Green, AL, daughters, Sandy (Bill) Perry of Hazel Green, AL and Allison Esch of Huntsville, AL, sons, Tim

AL, brother, Billy (Joyce) Britton of Hazel Green, AL, eighteen grandchildren and ten great grandchildren. Graveside services will be conducted on Tuesday, August 25, 2015 at 11:00 a.m. in
