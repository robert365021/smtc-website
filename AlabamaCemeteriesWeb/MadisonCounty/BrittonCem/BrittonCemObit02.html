BRITTON CEMETERY, Isbell Road NE, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA

Orlena Reid Morrison

Birth: Sep. 4, 1923 Madison Co. AL
Death: Jun. 2, 2017

Orlena Morrison, age 93, of Petersburg, TN, passed away on June 2, 2017. She was born on September 4, 1923 in Madison County, AL, daughter of the late Vernon and Annie Britton Reid.
In addition to her parents, she is preceded in death by her husband: Elbert Morrison; daughter: Stella Allen; son: Ray Morrison; sister: Linda Henry; and brothers: Leon Reid, Carl Reid, Billy Reid and Paul Reid.
Funeral services will be conducted on Monday, June 5, 2017 at 11:00 a.m. in the chapel of Gallant-Riverview Funeral Home with Bro. Malcolm Gardner officiating. Burial will follow in Britton Cemetery. Visitation
is Sunday from 5:00 p.m. until 8:00 p.m. at the funeral home.
