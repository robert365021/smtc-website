DAVIE CEMETERY, (W. Limestone Rd) MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Davie Cemetery of Madison County Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked with the red check. GPS N 34.92327 X W -86.61495.
Alternate location: Section 27-Township 1S-Range 1W. SE NE SW.

In Huntsville go north on Hwy 231/431 drive to Hazel Green and turn left (or west) on West Limestone Road. Go about 2 1/2 miles, and the cemetery is upon the right (north) side of the road bank, before you get to Loveless Rd. Alternate directions: Approximately 2-1/2 miles southwest of Hazel Green, Ala., on West Limestone Road. On north side of road in clump of brush and trees. A house once stood next to the cemetery but it and other buildings have been cleared away and the land is presently in cultivation.

This is a Google street view of the cemetery.  Do not go here unless you have respect for the deceased and their memorial relics.
