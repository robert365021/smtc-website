PERKINS-JONES CEMETERY (Campus of UAH), MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Perkins Cemetery of Madison County Alabama. The location is indicated with the red check in the center of the map. GPS N 34.73237 X W 86.64207.

 LOCATION: Sec. 33-35-1W. NE SW NW SW. Located directly behind Morton Hall on the Huntsville campus of U.A.H. off University Drive. A low, wrought iron fence surrounds the graves.

Notice the red check behind the University building and inside a black wrought iron fence.
