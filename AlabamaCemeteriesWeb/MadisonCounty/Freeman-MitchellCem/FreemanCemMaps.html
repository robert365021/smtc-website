FREEMAN-MITCHELL CEMETERY, (Opp Reynolds Rd. & Carter Grove Roads), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Freeman Cemetery of Madison Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map with the check. Specific Location: Section 35, Township 1 S., Range 1 W., N/2, NE, NE. Located In a grove of trees one mile east and one mile north of Madison Cross Roads.

At the intersection of Pulaski Pike and Carter Grove Road turn north and go almost to the next intersection which is Opp Reynolds Rd and look (left) out into the field at a tiny grove of trees were the cemetery resides.

This zoomed in map with a red check shows where the cemetery can be found out in a cultivated field. There are only certain times of the year that this cemetery can be visited without harming the crops though there is supposed to be a right of way.
