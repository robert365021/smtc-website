BAILEY-HUSE CEMETERY, (Cecil Ashburn Dr), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Bailey-Huse Cemetery. This is at GPS coordinates of N 34.67187 W -86.53483.

 At the intersection of Whitesburg Drive and Four Mile Post Road go out Four mile Post Rd.. Pass Carl T. Jones Rd and the road becomes Cecil Ashburn Dr. Go another few hundred yards and turn right between two buildings and the cemetery is on your right behind one of the buildings inside a chain link fence. The gate is in front.

Notice the red check indicating the cemetery location occupying an area inside a fence and with a surrounding sidewalk outside the fence. Looks like they took a chunk out of the building and dedicated it to the cemetery.
