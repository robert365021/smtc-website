NORRIS CEMETERY #2, (Upper Hurricane Valley, Ray Rd), MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS of the Norris Cemetery #2. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked at GPS N 34.86842 X W -86.38810. Specific Location: Section 14, Township 2S, Range 2E. NW NW SW, on hill overlooking Hurricane Creek and Valley on east side of Ray Road.

From Huntsville go northeast on Winchester Rd to New Market and turn right on Hurricane Road. Go to Upper Hurricane Rd and turn right or east and follow that to Sharps Hollow Road. Turn into that right and after a mile or so turn right on Ray Road. Follow that to the just over a half mile and turn left (with permission) into the Norris Driveway and go up the hill to the home and get advice on how to visit this cemetery south west of the home place 400 yards. If no one is home one is  (defacto) denied permission to visit.
