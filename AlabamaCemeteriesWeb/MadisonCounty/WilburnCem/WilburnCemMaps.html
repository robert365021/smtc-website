WILBURN CEMETERY, Jeff Road, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Wilburn Cemetery. The location is indicated on the map marked at GPS N 34.85426 X W -86.371490. Specific Location: Section 22, Township 2S, Range 2W. NE SE NE. On west side of Jeff Road behind a residual home.

From Huntsville go northwest on Hwy 53 Ardmore Highway to Jeff Road and turn right. Go then about 1/2 mile and look to your left at a home. The Cemetery is behind the home. The folks there are cordial as of my visit.

This aerial view shows a yellow check in the center of the cemetery premises.
