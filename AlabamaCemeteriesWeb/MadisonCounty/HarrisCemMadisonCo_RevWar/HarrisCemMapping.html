HARRIS CEMETERY, Timbercrest Rd, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This ACME Mapper Map 2.1 originally from Google Shows the GPS of the Harris Cemetery. This is at GPS coordinates of N. 34.78051 W. -86.59471. Alternate Location: Section 14 T-3S R1W. NE NE SW NE. Off Blue Springs Road in the Lakewood Subdivision in northwest Huntsville.

Go north on Blue Springs Road and turn right (east) on Tolen St NW then right (south) on Glendale Dr NW then left (east) on McClure St NW then left on Timbercrest Dr NW. Pass three houses on your right and the next lot with trees and a chain link fence is the location of the cemetery.

This is a photo view of the cemetery showing the cemetery on the east side of the road and occupying a town lot.
