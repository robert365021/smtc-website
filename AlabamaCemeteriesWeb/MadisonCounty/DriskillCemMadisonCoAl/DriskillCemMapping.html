DRISKILL CEMETERY, (Hwy 231 near state line) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Driskill Cemetery of Madison Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map. GPS: N 34.98741 X W -86.56782.

Traveling north on Hwy 231/431 just before crossing the state line turn right on Agua Dr and the cemetery is behind the first house on the left. Alternate Location: Section 6-1-1E, SE SE NW NW. This cemetery is located approximately 1/4 mile southeast of the State Line Methodist Church Cemetery & approximately 1/6 mile east of Highway 231-431 behind a residence.

Notice the cemetery under the red check and behind a house. You must have permission to visit this cemetery.
