DRISKILL - Rev A.F., In Memory of Rev. A. F. Driskill, son of Rev. John & Mary Driskill, Born in N. C. June 16. 1801. Converted 1810, Licensed to preach & Joined the Tenn Conference 1822, of which he was a member 48 yrs., & In his 5th yr. in the N. Alabama Conference March 17. 1875, died triumphantly. Soldier of Christ well done! Praise be thy new employ; And while eternal ages run. Rest In thy Saviour's Joy. Footstone: "An Israelite in-deed."  8154U / 8154 / 8155
DRISKILL - S. (Sarah) J., born May 16, 1820. married to Ambrose F. Driskill May 10, 1837. Died March 16, 1906. She hath done what she could. Note: Ambrose F. Driskill married Sarah Jane McGehee In Madison Co., Ala. 8153
HATCHER - Miss Mary A., Died Sept. 18. 1858 age 89 years and 5 days. Mary hath chosen that good part which shall not be taken from her. (This stone could not be found)
