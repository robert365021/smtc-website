HIGGINBOTHAM aka McCALEB/FISK CEMETERY, Grimwood Road, MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS of the Higginbotham aka McCaleb-Fisk Cemetery as N 34.907063 W -86.601825.
 Go north on Hwy 231 several miles and turn left on Grimwood Road. Follow that for about 1 3/4 miles and the cemetery is on the right out in a massive cultivated field (corn today)  about 1,200 feet (400 yards) north of the road behind a chain link fence.

Notice this aerial view from Google shows the cemetery in the midst of a large field where giant irrigation machinery revolves around. Notice the GPS measurements between the Google Maps and the ACME map are slightly different. I trust the bottom map or the ACME map more.
