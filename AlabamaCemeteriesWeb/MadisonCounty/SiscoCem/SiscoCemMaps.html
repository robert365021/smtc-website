BAILES-BROYLES CEMETERY (Hurricane Creek Valley) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Sisco Cemetery. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked at GPS N 34.86637 X W -86.40107. Alternate Location: Section 15 Township 2S Range 2E - Center of section 15, Elevation 730. Covered with Vinca Minor (vegetation found in old graveyards). Off a dirt field-road east off Hurricane Road in Hurricane Valley.
From Huntsville go out Winchester Rd several miles past Bell Factory to County Lake Road and turn (east) right. Go less than a half mile and turn left on Fussy Hill Rd. Follow that a mile or so until it dead ends into Hurricane Rd. Turn north (left) on Hurricane Rd and go two mile and pass Ray Road turn off on the right and go about another 3/4 mile to the Bragg Cemetery Road on the right. Turn into Bragg Cemetery which doubles as a farm road and residential access and go 2 to 4 hundred yards to just before the Bragg Cemetery on right. Park and with permission walk (left) north to the thicket in the distance which contains the cemetery. You may encounter insurmountable fences (do not trample on them) on this route so ask at the nearby homes for the designated path to the cemetery. I have given you the route with the least amount of walking.
