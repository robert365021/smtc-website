CLIFT CEMETERY, (south side of Hwy 72 in present day shopping center), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

 Clift Lots in Old Madison City Cemetery
 Mapping the location
 

Area photos of the Clift Cemetery moved to the Madison (Old City) Cemetery and combined with later generations  appears to be at least 3 generations: 1592 / 1588 / 3415

CLIFT, John H., 5 Sep 1821 - 25  May 1893. 1583C / 1583 / 1584C / 1584 / 1593 / 1595 / 1596 / footstone J.H.C.: 1596C
CLIFT, Mary (Carouthers), 4 Aug 1820 - 24 Jun 1897. (wife of John H. Clift) 1581 / 1582

These graves and/or stones were moved to the Old Madison City Cemetery behind city hall to the Clift Lots. I am unaware of the time and particulars of that, if the graves were moved or abandoned and just the stones moved. They showed up on findagrave on 6 July of 2012 as added by Susan Bennett. The shopping center was built in 2013 and any possibility of finding any other lost graves were destroyed in that expansion if due diligence was not utilized prior to that.  Madison Building Permits may have information on the construction and how that related to this cemetery which was abolished in a 10 foot deep excavation assuming the graves (not the gravestones) were still there.
