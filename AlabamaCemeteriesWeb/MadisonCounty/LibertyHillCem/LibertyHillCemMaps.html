LIBERTY HILL CEMETERY, (Liberty Hill Road just east of Pulaski Pike), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Liberty Hill Cemetery of Madison Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map listed with the name, It is located on both sides of the road, though some accounts have the east side as the Darwin Cemetery. Specific Location: Section 21-2S-1W. S/2 S/2.


At the intersection of Research Park Blvd & Pulaski Pike turn left (or north) and go about a mile and turn right into Prosperity Dr. Follow that for about 1/2 mile and turn back north onto Liberty Hill Road. Cemetery is 1/4 mile up the road on the right and left sides of the road into the tree line, behind locked gates and tall fences. Permission is required to get a key.

Notice the red check marking the cemetery on both sides of the road behind fences. They two locations are not exactly across from each other.
