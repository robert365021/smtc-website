BIBB CEMETERY, (Mill Road, Madison, Al.), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Bibb Cemetery of Madison Alabama. The location is indicated with a red check.

At the intersection of County Line & Mill Roads go east toward downtown Madison about 1 & 1/2 miles and turn left or north on Bibb Drive. Look left after the turn and you will see the cemetery. Vandals beware - the neighbors & Madison Police are watching.

Notice the red check indicating the cemetery location occupying some of the lots in a subdivision.
