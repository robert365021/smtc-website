FORD CEMETERY, Countess Rd NE, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS of the Ford Cemetery. This is at GPS coordinates of N 34.81978 W -86.538118. Alternate Location: Section 32-2-1E - NE SE. Off Moore's Mill Road in a small plot directly behind a brick ranch-style home.

 Go out Winchester Road to Moores Mill Road and turn left (north) and go about a mile and the cemetery is on your left behind a garage style out-building behind a home which sits off the road a hundred yards or so as shown in the first map. You will merge straight ahead on to Countess Road NE to get there and no longer be on Moore's Mill Road. The home is a brick home and the brick light color. Permission should be required as you don't want to go snooping behind anyone's home to visit this cemetery

Notice the Yellow estimated outline of this cemetery behind the out building and in the trees.
