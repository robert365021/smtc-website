STRONG CEMETERY - (Monroe Rd & Pulaski Pike NE corner) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Strong-Douglass Cemetery of Madison Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked with the white check. However there does not appear to be a cemetery at this location. LOCATION: Section 6 Must actually be 16) Township 2, Range 1 east C. E/2 NW/4. In the valley a quarter of a mile east of the big curve in Meridian Pike aka Monroe Road before it meets with Highway 231/431 north of Huntsville, Alabama.


Go out Pulaski Pike and pass Monroe Road and look right or east into a giant field where two ponds exist. I recently visited this mapped place in attempting to find this cemetery and was disappointed to find nothing but a large open field with no sign of a cemetery. It appears not to be here anymore or possibly it was miss-mapped. It probably has been destroyed by the current owner who added giant irrigation equipment recently. He evidently bulldozed the cemetery to clear the area for the equipment. The mapping I have shows no conclusion as to the location of the cemetery, but shows it in two places out in the giant field as shown in these two maps and next to the Pulaski Pike. I saw no evidence it was in neither location. However, I did not get a good look.
