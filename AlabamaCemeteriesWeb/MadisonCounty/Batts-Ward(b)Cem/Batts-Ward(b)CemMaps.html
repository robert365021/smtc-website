BATTS-WARD (black) CEMETERY, Triana, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Batts-Ward Cemetery as N 34.58440 X W 86.74570.
 From old Hwy 20 in Madison take Zierdt Road south several miles and turn right on 6th St. Go to Lakeside Dr and the cemetery resides in that junction in the northeast section.

Notice the balloon marking the spot where the cemetery can be found.
