McCRARY-MACON-WRIGHT CEMETERY (Macon Lane) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the McCrary-Macon Wright Cemetery. The location is indicated under the red check near the center of the map. The GPS is: N 34.86050 X W -86.49878. Alternate Locator: Section 14-Township 2S- Range 1E, W/2 SW SW.   This cemetery has been mapped by the USGS.

From Huntsville go out Winchester Rd several miles and turn north (left) on Riverton Road. Go 2 miles and the road eventually ends on Macon Lane. Turn right there and go about a mile and with advanced permission go to the left behind the house of the home owner where the cemetery is inside a fence. Please respect privacy rights and the private assets all around you here and do not have a constant stream of people going here.

This close up aerial view shows a red check in the center of the cemetery premises. I have mostly faded the private assets. If you go here leave a card or something to let the home owner know who your are and your reason for being here. They are doing a great job of caring for this cemetery and deserve your utmost respect for their privacy. It is my policy not to release names or phone numbers in this instance.
