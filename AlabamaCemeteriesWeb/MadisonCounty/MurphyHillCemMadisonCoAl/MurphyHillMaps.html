MURPHY HILL CHURCH CEMETERY (Murphy Hill Rd), MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Murphy Hill Cemetery of Madison Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked as indicated. N 34.89759 W -86.635. Specific Location: Section 4-28-1W - NE SE NW. This cemetery is located behind the Murphy Hill Baptist Church on Murphy Hill Road, west side.

From Huntsville go north on Hwy 231/431 to Grimwood Rd. turn left and follow that to Murphy Hill Rd  and turn south (left) and go about a 1/2 mile and the cemetery is behind the Murphy Hill Baptist Church on the (right)west side of the road.

Notice this area view of the premises of the church, parking lot, and cemetery.
