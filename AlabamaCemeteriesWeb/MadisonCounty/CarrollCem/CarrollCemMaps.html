CARROLL CEMETERY, (Carroll Rd), LIMESTONE COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Carroll Cemetery of Harvest Alabama.

At the intersection of Harvest Road and Old Railroad Bed Road turn west and go 1 mile to the Carroll Road and turn north (or right). Go 9 tenths of a mile and look to your right under the trees next to the road between two houses at the stones and the Carroll Cemetery sign. There is not a parking place because the shoulder of the road is narrow.

Here we see tall trees hovering over the cemetery between two driveways of residential houses.
