NANCE-SALE CEMETERY, Burwell Rd, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS of the Nance-Sale Cemetery. This is at GPS coordinates of N 34.84275 W -86.665897. Alternate Location: Section 30 Township 2S Range 1W - S/2 SE NE NW NE. On Burwell Road.

 At the intersection of Highway 53 and Burwell Road go out Burwell Road toward the northeast and after about a mile the road bends back east. Continue on about another mile and look left at the cemetery nearly on the bank of the road and southwest of a nice home. Permission lies with the owner of the home this cemetery nearly lies in the yard of. The official name of this cemetery should be Sale-Nance Cemetery because the Sale family founded this place and also the cemetery. However since it is named the Nance Cemetery on mapping I chose to stay with Nance-Sale so name recognition is easier.

Notice the yellow labeling indicating the cemetery, an old now extinct home place and an old nearby barn. Believe the home was south of the yellow marking about 75 yards.
