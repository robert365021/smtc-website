CLOPTON, William Hales, 29 Sep 1787 - 10 May 1826. Modern Granite Marker:1036/1037/1038/Original Markers:1040C/1040/1039
Sacred to the Memory of William H. Clopton, Born September 29th 1787 - Died May 10th 1826.

CLOPTON, Avery Smith, 30 Aug 1785 - 31 Dec 1841. Modern Granite Marker:1036/1037/1038/Original Markers:1041C/1041/1042/1044/1039
Sacred to the Memory of Avey G. Clopton, Consort of William H. Clopton, Born August 30, 1785 - Died December 31, 1841

Note: When I visited this cemetery on March 28, 1970, the only markers left were the above two, however, there appeared to have been many other graves in the small tree-shaded cemetery. [D.S.J. abt 1985.] Appears someone is at least keeping the graves free of brush today.
