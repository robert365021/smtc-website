BRAGG CEMETERY (New Market), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Bragg Cemetery of Madison Alabama as N 34.86416 X W -86.40054. The location is indicated in the center of the map under the white check. Alternate Location: Section 15, Township 2 Range 2E, SE then NW then SW. This means the southeast quarter of section 15 then draw a box quartering that and select the northwest 1/4 part of that and then draw a box selecting the southwest 1/4 part of that and the cemetery is inside that 40 acre square area of the original section 15 or 640 acres . However the cemetery is fenced is in about a 1 acre plot east off of Hurricane Road.

At the intersection of Highway 231/431 North, at the Winchester Pike go out Winchester Pike 5+ miles to County Lake Road and turn right go about a mile and turn left on fussy hill road. when the road dead ends into Hurricane Road turn left and go about 1 & 1/2 miles and turn right into a long gravel road that doubles as a residential road and the Bragg Cemetery Road. Follow that to the cemetery on your right. The cemetery is maintained as a community cemetery.

Notice the cemetery in the center of the map. The monuments show up as splotchy shadows.
