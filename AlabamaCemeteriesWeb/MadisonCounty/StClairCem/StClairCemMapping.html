St CLAIR CEMETERY, Hurricane Creek Valley, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS of the St Clair Cemetery. This is at GPS coordinates of N 34.85509 W -86.39664.  Section 22, of Township 2, Range 2E, NE Quad on Ray Road on right.

 At the intersection of Hwy 431 & Winchester Rd turn east on Winchester Road and go to County Lake Road and turn right. Go to Hurricane Rd and turn north and go to Ray Road and  turn right. When the road bends north  go about a quarter of a mile up the hill and the cemetery is on your right. You will see the signage.

Notice the cemetery as marked on the map inside a fence  and well maintained. This cemetery is generally expanding to the west from the east.
