ELLIS CEMETERY (Murphy Hill Rd) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Ellis Cemetery of Madison County Alabama. The location is indicated with the red check in the center of the map as GPS N 34.88585 X W 86.63727.

LOCATION: Sec. 9-2S-1W - W/2 SW NE NW. 1/3 mile north of Patterson Lane behind several grain silos on the west side of Murphy Hill Road.

Notice the labeling showing where this cemetery is located behind a chain link fence. I am told a previous land owner partially destroyed this cemetery and was made to pay for it restoration. Yikes!
