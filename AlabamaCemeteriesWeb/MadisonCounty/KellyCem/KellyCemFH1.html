KELLY CEMETERY Kelly Cemetery Rd, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA

DAVISON, Infant daughter of S. E. & A. M. Davison, Born and died Nov. 6, 1865. 0628

The Lillie Davison and the infant daughter were twins. Note: Eight of the nine children of Amanda M. (Kelly) and Samuel E. Davison are listed above. Another daughter, Laura, born c. 1870 (from census records) died in infancy, is buried here but has no stone. After Amanda died Samuel Davison married a second time and had a son and two daughters, none of whom married. Thus, this man fathered 12 children and not a single one of them married although several lived many years.
