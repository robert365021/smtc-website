KELLY CEMETERY Kelly Cemetery Rd, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA

KELLY Jane,  (Concrete marker; no dates. Jane was the wife of J. L. T. Kelly. Her maiden name was Davison and she was born c. 1842 and died in 1897.) 0653

Josiah P., Martha E., Joshua 0., Laura, Amanda M., Susan J., Caladonia C., David E., Jr., and J. L. T. Kelly were the children of David E. and Nancy R. Kelly and their stones are listed here in the order of their birth. William Solon Kelly (1835-1926), the only other child of D. E. and Nancy R. Kelly, is buried in Cameron, Texas.
