DRAKE CEMETERY, Garth Rd, HUNTSVILLE, MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the John Drake Cemetery. This is at GPS coordinates of N 34.68353 W -86.55310. Drake Cemetery is on Garth Road near the intersection with Carl T. Jones Road in Jones Valley.

 At the intersection of Memorial Parkway & Airport Road go east on Airport Road past Whitesburg Drive where it becomes Carl T. Jones Dr.Follow that over the hill and at the bottom turn left on Garth Road. The Cemetery is on the right after turning right almost at the intersection.

Notice the yellow check indicating the cemetery location occupying an area inside a fence and adjacent to Garth Road SE.
