LOVE-WILBURN CEMETERY, near Douglas Rd., MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Bouldin-Hammond Cemetery of Madison Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map using Cem.

Go out Jordan Lane north to Douglas Road and thru left (west). Go about  1/2 mile and turn right (north) on Tall Sky Lane. After that bends back toward the east (right) turn left into Larks Aire Dr and dead end at the cemetery in front of you across the street from Falling Star Walk.

Here we see the more exact location of the cemetery under a tall stand of trees on Falling Star Walk.
