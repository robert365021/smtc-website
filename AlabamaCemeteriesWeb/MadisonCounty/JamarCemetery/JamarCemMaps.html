JAMAR CEMETERY, (Martin Road) MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This aerial map shows the GPS of the Jamar Cemetery as N 34.64781 W 86.72444.  Alternate Location: Section 3-4S-2W. SE SE SW NE. On Williams Road approx. one-half mile west of Redstone Arsenal Gate No. 7.

 From James Record Road and Martin Road go east on Martin Road about 3 miles. The road follows curves at first then finally goes due east. When it starts to bend back right continue onto the gravel road and at the thicket on the left you will find the cemetery behind privy hedge. The large monument sometimes is in view and when the hedge is not cut it is not seen. 

Notice the red check indicating the cemetery location under the Privy Hedge lined thicket next to the road.
