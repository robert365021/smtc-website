DONALDSON CEMETERY, (1 Mi east of Hazel Green on Jimmy Fisk Rd), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Donaldson Cemetery. The GPS according to Topozone above is N 34.91175 X W 86.5561. Alternate Location: Section 31-1-1E. Center of the SE NE. Located on a lane leading to a home off Jimmy Fisk Road, approximately one mile east of Highway 231-431.

Go north on Hwy 431/231 to Hazel Green and turn right at the junction of 231 & Grimwood Road. Go about 2/3 of a mile and turn left (North) and just before you reach the nursing home on the left turn left into the private driveway and with permission from the owners the cemetery is on your right under the trees behind a rock wall. Vandals beware - the neighbors & Madison County Sheriff are watching.

Notice the red check pointer indicating the cemetery location under the trees behind the Nursing home facility.
