BONE FAMILY CEMETERY,  Hurricane Creek Rd., MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This ACME Mapper Map 2.1 originally from Google Shows the GPS of the Bone Family Cemetery. This is at GPS coordinates of N. 34.79440 W. -86.40888.

 On Winchester Road turn right (east) on County Lake Road. Follow that until you find Hurricane Creek Road which turns right. So turn right (south) and go about 3 miles and the old Bone Plantation home is on your left. With permission walk north of the home toward the pasture and the cemetery is between the yard and pasture inside a concrete cinder block wall about as far from the road as the home.

This is a Google Street View of the old Bone home. The cemetery is just north (our left) of this view out in a pasture behind a cinder block wall.
