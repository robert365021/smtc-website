DANIEL-LAWLER CEMETERY, Ryland, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This ACME Mapper Map from Google Shows the GPS of the Daniel-Lawler Cemetery. This is at GPS coordinates of N 34.76848 W -86.42500.

 On Ryland Pike in Maysville go east 300 yards and turn south (right) into a private drive and with permission you will find the Daniel- Lawler Cemetery on the right behind a fence and the Drake-Keith Cem on your left behind a trailer home next to the wood line. These Cemeteries are about 200 yards south of the Ryland Pike.
