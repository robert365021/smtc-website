THOMPSON, Willie, 26 Oct 1797 - 11 Apr 1862 (Willie Thompson is often called Wyley or Wiley in other old records of this county. He died the day Federal troops captured the town of Huntsville during the
Civil War. It is said that Mr. Thompson saw the army coming and died of a heart attack. From the records here we learn he had two sons of fighting age who undoubtedly were fighting for the Confederacy. At this point maybe he also had not heard of their safety.) 3894
