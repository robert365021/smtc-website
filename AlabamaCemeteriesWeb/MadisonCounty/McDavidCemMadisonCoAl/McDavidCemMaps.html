McDAVID CEMETERY, Bobo Section Rd, MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the McDavid Cemetery of Madison Co., Alabama. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked with the red check GPS N 34.95777 X W -86.57397.
Alternate Location: Section 13, Township 1S, Range 1W. Center of E/2 NE/4.

At the intersection of Hwy 231 & Hwy 72 go north on Hwy 231/431 past Meridianville and on past Hazel Green. Veer left off of Hwy 231 on the north bound part of Bobo Section Rd and go about 1/4 of a mile and the cemetery is on the left behind the brick home and on the land of Betsy Gatlin. She will direct you to it.

Notice the red marker of the McDavid  Cemetery indicating the cemetery location. This zoomed in map with a red check shows where the cemetery can be found. In case you have advance permission from Ms Becky the cemetery is just beyond the open field following along the left side of that tree line westward and into the woods about 20 yards, under a giant Beech tree, that is decaying and dropping limbs.
