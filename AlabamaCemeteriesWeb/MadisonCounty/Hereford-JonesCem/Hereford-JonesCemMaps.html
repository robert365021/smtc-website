HEREFORD-JONES CEMETERY, (Mountain Fork Rd), MADISON COUNTY, ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Hereford-Jones Cemetery. The location is indicated in the center of the map marked at GPS N 34.92222 X W -86.39538. Specific Location: Section 27, Township 1S, Range 2E. SE corner of section. 2 1/2 miles northeast of New Market.

From Huntsville go northeast on Winchester Rd past New Market to Old Mountain Fork Rd and turn right. follow that and the road will bend back northward in about 1 1/2 miles. Bear left and when the road bends back to the east the cemetery is on your left under the trees and not visible from the road.

This aerial view shows a yellow highlighted outline of the cemetery premises.
