FARLEY-CRUTCHER CEMETERY (Shelton Rd) MADISON COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Farley-Crutcher Cemetery of Madison City, Madison County. Alabama. The location is indicated near the center of the map at N 34.70259 X W -86.71886.

At the intersection of Wall Triana Highway and Madison Pike go east on Madison Pike to Shelton Road and turn south (right) and go to Alderwood Dr and turn left and go to the bend of the road and turn right and go to the end of the dead end street and park. Follow the street into the woods 15 yards and turn due east (or left) and walk 350 yards east to the cemetery. Since this is all private land get permission. Stay in the woods though and do not cross the neighbor's grassy lawns.

Notice the instructions here for getting to this cemetery. You must have permission to use any route as this cemetery is far from the usual driving routes. There is another route but it is from Madison Pike and is a long field road. In most times of the year you will need a four wheel drive to use that route
