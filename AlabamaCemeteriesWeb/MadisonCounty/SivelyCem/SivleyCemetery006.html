SIVLEY CEMETERY, (John Hunt Park) HUNTSVILLE ALABAMA.

The Andrew Sivley monument to five members of his family. East side of monument.

SIVLEY, Lucy C., 22 May 1822 - 12 Oct 1834. (daughter of Rebecca Denton and Andrew Sivley; granddaughter of Jacob Sivley who was the senior member of the Sivley family pioneer to Madison County; Inscribed on the tall common marble obelisk with five other members of the Andrew Sivley)
