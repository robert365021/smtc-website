SIVLEY CEMETERY, (John Hunt Park) HUNTSVILLE ALABAMA.

The Andrew Sivley family monument showing the Eliza A. Cooper inscription on the south side of the monument

COOPER, Eliza A. (Sivley), 22 Sep 1813 - 20 Oct 1833. "Daughter of Andrew &: Rebecca Sivley" (Married John B. Cooper, May 31, 1832; Inscribed on the tall common marble obelisk with five other members of the Andrew Sivley family.) Inscription on south side of base.
