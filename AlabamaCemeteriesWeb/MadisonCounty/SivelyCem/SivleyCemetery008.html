SIVLEY CEMETERY, (John Hunt Park) HUNTSVILLE ALABAMA.

The third monument left from the Jacob Sivley monument on our right is for Joseph Sivley, son of Jacob & Alcey Sivley.

SILVEY, Joseph, Died 1818. Son. (Birthdate unknown; son of Jacob & Alcey Sivley; was married to Rachael Sivley from whom seven children were born.)
