SIVLEY CEMETERY, (John Hunt Park) HUNTSVILLE ALABAMA.

The stone on the left of Jacob's tall military marker is our subject monument. Alcey Sivley, wife of Jacob.

SILVEY, Alcey, Died 1818. (Birth unknown; wife of Jacob who was born during 1752; her birth date & maiden name are currently unknown. She was likely born in Shenandoah Valley Virginia as was her husband Jacob. They spent a significant part of their lives there before coming as pioneers to early Huntsville about 1809.)
