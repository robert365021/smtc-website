OLIVER-BEDINGFIELD CEMETERY, (Old Rogersville Rd), LAUDERDALE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Oliver-Bedingfield Cemetery.

In Rogersville take Lee Street west a mile and the cemetery is on the right in the junction of the road across from the Rogersville United Methodist Church.

Notice the Oliver-Bedingfield Cemetery at the junction of Turner Lane and Lauderdale County Rd 66.
