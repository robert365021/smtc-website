INGRAM-THORNTON (2-3/4 miles west of Rogersville just off of Hwy 72), LAUDERDALE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Ingram-Thornton Cemetery aka Thornton Cemetery.

From Rogersville at the intersection of Hwy 72 & Hwy 207 take Hwy 72 west about 2 & 3/4 miles and turn north on County Road 569. The cemetery is on your right about 500 yards from Highway 72 behind a fence.

Notice with this Aerial view we see the large cemetery on the right not far from Highway 72.
