DEAN-McNAIRY CEMETERY, (1 mi. S. of Hwy 72 between Rogersville & Elgin), LAUDERDALE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Liberty Cemetery.

In Rogersville turn west (or left going north) on Lee Street and go about 1/2 mile and the cemetery will be on your left on a slope under the trees.

Notice the Liberty Cemetery on the east side of County Rd 66 or also called Lee Street.
