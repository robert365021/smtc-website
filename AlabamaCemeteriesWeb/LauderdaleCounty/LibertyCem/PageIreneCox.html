LIBERTY CEMETERY, (Co Rd 66, aka Lee St., Rogersville), LAUDERDALE COUNTY, ALABAMA

PAGE, Irene Cox, 28 Jun 1901 - 25 Mar 1938. "Wife of Luther Page", "I have fought a good fight I have finished my course. I have kept the faith".

MRS. PAGE, 36, GOES TO REWARD - Final Rites Are Conducted This Afternoon

Mrs. Irene Page, aged 36, died at her home in Rogersville, East Lauderdale county, Friday noon after an illness of several months.
Surviving in addition to her husband, Luther Page, are two small daughters and other relatives.
Funeral services were conducted from the Rogersville Methodist church this afternoon at 2 o'clock with the pastor, Rev. W. E. Dean, officiating. Interment followed in Liberty cemetery with Spry, Rogersville, directing.
Active pallbearers were Otho Rose, Mike Beckman, Lewis Fuqua, C. P. Guttery, Bryan Dean, and Jerome Wilbanks.
Honorary pallbearers were Tom Andrews, Robert DeBusk, Ross Patterson, J. M. Pate, James Patton, and Dr. J. R. Wadell.
Published in the Florence Times, March 26, 1938. Ref: Kemu findagrave member #46580412. Sent in by Mary Bob Richardson 1 Jun 2011.
