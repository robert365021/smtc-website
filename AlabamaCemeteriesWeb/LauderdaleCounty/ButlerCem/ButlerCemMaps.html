BUTLER CEMETERY, (Wheeler Dam Road right on County Rd 84), LAUDERDALE COUNTY, ALABAMA

This topographical map shows the GPS of the Butler Cemetery.

At the intersection of Hwy 72 at Elgin Cross Road turn south on Wheeler Dam Road and go about 1 mile and turn left (east) on County Rd 84. When this road bends left continue straight on County Road 579. When County Rd 579 bends right 90 degrees look left at the fenced in area of the cemetery in a pasture, but next to the road where you will see the lone woodman of the World Monument.

Notice the yellow pointer indicating the cemetery location. Permission should be in sought from the nearby owners of the property.
