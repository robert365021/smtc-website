STEPHENSON-BURLESON CEMETERY Danville Rd, Decatur, MORGAN COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Stephen-Burleson Cemetery located  (south side) next to the Danville Church of Christ.  as N 34.56164 W 87.01744.

Go Hwy 67 (Beltline Drive) to Danville Road and turn south. follow that for less than a mile and look for the cemetery next to the (left) west side of the road inside an iron fence. You will need a key for the locked gate.
