BLACKWELL FAMILY CEMETERY, DECATUR, MORGAN COUNTY ALABAMA
This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Blackwell Family Cemetery located on the Decatur Golf and Country Club premises at N 34.56537 W 86.96689 in Decatur Alabama.
From 6th Ave at the Bridge over the Tennessee River travel south on down 6th Ave and turn left on 8th Ave and go several blocks and turn (south) right on Sommerville Rd. Follow that until it becomes Country Club Rd. SE, and go on to the country club. Turn right into the Decatur Golf and Country Club and go past the building to the Tennis court area in the back. The cemetery is on the east side of the Tennis coutts under the  tall black oak trees.
