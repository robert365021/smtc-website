KIMBELL CEMETERY, Trinity, U.L.A. Corporate property, MORGAN COUNTY ALABAMA
This topographical map shows the GPS of the Kimbell Cemetery.

This road map shows the directions: On Hwy 72/20 going west from Decatur go past the Beltline road on your left. travel another 2 miles and the cemetery will be on your right out from the highway about 100 yards. For permission see the guard at the gate on the left of Ret Hat Road or you could be in trouble here.

Notice the large rock wall surrounding the cemetery in this image. It is nearly 50 feet wide and 100 feet long.
