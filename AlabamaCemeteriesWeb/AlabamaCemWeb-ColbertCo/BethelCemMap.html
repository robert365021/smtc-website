Bethel Cemetery Map

Location: From the intersection of County Road 48 (County Line Road) and County Road 22 (Old Highway 20) in Leighton proceed approximately 8.5 miles south on County Line Road 48 aka Hwy 99. Old Bethel Baptist church is on the right (West) of the road and the cemetery is on the left (East) of the road. This church and cemetery are approximately 0.5 miles north of the Franklin Co. line.
