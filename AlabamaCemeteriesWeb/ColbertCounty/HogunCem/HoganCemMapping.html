HOGUN/(Hogan) aka LEE HI ACRES CEMETERY, (south of Gargis Lane in stand of trees), COLBERT COUNTY, ALABAMA
This Aerial map shows the GPS of the Hogan/(Hogun) Cemetery. ACME Mapper 2.0 says these coordinates are: N 34.69678 W 87.61923

To Reach this cemetery - from the intersection of Highway 72 and 157/20 go east on 157 until you come to Gargis Lane. Turn left (or north) and go about 1/4 mile and look left at the clump of trees on your left out in the field or subdivision. After another 1/4 mile turn left into the new road Cornelius Landing subdivision and then take the first left turn and travel until you are nearest the stand of trees. Go into the stand of trees where you will find the cemetery. You can see the last series of turns on the map below showing the unfinished subdivision. I cannot guarantee this will be the correct right of way to this cemetery in the future. This subdivision is under development and with houses lining the road it will become difficult to get into this cemetery without crossing private and very occupied premises. In that case use the field from Gargis Lane.
  
Notice the red check pointer indicating a group of trees under which the cemetery exist. Also notice the new streets with unfinished houses on them where you will need to negotiate to get to this cemetery in the future unless someone takes special efforts to get a reserved right-of-way.
