MAYERS CEMETERY (Old Hwy 20) COLBERT COUNTY ALABAMA

This aerial map shows the GPS of the Mayers Cemetery. ACME Mapper 2.0 says these coordinates are: N 34.69909 W 87.57755

To reach this cemetery from the intersection of Co. Road 22 (Old Hwy. 20.) and Hwy 133 (Wilson Dam Road ), Drive 3.2 miles on old Highway 20 to a gravel road. Pass Glendale Cemetery on your left and go another 100 yards and turn right into a field road that goes back toward an old home place & farm operation. Right after you turn into the field road there is a small clump of trees on the left where the cemetery resides.

Notice the red check indicating a small clump of trees under which is the cemetery is located on the south side of Old Hwy 20.
