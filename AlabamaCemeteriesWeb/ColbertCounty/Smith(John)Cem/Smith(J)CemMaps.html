SMITH (John) CEMETERY (Old Hwy 20 east of Leighton Al) COLBERT COUNTY ALABAMA

This aerial map shows the GPS of the John Smith Cemetery. ACME Mapper 2.0 says these coordinates are: N 34.69748 W 87.50941

To reach this cemetery from the intersection in Leighton Alabama, at the junction of of County Line Road and Old Hwy 20 go east toward Town Creek 1 mile and turn right or south into a field road and the cemetery is on the left of a thicket out in the field about 200 Feet from the road.

Notice the red check indicating the stones in the field surrounded by growing crops.
