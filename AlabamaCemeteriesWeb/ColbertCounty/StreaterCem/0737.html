STREATER CEMETERY, (along Highway 20 on S. side just east of Mathaler Lane), COLBERT COUNTY, ALABAMA

William Pleasant Pinion.

PENICK, William Pleasant, son of H.T. & M.J. Penick, 27 Sep 1879 - 27 Apr 1900. "Sleep on sweet child and take thy rest. God called the home, He thought it best." 
Parents are interred in the La Grange College Cemetery or at least the mother has a known listing there as Mary J. Pennick.
