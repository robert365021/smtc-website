OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, (Mt Pleasant Road aka Brick Road), COLBERT (eastern) COUNTY, ALABAMA
 

MINOR, Kathy Lynn, 13 Oct 1959 - 28 Feb 2013. 
 
Kathy Lynn Minor, age 53, of Muscle Shoals, died Thursday, Feb. 28, 2013. The family will receive friends Friday from 1-2 p.m. at East Colbert Church of Christ. Her service will follow at 2 p.m. at the church with Tim Spivy officiating. Burial will be in Old Brick Cemetery.
Ms. Minor was a member of East Colbert Church of Christ. She was preceded in death by her mother, Sarah Schulte Minor; grandparents, Paul and Vivian Minor; and niece, Ashley Brook Minor. 
She is survived by her father, Bobby Minor and wife, Betty Lou; brother, Randy Minor and wife, Debra; nieces, Dawn Moreland and husband, Keith, and Lori Marshall and husband, Michael; great nephews, Kane Moreland, Sam Marshall and Cash Marshall; great niece, Kyla Moreland; and special friend, Pat Moody. 
Pallbearers will be Keith Moreland, Michael Marshall, Josh Minor, Tommy Minor, Doug Minor, Jay Moody, Jackie Walker and Jerry Walker.
Published in Florence Times Daily on March 1, 2013
