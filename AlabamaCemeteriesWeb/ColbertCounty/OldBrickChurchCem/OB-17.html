OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, (Mt Pleasant Road aka Brick Road), COLBERT (eastern) COUNTY, ALABAMA
 

MALONE, Gypsie Laneal, 1948 - 24 Sept. 2013.
 
Mrs. Malone was a Colbert County native and attended Cottontown Baptist Church.

She is survived by her sons, Jamie Malone and Billy Reynolds Jr., both of Leighton; daughter, Julie Malone, of Tuscumbia; brother, John Whitlock Harrison, of Sheffield; sister, Bonnie Bates, of Muscle Shoals; two grandchildren; and two great-grandchildren.
Excerpts from obituary published In the Florence Times Daily on September 26, 2013.
