OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, (Mt Pleasant Road aka Brick Road), COLBERT (eastern) COUNTY, ALABAMA
 

NICHOLS, William J. 9 Apr 1872 - 5 Jan 1952. "Father".  (shares stone with Martha E. Nichols.) 7369
Times Daily - January 7, 1952
William Nichols Dies In Leighton Saturday
William J. Nichols, 80, Leighton route one, died at home Saturday night at 8:20. Funeral arrangements will be announced later pending arrival of relatives.
Active bearers will be Fred Simmons, Bruce McGregory, M.D. McGregory, Bruce Smith, Quinton Smith, and Howard Wimberly.
Survivors are the wife, Martha; four sons, Alfred of Miss., Lillborn of Texas, Brook of Mt. Hope and Luther of Leighton, two daughters, Mrs. Julie Weldon of Calif., and Mrs. Herbert Aday of Leighton; two brothers, Johnny of Leighton and Huston of Tennessee; one sister, Myrrow of Tennessee, 45 grandchildren and 40 great grandchildren.
