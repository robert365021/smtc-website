LD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, (Mt Pleasant Road aka Brick Road), COLBERT (eastern) COUNTY, ALABAMA
 


 
 
LEDLOW, Paulette Peden, 13 Dec. 1962 - 27 Sep. 2015. (d/o Howard & Jan Peden; w/o Gary K. Ledlow.) 
 

Paulette Peden Ledlow finished her journey here on earth on Sunday, Sept. 27, 2015, at Helen Keller Hospital.
Funeral services will be on Sept. 30, 2015, at Colbert Memorial Chapel, with Eddie Osborn officiating. Visitation will be 11 a.m. to 1 p.m., with the funeral at 1 p.m. in the chapel. Burial will be at Old Brick Cemetery.
She was preceded in death by her parents, Howard and Jan Peden, and a brother, Larry Peden.
She is survived by her husband of 27 years, Gary K. Ledlow; two sons, Casey and Brady Ledlow; two brothers, Paul (her twin) and Leslie Peden; one sister, Tina Duncan; and several nieces and nephews.
She was a hard worker and was always trying to please others. She enjoyed spending time at the shop with family and friends.
Special thanks to the PCU staff at Helen Keller Hospital for their kindness and quality care shown to all of us. Also, special thanks to her sister-in-law, Carol Ledlow, for her care giving.
Pallbearers will be Allen Glover, Billy Landers, A.J. Ledlow, Richard Leslie, Jason Stone and Jeremy Stone.
