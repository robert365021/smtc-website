SAINT, Olin,  16 Oct 1906 - 28 Feb 1950. (native of Colbert County died in a construction accident  in Lucedale, George County, Mississippi. Interred here in an
unmarked grave as of 2010 if the obituary is correct & interpreted accordingly.)
 
Florence Times Daily, Mar. 1, 1950, p. 12 (Obituary)

Rites For O. Saint Set Thursday At 1 pm

Funeral services for Olon Saint, 43, formerly of Tuscumbia, who was killed Monday afternoon at Lucedale, Miss. Near Hattiesburg while
working with a bridge construction crew, will be held Thursday afternoon at 1 o'clock from the residence of a brother, Claud Saint, 504 West Fifth street,
Tuscumbia. Rev. J. L. Gaines, pastor of the Calvary Baptist church, Tuscumbia, will officiate. Burial will be in Old Brick cemetery, Brown-Services, Tuscumbia in charge.

Mr. Saint had been with the bridge construction crew for nine years and had been in Mississippi for the past six months. He was born in Colbert county.

Surviving are a son, Jimmy, a daughter Delores of Florence, N. C.; his mother, Mrs. Susie Saint, Tuscumbia; five brothers, Claud, Alvie, Grady, O'Neal,
Elree, all of Tuscumbia; two sisters, Miss Pearlie saint, Tuscumbia, and Mrs. Pauline Aday, Sheffield, route 1.

Bearers will be members of Iron Workers Union No. 600, Mobile.
