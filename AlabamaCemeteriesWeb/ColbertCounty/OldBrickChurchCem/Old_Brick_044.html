OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, (Mt Pleasant Road aka Brick Road), COLBERT (eastern) COUNTY, ALABAMA

MYRICK, Martha Lou, 10 Aug 1937 - 1 Feb 2013. (w/o Curtis E. Myrick.) obit

Martha Lou Myrick, age 75, of Muscle Shoals, died Friday, Feb. 1, 2013.
Visitation will be 2-4 p.m. Sunday, Feb. 3, at Spry-Williams Funeral Home of Florence.
She was a member of Grace Life Baptist Church of Muscle Shoals.
She was preceded in death by her husband, Curtis E. Myrick.
Survivors are her sons, Billy Brewer (Ginnie), of Sheffield, David Brewer (Beverly), of Florence, and Jesse Wayne Brewer (Falena), of Florence; sister, Alice Holland, of Daytona, Fla.; eight grandchildren; six great-grandchildren; and a number of nieces and nephews.
In lieu of flowers, memorials may be made to the charity of your choice.
Published in Florence Times Daily on February 3, 2013

Sent in by Mary Bob McClain Richardson. 5 Mar 2015
