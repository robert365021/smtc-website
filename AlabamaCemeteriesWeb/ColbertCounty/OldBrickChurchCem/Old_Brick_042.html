OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, (Mt Pleasant Road aka Brick Road), COLBERT (eastern) COUNTY, ALABAMA


HOVATER, Roxane Hennigan, d. 23 Feb 2011. (d/o Isabell Hennigan.) Obituary

Roxane Hennigan Hovater, 49 of Mount Hope, died Wednesday, February 23, 2011. Visitation will be Saturday, February 26, 2011 from 12:00-2:00 p.m. at Morrison Funeral Home in Tuscumbia with the funeral service following in the funeral home chapel with Dr. Charles Marshall officiating. Interment will follow in Old Brick Cemetery.
Roxane was a native of Colbert County. She was a member of Mt. Hope Church of Christ. She was a graduate of Colbert County High School and earned an Associate of Applied Science in design drafting from Shoals Community College. She retired from the Alabama Department of Transportation after 25 years of service.
Roxane loved to sew, quilt, cook, farm and work in her flower garden. She was a loving wife, mother, daughter and grandmother.
She is preceded in death by her mother, Isabel Hennigan and sister, Kim Hennigan.
She is survived by her husband, Jeff Hovater; daughters, Amy Hovater Moore (Mike), Abby Hovater Bernauer (Matt) and Kerri Hovater; father, Stuart Hennigan; brother, Greg Hennigan (Belinda); and three grandchildren, Brian, Madison and Zachary Moore.
Pallbearers are Sean Hennigan, John Marshall, Jason Kerby, Dalton Kerby, Granvil Coons and Dwight Daves.
Honorary pallbearers are Clay Clemons, Lcpl Andrew Morris, Dalton Daves and Ingram Collier.
Morrison Funeral Home in Tuscumbia is directing.
