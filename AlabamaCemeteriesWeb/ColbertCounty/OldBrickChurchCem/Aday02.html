ADAY, Helen (Marie) Keenum, 22 Mar 1936 - 20 Aug 2001. "Married Aug 22, 1953". (Sheffield, Al. native; d/o late George Keenum  and Clara Keenum; mar. 48 yrs. to Weart Aday and shares same stone; preceded in death by son, Rex Aday.) 8120  obit
 
Published on: 8/22/2001 
Helen M. Aday
Helen Marie Aday , 65 , of Muscle Shoals , Ala. , died Monday , Aug. 20 , 2001
Visitation will be 6-8 p.m. Wednesday , Aug. 22 , 2001 , at Morrison Funeral Home , Tuscumbia.The funeral will be at 2 p.m. Thursday , Aug. 23 , 2001 , at Morrison Funeral Home chapel , with burial in Old Brick Presbyterian Cemetery. Mrs. Aday was a native of Sheffield and a member of the Nazarene Church. She is preceded in death by her father , George Keenum ; and son , Rex Aday. Survivors include her husband of 48 years , Weart Aday of Muscle Shoals ; mother , Clara Keenum of Tuscumbia ; son , David Aday of Tuscumbia ; daughter , Ida Wellington and husband , Buddy , of Tuscumbia ; daughter-in-law , Brenda Aday of Sheffield ; brother , Robert Keenum of Morganton , N.C. ; sisters , Glenice Thomas and husband , John , of Morganton , N.C. , Nannie Lovelady and husband , Woodrow , of Hatton , Ala. , Ardith Berryman and husband , Danny , also of Hatton , Ala. ; half-sister , Rebecca Fitzgerald of Columbus , Tenn. ; grandchildren , Edward Wellington and wife , Cheryl , of Sheffield , Amanda Morgan and husband , Howard , of Tuscumbia , Ala. , Tara Aday of Morganton , N.C. ; four great-grandchildren , Cody Wellington , Ashley Wellington , both of Sheffield , Lane Morgan and Hannah Morgan , both of Tuscumbia ; special friends , Dan and Georgie Ledlow , Guy Jr. and Doyle Jean Aday.
