OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, (Mt Pleasant Road aka Brick Road), COLBERT (eastern) COUNTY, ALABAMA


CLARK, Belle P., 25 Mar 1916 - 31 Mar 2001. (shares stone with Joe A. Clark)
 
CLARK, Bell, 85, of Tullahoma, formerly of Estill Springs, died Saturday, March 31,2001. She was preceded in death by her husband, Joe Clark, and a son, Jimmy Ray Clark. Survivors include two daughters, Kathleen (Jimmy) Gullick, Tuscumbia, Ala., and JoAnn Martin, Tullahoma; three sons, Billy Joe Clark, Tullahoma; Gary (Gail) Clark, Estill Springs, and Mike (Tricia) Clark, Georgia; nine grandchildren, 18 great-grandchildren and seven great-great-grandchildren. Morrison Funeral Home Tuscumbia, Ala., was in charge of arrangements.
