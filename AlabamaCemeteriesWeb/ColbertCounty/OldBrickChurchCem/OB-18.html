OLD BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, (Mt Pleasant Road aka Brick Road), COLBERT (eastern) COUNTY, ALABAMA
 

 
MARTIN, Joann Clark, 28 Jan 1939 - 2 Jan 2004. "Precious Lord Take my hand", "Loving Sister & Mother". 
 
Jo Ann Clark Martin, 64, of Sheffield, died Friday, Jan. 2, 2004.
Visitation will be 6-8 p.m. Sunday, Jan 4, 2004 at Morrison Funeral Home, Tuscumbia.
The funeral services will be at 2 p.m. Monday at the funeral home chapel, with burial at Old Brick Cemetery.
Brother Reggie Bruce will officiate.
Mrs. Martin was a native of Decatur and lived most of her life in Tullahoma, Tenn., before moving to Sheffield.
She is survived by her sons, Bobby Joe Whipple, of Florence, Mark Whipple, of Tuscumbia, and Steve Whipple, of Florence; daughter, Debra Pursley, of Tullahoma; brothers, Billy Joe Clark, of Tullahoma, and Gary Wayne Clark, of Estill Springs, Tenn.; sister, Kathryn L. Gullick, of Tuscumbia; 11 grandchildren and 14 great-grandchildren.
Pallbearers will be Randal Cyree, Tommy Miller, Dale Phillips, Gary Taylor, Chris Darby and Oneal Price.
Morrison Funeral Home, Tuscumbia, is directing.
Times Daily/Sunday, January 4, 2004
