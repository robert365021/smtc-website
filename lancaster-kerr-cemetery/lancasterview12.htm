LANCASTER-KERR CEMETERY-BRYANT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Looking east but from a more southward position from the road. Not much visible in this scene because of the high brush but unmarked & field stones are all through the brush. Just in front of the tree and pile of rocks is the stone of Joseph Wright.  Photo by WA. 5/31/2003
