LANCASTER-KERR CEMETERY-BRYANT ROAD, MAURY COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Looking east but from a more southward position from the road. The Nancy Scribner Stone is just behind the tree on your right. Not much visible in this scene because of the high brush but the Smith stone can be seen back in the center left. Just to the left of the big tree on your left is the unmarked stone of the 1930s-1940s style that is not inscribed.  Photo by WA. 5/31/2003
