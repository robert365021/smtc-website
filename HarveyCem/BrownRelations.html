Ruby Thomas's Mother (Alma Alice Brown Harris) is not buried in the Harvey Cemetery. She is in the Bethlehem Cemetery with her late husband, Lyndon Grady Harris.
Walter Brown was Alma Alice Brown Harris's father, James David "Jim" Brown was her Mother's grandfather (Walter's father). His name on the rock is Jim.
Margaret Alice Harvey Brown was her Mother's grandmother (Walter's Mother). Dave Brown was Walter's brother, with his wife Minnie buried beside him. James Marion Brown was Walter's brother. Joe McKinley Brown was Walter's brother. Tivie Brown was Joe McKinley Brown's wife. Willie R Brown was Walter's brother and shares a rock with his wife Ellen L.
Charles Marion Harvey and Martha J. Hargrove were Mother's great grandparents being the parents of Margaret Alice Harvey Brown.
Charles Lee Harvey was the son of Charles Marion and Martha J and therefore Margaret Alice's brother. Charles Lee shares a rock with his wife Sarah Elizabeth. So you see Ruby has lots of kin there.
Ruby's Mother's Mother, Johnnie Mae Brown, was a widow for many years and per her desire she is buried in the nearby Ring Cemetery in Pottsville.
