MT NEBO CEMETERY,  SAWDUST, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

SHIPP, Ollie Ray, 3 Dec 1902 - 6 Apr, 1936. (d/o Andrew Wray & Queen Victoria George Ray; w/o Burton Shipp.)

Tennessee Death Certificate has Ollie Shipp of Century Mines, Maury County, TN, born 3 Dec 1902 in TN to Andrew Ray and Queenie George, both born in TN; she was married to Buton Shipp; died 5 Apr 1936 in Holland Mines, Maury County, TN at age 34 due to injury due to tornado - nature of injury was fractured leg and general concussion. Buried 8 Apr 1936 at Nebo Cemetery. Informant was Mr Will Ray of Louisville, Jefferson County, KY.
