ADKISON CEMETERY, SANTA FE, (West) MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE

Mr. Fred's instructions of 1987: From Hilltown go east (or right) on Chestnut Ridge Rd for 1 & 1/4 miles and turn right (or south) on Lewis Rd. The cemetery is on the right 1/4 mile down Lewis Road road.
Another set of instructions: From Santa Fe go northwest on Hwy 7 (Fly Road) for a short distance and turn north (right) on Lewis Road. Follow Lewis Road for about 2 miles and the cemetery is on your left.
