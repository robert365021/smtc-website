DUNNAVANT CEMETERY, Newman Rd, near Elkton, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE


KING, Ann H. King, wife of G. L. King, Aug 1815 - 28 Dec 1872. (can still make out the death date of "Dec 28, 1872") (stone broken thru the death date.)(Nee Nancy Ann Howard Burrow King, b. Aug., 1815 Warren County North Carolina, USA Death: Dec. 28, 1872 Giles County Tennessee, USA "Annie" the wife of Griffin L. King. Married Sept. 21, 1833. Mr. Tom King believes Nancy Howard Burrow King & Ann H King to be the same lady. The tombstone inscription confirms her name is Ann H. King (or possibly Annie H King, not so clear). Tombstone inscribed as: "Ann H. King, wife of G. L. King born Aug 1815 Died Dec 28, 1872." [Ref. Tom King on FindAGrave and further analysis by Wayne Austin, 5 Jan 2010.
