LOYD CEMETERY  (Stephens Creek Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Loyd Cemetery as 35.18741 X -86.42698.

 Location: From Fayetteville go Hwy 64 east to Hwy 50 then left and on Past Mulberry to Crystal Ridge Rd and turn right. Follow that for more than 2 miles to Stephens Creek Rd and turn right. Follow that for about 1 1/2 miles and the cemetery is on the right behind a house under a cedar tree.  Get permission from the land owner to get into this cemetery.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly in a grove of trees next to the road. The marking above as Loyd Cemetery is incorrect but it is actually due north 80 feet under the cedar seen above.
