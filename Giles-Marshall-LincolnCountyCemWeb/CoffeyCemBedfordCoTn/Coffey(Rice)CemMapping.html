COFFEY (Rice) FAMILY CEMETERY, Higgins Rd., Wartrace, BEDFORD COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Rice Coffey Family Cemetery as N 35.52442 X W -86.34226.

Location: From Shelbyville go east on Alt Hwy 41 to Wartrace Pike Hwy 64. Follow Hwy 64 for something less than 5 miles and turn left on West Rd. and follow that until it crosses the RR tracks and becomes Higgings Rd. Follow Higgings Rd another 1/2 mile and left into a long private drive. The cemetery is generally well kept very near (to your right of) someone's front yard.
