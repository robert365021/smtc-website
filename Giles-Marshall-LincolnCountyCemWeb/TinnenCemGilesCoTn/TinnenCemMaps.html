TINNEN (Alexander) CEMETERY, Hwy 31, west side, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Tinnen Cemetery as N 35.26383 X W -87.04518. 

From Pulaski go north on Highway 31 about 4 miles, but first you will pass Industrial Loop Road, then Guthrie Rd/Pigeon Roost Rd & lastly pass Annie White Road all on the left. After another mile you will see the home a white siding house for what is today the Archie Williams & wife farm, a country cattle farmer. This is on your left upon the slope. This used to be the home of the William & Mary (Myers) Reynolds, but not sure if the home is new. Archie is the current caretaker of the Judge Lee farm, the Tinnon (white) & Myers (black) Cemeteries. The Shadden (also white) Cemetery also is on this place, but far back into the woodland. If your intentions are honorable Archie or his wife can allow or accompany you to either the recent Myers or the very old Tinnon cemetery. The Cemetery resides about 250 yards behind Archie's home. Archie's wife's (who is the daughter of William & Mary Reynolds) parents & grandparents are interred in the Myers Cemetery. Myers Cemetery is near the front of the field road and the Tinnon Cemetery is back from that about 30 yards. This is back southwest of the home. I was informed because of the rocky nature of the soil there will be no further burials in the Myers Cemetery.
