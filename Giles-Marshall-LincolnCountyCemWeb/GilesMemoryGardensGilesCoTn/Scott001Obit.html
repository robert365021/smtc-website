SCOTT, Mary Kathryn [Dixon], 25 Aug 1940 - 02 Jun 2016, w/o Earnest Floyd Scott, d/o Monnie Alton & Bessie Laverne Milner Dixon, Devotion 52A1


SCOTT, Mary Kathryn Dixon, Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home 02 Jun 2016
Mary Kathryn Scott of Pulaski passed away on Thursday, June 02, 2016 at Maury Regional Medical Center. She was born in Sulfur Springs, Texas on August 25, 1940 and was 75 years old. She was a loving mother, grandmother and devoted to her family. She was a long time employee of Pulaski Jewelry. Visitation will be Saturday, June 4th from 1:00pm until 3:00pm at Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home. Funeral Service will take place Saturday, June 4th at 3:00pm in the chapel of the funeral home. Burial will follow in Giles Memory Gardens. Memorial suggestions as asked to go to a church of your choice. She was preceded in death by her husband, Ernest F. Scott and sister, Sandra Stewart. She is survived by daughter, Lynnette Shellhouse of Pulaski; son, Tracy Scott of Pulaski; grandsons, Scott Shellhouse and Jeremiah Scott; granddaughter, Ashley Hedrick; sister, Sue King of Pulaski; brothers, Norman Dixon and Wesley Dixon both of Pine Bluff AR, and Mike Dixon of Jonesboro AR. Several nieces, nephews, great nieces, and great nephews also survive. Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home is in charge of all arrangements for Mary Kathryn Scott.
