McGEE, Thomas Fagan, 26 Aug 1942 - 28 Aug. 2013. h/o Joan Holley McGee & s/o Herman Wesley & Lillie Florence Snow McGee, Devotion 178A2 obit


Thomas Fagan (Bud) McGee, age 71, died Wednesday, Aug. 28, at his home in the Bodenham Community.

Mr. McGee was born in Giles County and retired from Murray Ohio and was a farmer.

Visitation will be Saturday from 4-8 p.m. at Carr and Erwin Funeral Home. Funeral services will be Sunday at 3:30 p.m. at Carr and Erwin Funeral Home with burial in Giles Memory Garden.

Survivors are his wife Jo Ann Holley McGee of the Bodenham Community; sons Tommy McGee and wife Beth and Mike McGee and wife Kimba all of Bodenham; daughter Keila Smith and husband Rickey of Ethridge; brothers J.C. McGee of Pulaski, and William Edward McGee of Mississippi; sister Sandra Wasson of Bodenham and five grandchildren.
Obituary Sent in by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 26 Jun 2016.
