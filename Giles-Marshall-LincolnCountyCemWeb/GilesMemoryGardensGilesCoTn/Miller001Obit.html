MILLER, Lillian [Hosay], 29 Oct 1924 - 08 Jun 2016, w/o Robert Allen Miller/J. Burrow McNeese, d/o Oliver Leslie & Martha L. Hurt Hosay, Devotion 12C3


McNEESE, Lillian Hosay Miller, Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home 06 Jun 2016

(10/29/2016)

Lillian Hosay Miller McNeese of Pulaski passed away on Wednesday, June 8, 2016 at NHC Healthcare in Pulaski. She was born in Nashville, TN. on October 29, 1924 and was 91 years old. She was a homemaker, loving wife, mother, grandmother, and great-grandmother and great great-grandmother. Lillian was the longest active member of First Baptist Church in Pulaski.Visitation will be on Friday, June 10th from 4:00pm until 7:00pm at Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home. Funeral Service will follow on Saturday, June 11th at 3:00pm in the Chapel of Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home with Tony Gomillion officiating. Burial will take place in Giles Memory Gardens. Memorial donations are suggested to St. Jude Childrens Research Hospital in memory of her grandson, Joel Blake Hutton, 501 St. Jude Place, Memphis, Tn. 38105. She was preceded in death by her parents, Oliver Leslie and Martha Hurt Hosay. Her daughter, Mary Ann Miller, son, Bobby Miller and grandson, Blake Hutton. Her husbands, Robert Allen Miller, Sr and James Burrow McNeese, and brothers, Oliver Hosay, and Jim Hosay. She is survived by her sons, Danny (Yvonne) Miller of Montgomery, Alabama, Son, Doug (Dede) Miller of Pulaski, Randy (Jane) Miller of Columbia; daughters, Elaine (Larry) Hutton of Pulaski, Janice (David) Dunavant of Campbellsville, Nancy (Terry) Williams of Smyrna, brother, Charlie (Jane) Hosay of Norfolk, Virginia; daughter-in-law, Martha Sue Miller of Chattanooga; 20 grandchildren, 34 great-grandchildren and 1 great-great-grandchild and numerous nieces and nephews also survive. Mrs. McNEESE was buried beside her 1st husband, Robert Allen Miller.
Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home in charge of arrangements for Lillian Hosay Miller McNeese.
