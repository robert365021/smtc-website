WOLLARD, James E. Citizen-Press 14 Sep 2004

Mr. James E. Wollard, 67, of Prospect died Sept. 30, 2004, at his residence. He was the son of the late James Townsend and Mary Margaret Wollard and was a member of Bethel Masonic Lodge No. 194 and Bethel Methodist Church. Masonic Services was held Sept. 11 immediately following visitation at Bennett-May Funeral Home. Funeral services were held Sept. 12 in the chapel of Bennett-May Funeral Home with the Rev. Jeff Young officiating. Burial was in Giles Memory Garden in Pulaski. Bennett May and Giles County Funeral Homes are in charge of arrangements. Survivors include his wife, Dorothy Wollard of Prospect; son Howard Wollard of Prospect; daughter, Linda Brown of Chapel Hill; sister, Juanita Elders of Festus, Mo.; and two grandchildren.

WOLLARD, Dorothy M. Kirby, 20 Dec. 1945 - 11 Aug. 2013. Married Feb. 27, 1990 (w/o James Ernest Wollard.)

Dorothy Kirby Wollard of Prospect, passed away Sunday, August 11, 2013 in Birmingham, Alabama.
She was born on December 20, 1945 in Moraur, Illinois and was 67 years old.
Visitation will be on Tuesday, August 13th from 4:00pm until 8:00pm at Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home.
Funeral Service will be on Wednesday, August 14th at 3:00pm in the Chapel of Bennett-May-Giles Giles County Funeral Home.
Burial will follow in Giles Memory Gardens.
She was preceded in death by her, Husband, James Ernest Wollard
She is survived by her, Son, Howard Wollard of Prospect
