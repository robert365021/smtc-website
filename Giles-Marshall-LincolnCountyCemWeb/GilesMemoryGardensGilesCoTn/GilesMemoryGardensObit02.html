GILES MEMORY GARDENS, Hwy 64 Bypass/Bethel

NOLLER, Harold David, 31 Dec 1940 - 30 Mar 2011. s/o William & Jesse Tomkins Noller; h/o May (?) Noller.


Harold David Noller, age 70, died on Wednesday, 3-30-2011, at his home.

He was born in Newburgh, New York on December 31,1940, worked for the Marshall County Board of Education in transportation, his parents were the late William and Jesse Tomkins Noller. He was a member of the Moose Lodge and the American Legion.

Survivors are: wife, May Noller of Pulaski, TN, daughters, Jessica Ryder and husband Frederick, Jennifer Noller, Jeannette and husband Kenneth David Sheets all of Pulaski, TN, 8 grandchildren, 2 great grandchildren.

Funeral services held at the Carr & Erwin Funeral Home with burial in Giles Memory Garden.
