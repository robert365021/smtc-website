DABNEY CEMETERY #2, Charlie Thomas Road, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Dabney Cemetery aka Dabney Cemetery #2 as N 35.32269 X W -86.89739. 

From Exit 22 of I-65 go southeast on Hwy 31 Alt to Buford Station Road and turn right. Go a 3 tenths of a mile and turn right (north) into Hart Leonard Road. Follow that up the slope until it goes into Marshall County and bear right (east) on Hooker Rd aka Charlie Thomas Rd. That  road goes back into Giles Co and then back into Marshall Co again changing road names. When the road turns 90 degrees (right) toward the east go about 300 yards further and turn left up into the drive of the old Dabney Farm home road now belonging to others. The cemetery is between the house and road along the road. You will see it with the box tombs and other monuments on your left.
