Photographed on 4 May  2016 by C. Wayne Austin. Past inscriptions were updated with any exception found. This publication culminated from various sources and attempts to portray this cemetery as complete as possible. Listed on page 181 of the book Tombstone Inscriptions of Marshall County Cemeteries by the Tim & Helen Marsh & some by Ralph Whitesell, read abt 1978. Added here 19 May 2016 by CWA. Findagrave.com has also listed this cemetery, but missed major stones & Emily Smith was reported as a Dabney.
