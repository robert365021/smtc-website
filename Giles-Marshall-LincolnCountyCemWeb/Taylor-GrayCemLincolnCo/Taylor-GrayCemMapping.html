TAYLOR-GRAY CEMETERY, Teal Hollow Rd, Kelso, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Taylor-Gray Cemetery as N 35.11887 X W -86.46568.

 Location: From Fayetteville go east on Hwy 64 to Kelso and turn (right) south on Teal Hollow Rd or Old Highway 64. Go to the Main Kelso intersection and turn left (southeast) following Teal Hollow Rd. Follow that a fw hundred yards until you see a slope on your left and up a slope will be a new home. Just past that and down in front (to your right of the home) is the cemetery under a grove of trees. The gentleman who owns the home is the owner of the land surrounding the cemetery, so permission is needed. He was the one that pointed this cemetery out to me when I was at Kelso Cemetery. It is well mapped. Careful here the flesh ripping Saw Briars are rough in this cemetery. Wear thick clothing
