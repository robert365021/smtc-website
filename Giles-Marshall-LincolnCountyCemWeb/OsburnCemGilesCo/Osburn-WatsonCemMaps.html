OSBURN-WATSON CEMETERY, Indian Creek Rd., GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Osburn-Watson Cemetery. 

 Location: Hwy 64E, right on Pisgah Road, turn right north side of church, on road marked as Braly Hollow Rd., go about 2 miles and the road merges with Indian Creek Road coming from the northeast. Go about a 1/2 mile and turn right into the long driveway and the gentleman there may accompany you to the cemetery if you give him advance notice. This was the Jim Sanders Place in 1986. The cemetery is upon a steep slope on your left as you near the house at the end of the drive about 500 yards due south of you in a stand of trees as marked on the below map. It is also across a creek where you will get your feet wet without boots.

With this aerial map we see the cemetery as outlined by the yellow highlighter near the top of the irregular shaped stand of trees. The creek goes in a circle around the base of the steep hill. North of this place is the residential road to the home as seen in the first map above.
