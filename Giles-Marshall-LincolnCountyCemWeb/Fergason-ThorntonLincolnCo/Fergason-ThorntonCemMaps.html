FERGASON-THORNTON CEMETERY, Belleville, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Fergason-Thornton Cemetery as N 35.27874 X W -86.55041.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 and after you pass Wiley Hollow Rd look to your left behind a home which sits back from the road about 200 yards and the cemetery is due west behind that house in a pasture. It was once fenced with a chain link fence, but that has been pushed over by livestock and no longer protects the tombstones.

This pictorial view shows the cemetery in the distance in a pasture out about 120 yards.
