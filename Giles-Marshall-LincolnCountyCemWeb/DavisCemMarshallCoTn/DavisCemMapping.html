DAVIS CEMETERY, Ostella Road, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the Davis Cemetery GPS location as N 35.32356 X W -86.81581. The yellow check or center of the cemetery was selected as the plotting point.

Location: From Hwy 11 Alt 31 select Ostella Rd and go past the New Hope Cemetery on your left. Make the next bend in the road back left and go about a half mile and you will see the cemetery. It is located above the road on the left spanning up the slope. The entrance road is a bit steep but will accommodate your vehicle.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly surrounded by the yellow graphic line. The entrance is from the road with a gravel road available for parking.
