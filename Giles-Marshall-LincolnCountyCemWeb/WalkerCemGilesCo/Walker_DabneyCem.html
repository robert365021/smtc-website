Many footstone markers without dates or headstones abound here. Several graves have sunken. At least two tombstones are fallen. Deep weeds have grown up around the stones making the stones hard to find. Better to visit this cemetery in the Winter time. Because of the deep weeds and possible chiggers I did not find and therefore confirm all of the memorials as reflected in the ones which were photographed. [WA 28 Apr 2009]
