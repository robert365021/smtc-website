WALKER CEMETERY, Highway 129, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Shown as the Dabney Cemetery on USGS mapping. The major progenitors appear to the be Walkers and thus this cemetery should be known as the Walker or Walker-Dabney Cemetery. It is known as the Walker Cemetery in Giles County Historical circles. It is also called the Dabney-Walker Cemetery.

Going north on Highway 31, go right (east) at Old Lynnville (Waco) on Hwy 129 , pass thru Lynnville. Pass by Round Hill then Mullins Road both on left and the Cemetery is on the left on the old R. L. Sands farm about 400 yards north of the road & northwest of the Sands home. A son owns the property today. From the road in the wintertime a rock fence is seen surrounding the cemetery.
