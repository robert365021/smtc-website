EAGIN CEMETERY, (Baugh Rd next to the Rails on Eagin Cem. Rd)GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map plots GPS Coordinates of the Eagin Cemetery.

Location from Pulaski: Take highway 31 south to Elkton bearing to the left toward Elkton. Go east on Baugh Road a couple of miles to Eagin Cemetery Road toward the Rail Road Tracks. Once across the tracks look right in the field inside a chain link fence at the cemetery. This is near a home as this road also serves Adams Road and a residential Road.

This aerial view shows the cemetery marked with a red check on the west side of the the Rails and it is on the east side of a home.
