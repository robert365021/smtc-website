PITTS (RW) CEMETERY, John Campbell Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the R W Pitts Cemetery as N 35.12130 X W -86.69082.
Location: From Fayetteville go west out Hwy 64 to Old Elkton Pike (Hwy 273) and turn left. Follow that to Old Molina Rd and turn left. Follow that to Luttrell Rd and turn right and then back left on Dyer Rd to John Campbell Rd and turn left. When the road bends to the west (right) the residential road there on the left is for the home where the cemetery sits in back of the house and barns, but beside an equipment shed on the west side, in a clump of trees, and inside an iron fence.

This aerial map shows the cemetery beside an equipment shed.
