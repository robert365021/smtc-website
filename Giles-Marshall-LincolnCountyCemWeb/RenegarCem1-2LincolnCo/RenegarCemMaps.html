RENEGAR CEMETERY #1, (Parks Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Renegar Cemetery as N 35.19717 X W 86.47988.
Location: From Fayetteville go east of Hwy 64 to Hwy 50 and turn north. Travel then to Parks Road several miles away and turn south on that. Follow Parks Road for a mile and the cemetery is on a high security & very private Griffin-Mulberry farm. You will be unable to gain access to this cemetery even though you can see it from the gate. The gate has coded access and perhaps other security instrumentation.

This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly toward the end of Parks Road. As shown above it is inside a tall well built rock wall.
