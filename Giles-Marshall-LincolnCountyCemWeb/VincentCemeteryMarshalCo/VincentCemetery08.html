VINCENT CEMETERY, Near MOORESVILLE, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
Unknown fragments with the "Dec.", no other information or stones found.

Footstones are mostly all that remains here. Folks this is the work of a vandal, also known as the "Scum of the Earth." A tree falling on a stone will not cause widespread random scattering and missing pieces of stone that I found here. The works of a Vandal generally manifest itself this way. First they take stones and smash them together and break as many as they can. Next they engage in a throwing party, scattering the fragments around causing actually more damage than breaking them, since epoxy glue can repair them if found.
