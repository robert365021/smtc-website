MARY'S GROVE CEMETERY, (Hwy 110, Kirkland), LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Mary's Grove Cemetery as N 35.05577 X W 86.64125.

 Location: From Fayetteville Tennessee go south then west on Hwy 110 (Ardmore Hwy) Thru Skinem and also past Kirkland Church on your right. After passing the church go .6 of a mile and look left out into the field at a grove of trees where the cemetery is located. The folks just before the cemetery on the left are the owners of the land. They have reserved a graveled parking place for visitors though, but will not allow you to drive to this cemetery. Just walk.

This aerial map shows the cemetery marked with the red check in the middle of a large round clump of trees.
