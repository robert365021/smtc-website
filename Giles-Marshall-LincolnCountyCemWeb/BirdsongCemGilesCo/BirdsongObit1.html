BIRDSONG CEMETERY (Tarpley Shop Road), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

SAM ABERNATHY, The Pulaski Citizen, 18 Nov 1909

Sam Abernathy, aged about 8 years died Saturday at the home
of his father, Charlie Abernathy of Bethesda. The burial occurred Sunday, at the Birdsong graveyard. He was a good,
bright boy, and had many friends, who regret his death.
