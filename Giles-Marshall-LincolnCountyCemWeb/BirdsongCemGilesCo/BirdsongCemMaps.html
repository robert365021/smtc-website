BIRDSONG CEMETERY (Tarpley Shop Road), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Birdsong Cemetery. 

Go south on Hwy 31 from Pulaski and turn west (left) on Tarpley Shop Road. Follow that for just over 3 miles and with permission turn left into a private drive. The Cemetery can be seen from this road about 300 yards on your left out in a pasture at the gated stop to the private residence. It will be under a couple of old cedar trees.

This aerial map shows the cemetery under the small clump of trees out in a pasture. You will not be able to visit this cemetery without the help of the owner, because there are three fences and three gates between the residential road and the cemetery. All or none of them may be locked.
