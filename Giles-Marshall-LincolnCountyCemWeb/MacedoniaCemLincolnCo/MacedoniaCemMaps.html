MACEDONIA CEMETERY, (Old Fayetteville Highway), HUNTSVILLE, ALABAMA
From the above map we see the Macedonia Cemetery GPS location. Actually Fayetteville is the nearest Town about 3 miles northeast from here.
Location: Take Highway 231 south out of Fayetteville and go south. Turn right on Highway 110 (Ardmore Highway and again right or south on Old Huntsville Road.
This Aerial Map shows the plot where the cemetery resides next Old Huntsville Road on the east side. It is outlined above in yellow.
