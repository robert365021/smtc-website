CRAWFORD (John) CEMETERY, Clayton Lane, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the John Crawford Cemetery as N 35.20742 X W -86.62480.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 431 and before you get to the Howell community turn left on Clayton Lane. The cemetery is on the left before the road dead ends about a mile west of Hwy 431.
