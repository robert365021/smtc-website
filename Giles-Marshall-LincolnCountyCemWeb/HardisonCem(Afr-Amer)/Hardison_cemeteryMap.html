HARDISON CEMETERY, (Hwy 431, behind the New Hebrew Church of Christ), MARSHALL COUNTY, TENNESSEE AKA Louis Nicholson Cemetery

This is the map to the New Hebron (I think AKA New Hebrew shown on the signage photos) Church. This church may not be connected to the Cemetery in any way, but if I have the church location correct can be used to find this cemetery in back of the church. Faye said they were on the Lewisburg Highway but was lost as to the exact location but this appears to be correct based on the details. The cemetery is down a road behind the church - exact distance is unclear to me since the cemetery is not marked on GSIS Mapping. Here we have provided the location of the Church and not the Cemetery as provided by Topozone. The cemetery is further west than the GPS shown above behind the church just north of Maury/Marshall County boundary on the Marshall County side.


I have an arrow pointing in back of the New Hebrew church to where I believe the cemetery is located on a small road leading behind the church some 300 yards into a small clearing seen on this map. I have highlighted the road in yellow, but do not guarantee this is the correct location. I have not been here to verify this. WA 29 Apr 2010
