BOATRIGHT CEMETERY, [Waco (old Lynnville) to Yokley Rd] GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

NORMAN, Belle F., wife of J. F. Norman, 27 Mar 1863 - 1 May 1891. 1704C/1704/1705

 Buffalo Reflex, Dallas County, MO 14 May 1891

Mrs. Belle Norman died at her home in Lynnville, Tennessee 1 May 1891. She was born in Giles Co TN 27 Mar 1863. She was the daughter of Brother & Sister in Christ J P and H J Brownlow and was the wife of Brother Joseph F. Norman. She professed faith in Christ in the fall of 1866 and joined the Missionary Baptist Church. She died last Friday May 1st 1891. She leaves four children, one who is but seven weeks old.

Sent in by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 25 Mar 2015.
