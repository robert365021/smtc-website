WATKINS(John)CEMETERY (Ardmore Ridge Road, Ardmore, Tn) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the John Watkins Family Cemetery. 

 Location: From Ardmore go north on Ardmore Ridge Road about 1 mile, pass a pond on the right and look to your right under the trees beside a home at the cemetery.
