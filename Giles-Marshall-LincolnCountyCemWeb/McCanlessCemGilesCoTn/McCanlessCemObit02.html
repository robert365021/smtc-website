McCANLESS CEMETERY, Friendship Community, Beech Hill Rd. GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
 

McCANLESS, John Wesley, 01 Mar 1852 - 09 Feb 1917, h/o America Parsons McCanless, s/o Francis M. and Susan B. Tucker McCanless
 

                Obituary: The Pulaski Citizen   15 Feb 1917

                John Wesley McCanless died Friday afternoon and was buried Saturday afternoon and was buried Saturday afternoon at the family grave yard with funeral services by Rev. Will Nelson. Mr. McCanless is survived by his wife, Mrs. America McCanless, two sons, George         McCanless of Texas, Dudley McCanless of this place and two sisters, Mrs. Mollie Eslick and Mrs. America Whitsette. The bereaved family have our heartfelt sympathy.
