BRENTS CEMETERY, Twitty Rd., MARSHALL COUNTY, TENNESSEE

From the above map we are given the Brents Cemetery GPS location of N 35.36689 X W -86.67941.
Location: From Petersburg go out Fayetville/Lewisburg Hwy aka Hwy 431 three or four miles to Bert Watt Rd and turn right (or east). Travel that for 1/3 of a mile and turn left (north) into Twitty Rd. Follow that until it bends left then begins to curve back around right. After about 3/4 mile on Twitty Road you will see a field road that is actually a gravel bed road on the right going up a gentle slope. It was full of weeds when we were there. Walk or drive on that field road being careful to stay on the left side because there are holes on the right side of the field road. You will find the cemetery about 67 yards up the field road on the right after you pass a discarded concrete culvert pipe.
