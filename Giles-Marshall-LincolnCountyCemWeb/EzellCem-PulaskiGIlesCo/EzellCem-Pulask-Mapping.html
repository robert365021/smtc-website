EZELL CEMETERY (Pulaski, Old Hwy 15 behind home), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Ezell Cemetery at Pulaski as GPS: N 35.19976 X W -87.00190. 

Location: Go east out Old Hwy 15 aka East Jefferson Street aka Beech Hill Rd, Pulaski). Cemetery on the left. Enclosed by a rock wall, back of what used to be Patterson's Used Car lot about 350 yards north of the road.
Jean Cosby says you have to go through a gate or climb a Fence to get to it. It is up the hill behind the house. I visited this cemetery by access from the pleasant and gracious neighbor on the west side of the property. According to her most visitors to this cemetery use their long curvy driveway since the cemetery is just over the fence from their property. No gate climbing necessary, just slide under the fence. You will see the rock wall.
