SAMUEL (Anna) CEMETERY, Buford Station Rd, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Samuel (Annar) Cemetery as North 35.26481, West -86.93646. Fire-tower Rd starts on Hwy 31 Alt. and winds back again to the same Hwy 31 Alt..

This is a more detailed view showing the triangular shaped Fire Tower Road area. The cemetery is marked with a yellow cross.

 Location: Go northeast of Pulaski on Hwy 31 Alt aka Lewisburg Highway, to the second intersection of Fire tower Road on the left. The cemetery is today in a cow pasture of the large triangular shaped section of land bounded on the northeast & southwest sides by Fire Tower Road and on the southeast by Hwy 31 Alt. (Lewisburg Hwy). Fire tower Road has a north entrance nearer I-65. The road starts on Hwy 31 Alt and bends back and ends on the same Hwy 31 Alt., leaving a large triangular shaped chunk of land on its inside. Specifics: Turn left into Fire tower Road from the north entrance and go 6 tenths of a mile and there will be an older home on your left. Go past that home to the driveway that goes to the house & barn. This driveway is a J shaped circular drive that goes eventually toward the house. After permissions turn left and a gate is straight ahead of you after you turn right off the circular drive. Go to the gate & walk through it keeping it closed and look straight ahead (South) 100 feet at the cemetery out in the cow pasture. You will see two stones standing now under a small Hackberry tree growing up in the middle of the grave of Dorinda Samuel, the wife of William S. Samuel.
