WILEY-COLE CEMETERY, Wiley Hollow Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Wiley-Cole Cemetery as N 35.27807 X W -86.55913.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 and turn left on Wiley Hollow Rd. Go past the old home on the left and turn right into a residential road that serves two homes. At the first home get permission to climb the steep hill behind their home to the top where the cemetery resides at the top inside a grove of trees.
