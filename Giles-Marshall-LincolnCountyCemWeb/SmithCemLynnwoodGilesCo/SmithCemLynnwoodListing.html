Wayne Austin attempted to find this cemetery on 10 Oct 2012, back again on 26 Aug 2016 to survey it from location instructions by Nathan Smith. Back the 3rd time to slay more brush and photo the missed tombstones. Added here 1st 11 Oct 2012 from various records, but mostly from the book Giles County Cemeteries, Page 491 by the Historical Society about 1986. Not presented on Findagrave.com as of now. Added photos 29 Aug 2016 for most listings. Last visit dug out the inscription of William R. Smith and also the Clements headstone & also found in the far SE corner the 2 Williamson &  Mitchell Monuments in a row. Also looked for other stones not found but none were located.
