MARS HILL (NEW) CEMETERY, (HWY 129) MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates needed to find this cemetery

From I-65, Exit 27, go down the ramp and turn west on Hwy 129 & go 1/2 mile.,  Turn into the Mars Hill Church and go up the slope and under the signage. The cemetery is all around and back of the church building.
With this image one can see the church building surrounded by the cemetery, here marked with a red check.
