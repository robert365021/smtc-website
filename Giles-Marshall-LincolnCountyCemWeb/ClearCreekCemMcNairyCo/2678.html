Clear Creek Church (Christian) Cemetery, McNairy County, Tennessee

Thomas & Mary Ann Warden Kerr inside the iron fence.

Kerr, Thomas Baker, Apr. 29, 1818 - Jan. 10, 1895. (Native of Campbell Station, Maury County, Tennessee, son of William & Kate Ross Kerr.)
Kerr, Mary Ann Warden, Feb. 10, 1820 - Dec. 9, 1897 (Native of Campbell Station, Maury County, Tennessee, daughter of James & Mary Warden also of Campbell Station.)
Some cemetery info on their children in nearby Oak Hill Cemetery.
This stone looks to be of the style used in the 1940 - 1960s and was set in place then. This type of stone was not available in the late 1800s. Granite was just beginning to be used as memorials at that time and inscriptions were very weak, because of the hardness of the stone.
