Clear Creek Church (Christian) Cemetery, McNairy County, Tennessee



From Selmer Tennessee Go south on Highway 45 and while still inside the city limits on Highway 142 turn left/east & go about 9 miles and past Stantonville, Tn. Continue another mile and turn left/northeast on Dennie Barber Road. Go about half of a mile and turn north on Clear Creek Cemetery Road and the cemetery is about 300 yards out on the right. The cemetery sprawls back to the northeast. The above outline in yellow is not the boundary of the cemetery but shows where the stones lie.
