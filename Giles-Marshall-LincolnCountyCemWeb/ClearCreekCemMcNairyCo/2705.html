Clear Creek Church (Christian) Cemetery, McNairy County, Tennessee

Dancer, James H., Dec. 17, 1859 - Apr. 10, 1931. Mason. (hus. of Amanda J. Carroll Dancer, "Members of the Church of Christ")
Dancer, Amanda J. Carroll, Jun. 14, 1862 - Feb. 12, 1936. (wife of James H. Dancer, "Members of the Church of Christ"). 
It is known there was an early Christian Church here which spawned this cemetery. It probably had some connection to the Campbell Station, Maury County Tennessee Christian Church related to the early families who removed to here. Later in the 1890s there was a name change to Church of Christ but the beliefs remained the same. This name change was related to this church and others separating themselves from their liberal northern congregations.
