ABERNATHY (Alan) CEMETERY (Old Campbellsville Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Abernathy (Alan) Family Cemetery. 

Location: Go west on Highway 64 (toward Lawrenceburg) from Pulaski and turn north on Campbellsville Rd (Hwy 166). Go several miles and turn right on Hannah Road (or at least the left turn is the Hannah Road) when you get to the Old Campbellsville Road turn left and the cemetery will be about 1/4 of a mile on your left across from a house which might have been an old church.

This aerial map shows the cemetery on the left of the road just at the slight bend in the road to the right on Old Campbellsville Road. Right now it is in the edge of a very large field.
