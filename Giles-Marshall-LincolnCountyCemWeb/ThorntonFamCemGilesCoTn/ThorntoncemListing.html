All formal markers were concrete except for the memorial to W. A. Thornton.

Cemetery beside the back parking lot on the east side of the Nursing Home which is now (14 Jan 2014) a rehabilitation center (right side, facing the nursing home) inside a board fence. Open gate is on the south side. It was well kept.

Photographed on a 14 Jan 2014 by C. Wayne Austin using a Canon SLR camera. These inscriptions were updated with any exception found or known by C. Wayne Austin.
