ODD FELLOWS CEMETERY, Ardmore Tn, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

 From the above map we are given the Odd Fellows Cemetery location and can see the GPS location is about 200 yards south (but a bit eastward)  of the Elliot Cemetery. It is marked on this map and I added a yellow arrow indicating its location.

Location - Back 200 yards southeat from the large Elliot Cemetery. Another location detail: Highway 31 S., near cloverleaf on I-65. Road map above shows the Odd fellows Cemetery with a black arrow pointing it out on this map.

This aerial view shows the subject cemetery south of the Elliot Cemetery and it is about the same size but many graves are unmarked here.
