BLACK (Alexander) CEMETERY, PULASKI, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

We can see the Allen-Carpenter Cemetery  upon the slop in the pasture in a thicket. Not visible in this distant photo is the chain link fence surrounding the cemetery. It seems in the south we put up fences and the graveyards grow up in brush. No fence and the cattle destroy the cemetery. Neither way works without constant intervention and care.

The Allen-Carpenter Cemetery  is seen in this photo from the Apple Hill Road upon the slop in the pasture in a thicket. Not quite visible in this distant photo is the chain link fence surrounding the cemetery.
