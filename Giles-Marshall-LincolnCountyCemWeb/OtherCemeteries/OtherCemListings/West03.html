GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE CEMETERIES NOT LISTED BY CEMETERY


WEST, Christopher Carson, 10 Nov 1878 - 20 Oct 1965, son of John Walter & Nancy B. (Anderson) West. deceased spouse not known,  Lived in Dist 7, Pulaski, Giles County at time of death.  Interred in Maplewood Cemetery.

Draft registration filled out in 12 Sep 1918: Christopher Carson West was born 10 Nov 1878 (note gravestone says 1884. Because of the birth dates of his siblings I am staying with 1878), was white, tall, slender, had black
hair & brown eyes, and his occupation was an Auto Repairman. His nearest relative was (father) John Walter West who lived in Pulaski, Giles County Tennessee. The document was signed by Christopher Carson West and
certified by Henry McMillian of the board of registration, 12 Sep 1918. This was a WWI draft registration. He also gave his name as Carson C. West. [C. Wayne Austin,  Added: Nov. 22, 2011]
