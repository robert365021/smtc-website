FRANKLIN CEMETERY, Fire Tower Road, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Franklin Cemetery as: N 35.262259 X W -86.94891 marked with the yellow cross.

Go out Hwy 31 Alt Northeast toward Lewisburg/I-65 to the first left turnoff  to Fire-tower Road. There is a service road back to the large Farm Operation on your left. Turn there after you determine the owner and with that permission follow that road until it bends back to the left (southwest) and park your vehicle out of the way. This will be nearly up next to the northern most long building, but stay out of the way of large trucks coming and going here. Follow the visible field road north (to your right) going up the slope into the woods. When the field road bends back left (or west) continue and the cemetery is on the right (north) side of that field road about 220 yards west of where the road first bent westward.

This aerial view shows the cemetery in relation to the large farm buildings and field roads needed. You must secure permission. You will naturally encounter stiff security restrictions here as given by government guidelines in regards to the food supply chain. I cannot guarantee the cemetery is on the same property as the farming operation.
