MACKLIN/(MACLIN) (African American) CEMETERY, (Little Dry Creek Road), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

The lone Ida Macklin grave surrounded by a two wire fence. Sources say Ida wanted to be buried near where she lived much of her life and her wishes were granted. 6276
