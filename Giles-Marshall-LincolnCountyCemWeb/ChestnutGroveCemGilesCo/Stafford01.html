CHESTNUT GROVE (Old) CEMETERY, (Anthony Hill), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

STAFFORD, Lucille C., 19 Jul 1921 - 24 Jul 2013. "Together forever." (shares stone with Noah Stafford.)

Lucille Stafford, age 93, died on Wednesday, 7-24-2013, in Louisville, Kentucky.
She was born in Goodspring, TN on July 19, 1921, her husband was the late Noah Stafford, her parents were the late Ollie and Elsie Bivins Cates, she was preceded in death by sister, Opal Lamar and brother, Huell Cates.
Visitation will be on Sunday from 3PM to 8PM at the Carr & Erwin Funeral Home.
Funeral services will be held on Monday, 7-29-2013, at 1PM at the Carr & Erwin Funeral home with burial in Chestnut Grove Cemetery.
Survivors are: son, Charles Stafford and wife Ginger of Louisville, KY, brother, Clifford Cates of Lawrenceville, GA, grandchildren, Jeana Burstein, Jene Maday and Shelly Cook, 4 great granddaughters, Emily, Courtney, Jade and Josie.
