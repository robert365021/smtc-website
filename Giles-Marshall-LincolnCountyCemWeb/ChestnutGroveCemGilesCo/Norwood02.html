CHESTNUT GROVE (Old) CEMETERY, (Anthony Hill), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
NORWOOD, Susan A. Phillips, 12 Aug 1869 - 3 Apr 1900. " wife of Charlie E. Norwood."

NORWOOD, Susan A. Phillips The Pulaski Citizen 12 Apr 1900
Goodspring---On last Wednesday, April 2, death again visited earth and claimed for its victim, Mrs. Charles Norwood. A husband and four children survive her. We extend to them our sincere sympathy and trust that God will bless and help them bear their afflictions. Interment took place in Chestnut Grove Cemetery.
