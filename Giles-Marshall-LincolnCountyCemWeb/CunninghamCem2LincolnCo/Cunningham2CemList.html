Photographed on a cloudy Thursday 14 Apr 2016. The inscriptions were updated with exceptions found or known by C. Wayne Austin. This publication culminated from various sources. Listed on page 186 of the book Lincoln County County Cemeteries by Tim & Helen Marsh, abt 1978. Added here 30 Apr 2016. We found  very little of much of the remains of this cemetery. It has been demolished by many years of neglect. Hope this site can continue to serve as the truth of who is buried where, but the evidence is vanishing from the cemeteries.
