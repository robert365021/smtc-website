LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
Richmond (VA) Times-Dispatch, Sat, June 29, 1940, p. 8
James Louis Carden Jr. died Friday at the residence of his daughter, Mrs. G. J. Prentice, Snead Road, Chesterfield County. Mr. Carden is survived by his wife, Mrs. Lula L. Carden; five daughters, Mrs. Prentice, Mrs. H. W. Cobb, Mrs. R. F. Wilson, Mrs. Edward M. Ingram and Mrs. Harold Marsh, and two sons, M. H. and N. W. Carden. The body, resting at the Joseph W. Bliley Funeral Home, will be sent to Pulaski, Tenn., Saturday for burial in that town Monday.

Richmond (VA) Times-Dispatch, Sun, June 30, 1940, p. 26
Funeral services for James Louis Carden Jr., who died Friday at the home of his daughter, Mrs. G. J. Prentice, Snead Road, Chesterfield, will be held Monday in Pulaski, Tenn. He is survived by his wife, Mrs. Lula L. Carden; five daughters, Mrs. Prentice, Mrs. H. W. Cobb, Mrs. R. F. Wilson, Mrs. Edward M. Ingram and Mrs. Harold Marsh, and two sons, M. H. and N. W. Carden
