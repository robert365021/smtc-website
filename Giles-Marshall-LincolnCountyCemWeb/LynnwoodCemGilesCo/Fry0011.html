LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

The Fry Memorial, one of the largest in the cemetery.

Effie Laura Fry inscription. The only inscription I found on this massive monument. Her husbands stone lies beside hers on our right. It is much smaller. He died a couple of years after her. 

FRY, Samuel J. (Jesse), 28 Nov 1872 - 4 Sep 1945. (Son of James Polk and Sallie Jane Yokley Fry.) Ref: Jebean54@aol.com section E
FRY, Effie Laura, 29 Dec 1875 - 26 Sep 1943. (Dau. of James Polk and Sallie Jane Yokley Fry.) Ref: Sharon (Jebean54@aol.com) section E
