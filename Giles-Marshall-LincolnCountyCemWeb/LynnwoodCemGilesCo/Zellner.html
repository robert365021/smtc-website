LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

From the Cookeville Herald Citizen

Glenn Zellner Jr.

Cookeville - Graveside services and burial for Glenn Zellner Jr, 88, of Cookeville, will be held Wednesday, September 29, at 3 p.m. at Lynnwood Cemetery in Lynnville.

Mr. Zellner died Monday, September 27, 1993, in Cookeville General Hospital.

He was a native of Grundy County, Tennessee , born July 11, 1905, to the late Glenn Sr. and Cordie Fry Zellner.

A retired farmer and office worker, he was a member of the Church of Christ.

His family includes his wife, Esther Buck Zellner of Cookeville; three sons, Glenn Zellner III of Monterey, William M. Zellner of Jackson County, and Thomas L. Zellner of Cookeville; and four grandchildren, Glenn IV, Nora Marie, Billy Christopher, and Joshua Grant Zellner.

Sons, grandsons and nephews will serve as pallbearers.
The family received friends after 7 p.m. of that following evening at Whitson Funeral Home. Bro. Willie Anderson officiated at the services.
