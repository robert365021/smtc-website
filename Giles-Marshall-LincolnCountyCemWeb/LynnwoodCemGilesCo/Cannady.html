LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

CANNADY, Valda R. (Reydell) , 11 Oct 1943 - 6 Nov. 2015. (d/o Frank & Hassie Cannady.) section C


CANNADY, Valda R.

Valda Reydell Cannady, 72, homemaker and longtime resident of Tanyard Hollow Road, died Friday, November 6, 2015 at NHC Columbia.
Funeral services will be conducted Tuesday at 11:00 A.M. at Oakes & Nichols Funeral Home with Glen Alexander officiating. Burial will follow in Lynnwood Cemetery. The family will visit with friends Monday from 4:00 – 7:00 P.M. at the funeral home. Condolences may be extended online at www.oakesandnichols.com.
The Maury County native was the daughter of the late Robert Frank Cannady and Hassie Pearl Weatherford Cannady. She was a member of Campbell Station Church of Christ.
Survivors include nieces and nephews, Nancy Williams Hooks, Linda Williams Morton, Kathy Williams Price, Brenda Cannady McKee, Cindy Cannady Moore, Sharon Cannady Walls, Eyvonne Hickman Morris, Lisa Cannady Fischer, Jeff Williams, Don Cannady, Tim Cannady, Al Williams, Danny Hickman, Joey Cannady, and Robert Cannady.
She was preceded in death by sisters, Ruth Williams and Evelyn Hickman; and brothers, Joe Cannady, Frank Shirley Cannady, Barney Cannady, and Dennis Cannady; and nephews, Rickey Williams and Wilbur Hickman.
Active pallbearers will be her nephews. Honorary pallbearers will include Roger Smith, Johnnie "Peaches" Nowlin, and members of Campbell Station Church of Christ.
Information sent in by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, 10 Nov 2015
