LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

AUSTIN, Clyde Elbert, 8 Aug 1901 - 5 Apr 1979. (native of Culleoka, Maury County; 1st born and the son of Charles H. & Otie E. Kerr Austin.)
AUSTIN, Eula Anna Story, 8 Sep 1904 - 7 Sep 1993. (wife of Clyde E. Austin; Maury Co. native; she was orphaned early in life and lived with her brother Carl Story in the home of Ella Burkett & her husband James Tyler.)
The Clyde Austins owned a farm on the south side of Waco to Yokley Road. He worked for TVA in his earlier years and purchased the farm to become a farm family for the remainder of their lives. They had one son, Carl Milton Austin who moved to Florida and lived his life there. 
Photos 1994 & information by Wayne Austin, nephew of Clyde 15 Apr 2009.
