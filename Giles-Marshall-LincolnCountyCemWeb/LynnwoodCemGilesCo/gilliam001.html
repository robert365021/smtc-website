LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE


Ms. Betty Jean Gilliam, age 76 of Cornersville, died Tuesday, December 14, 2010 in Maury Regional Hospital.
Ms. Gilliam was a native of Mt. Vernon, Illinois and daughter of the late John Payne and Hattie Border Harshbarger. She was a homemaker and member of the Cornersville First Baptist Church.
She is survived by one daughter, Linaya (Barry) Fralix of Cornersville, son, Hugh Kelley Gilliam of Lewisburg, two sisters, Louise Sampson and Elsie Tucker both of Mt. Vernon, Illinois, five granddaughters, and nieces and nephews, also, survive.


Photos sent in by Mary Bob McClain Richardson, 15 May 2015.
