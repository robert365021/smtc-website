ADAMS, James F. (Franklin) (23 Sep) 1919 - (Aug) 1970). (h/o Wilma D. Adams) section A 1868

ADAMS, James F, Obituary
The Pulaski Citizen 2 September 1970
Funeral services for Mr Adams were held Tuesday at 3 pm at Carr and Erwin Funeral Home in Pulaski, with burial in Lynnwood Cemetery in Lynnville, TN.
He was the son of Mrs. Halsie Adams and the late Ed Adams.
In addition to his mother, he is survived by his wife, Mrs. Wilma D Adams of Cornersville, TN.; Three sons, Stanley, Mike and Jack Adams all of Cornersville; two brothers, Tom Adams of Pulaski and Edward Adams of Campbellsville; six sisters, Mrs Sally Poarch of Marshall County, Mrs Lee Frank Darmell and Mrs Albert Williams of Nashville, TN, Mrs Allen Frances and Mrs Florence Kilburn of Pulaski, TN and Mrs Wayne Glendering of Deca, Maine.
Information submitted by Mary Bob McClain Richardson 27 Jan 2015.
