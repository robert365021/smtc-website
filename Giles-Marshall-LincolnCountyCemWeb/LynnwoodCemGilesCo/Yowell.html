LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 YOWELL, Mary Virginia, 10 Nov 1925 - 16 Mar 2013. "Thy life was beauty, truth, goodness and love." section E

Carr & Erwin Funeral Home Pulaski, TN obituary:

Mary Virginia Yowell, age 87 of Lynnville passed away Saturday March 16, 2013.
She was born November 10, 1925 to the late Ora B. and Kathleen Clementine Foster Yowell. She is also preceded in death by a brother, Forrest B. Yowell.
Survivors include her brother William (Bill) Yowell of Lynnville, TN and several nieces and nephews. There will be a private graveside service Monday March 18, 2013 at Lynnwood Cemetery.
The Family requests memorial donations be made to Berea United Methodist Church, 2488 Haywood Creek Road, Pulaski, TN 38478.
