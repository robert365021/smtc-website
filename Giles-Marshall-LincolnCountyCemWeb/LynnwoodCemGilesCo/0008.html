LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

CHAFFIN, Maxwell, "Mack", (31 Jan) 1895 - (19 Nov) 1948. (husband of Ethel Nell Coffee; son of J. Thomas & Dora Trice Chaffin.)
CHAFFIN, Nell, (15 Feb) 1895 - (1 Nov) 1981. (Ethel Nell Coffee Chaffin Barlar; Daughter of William Fine & Samantha Virginia Wiley Coffee; 1st married to Mack Chaffin, 1912 (the mother of Wilson Chaffin), and 2nd, but later in life (1956) to Thomas J. Barlar.; lived her whole life in Blue Branch in Giles Co., around Stiversville and Covey Hollow Rd. in Maury County. Her 2nd marriage was to the widowed husband of her deceased sister. Aunt/Mamma Nell had a very lovely personality.)
Mack & Nell have common ancestors who were Coleman & Susan Weatherford Chaffin.
