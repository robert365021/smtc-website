LYNNWOOD CEMETERY, Lynnville, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
 

BIRDSONG, John Wesley "Wes", 7 Jan 1868 - 11 Feb 1939. (h/o Rila H. Birdsong.  section B 2708

Funeral rites for John Wesley Birdsong, 71, farmer of the Blue Creek section, who died Saturday, were held Sunday at Lynnwood Cemetery, with the Rev. G. M. Knox officiating. Surviving are his wife, one daughter, Mrs. E. E. Boatright; one son, Alexander Birdsong; and one brother, Ben Birdsong, all of Giles County. Bennett-May & Co. in charge.
(Published by The Record February 15, 1939 Markers place by James Glenn & Rita Birdsong on 2 Jun 2003. Ref: Rita Birdsong e-mail to Mary Bob)
