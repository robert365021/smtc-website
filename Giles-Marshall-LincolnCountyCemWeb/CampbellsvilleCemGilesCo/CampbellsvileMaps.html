CAMPBELLSVILLE CEMETERY (Campbellsville) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map plots GPS Coordinates of the Campbellsville Cemetery.

The Campbell Cemetery is shown highlighted above inside the village of Campbellsville Tennessee. Location from Pulaski: Take highway 64 West for 1/2 mile; right (north) onto Campbellsville Road and go about 10 miles. Cemetery on right as road bends left going into Campbellsville.

(1) Campbell (H.C.) Cemetery (2) Campbellsville Cemetery (3) Hannah-Yokley Cemetery (4) Poplar Grove/Campbellsville Cumberland Presbyterian Church (5) Old Clarence Campbell Home. (6) Campbellsville Church of Christ. This is not a comprehensive listing of all historical assets of Campbellsville as there are numerous antic homes and other churches.
