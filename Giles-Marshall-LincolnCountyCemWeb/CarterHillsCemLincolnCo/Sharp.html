CARTER HILLS CEMETERY, (Coldwater), LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE

SHARP, Amy Leann, 14 Oct 1989 - 24 Sep 2009.

Obituary of Amy Leann Sharp

Funeral services for Mrs. Amy Leann Sharp, age 19, of New Market, AL, who passed away Thursday, September 24, 2009 in Hazel Green, AL, conducted in the chapel of the Gallant-Riverview Funeral Home. Burial in Cedar Hills Memorial Gardens.

Miss Sharp was a native of Lincoln Co., TN, the daughter of Angela Ann Alexander Durrance, who survives, and the late Michael Steve Sharp.

She was a homemaker.

Survivors include her daughter: Jasamine Sharp of New Market, AL; significant other: Danny Lee Tucker of Hazel Green, AL; mother: Angela A. Durrance of New Market, AL; brothers: Bobby Lynn Sharp of Toney, AL, Michael Allen Sharp of New Market, AL; maternal grandparents: Morris Edward & Janice Alexander of Hazel Green, AL and niece and nephew.

Killed in an automobile accident.
