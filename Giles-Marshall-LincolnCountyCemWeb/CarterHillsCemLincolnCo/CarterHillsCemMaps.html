CARTER HILLS CEMETERY, (Coldwater), LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Carter Hills Cemetery.

Go west on Hwy 64 from Fayetteville and turn south on Hwy 273 and then back more south on Hwy 274 (Old Rail Road Bed Road). Go to the community of Coldwater and go about 1 miles south of there and turn left into the Carter Cemetery Road. Do not go here unless you have upstanding business.
