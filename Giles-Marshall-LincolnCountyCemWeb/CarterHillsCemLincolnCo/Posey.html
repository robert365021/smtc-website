CARTER HILLS CEMETERY, (Coldwater), LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE

POSEY, David Allen, 22 Jan 1983 - 1 Jan 2011

Funeral services for Mr. David Allen Posey, age 27, of Fayetteville conducted at Higgins Funeral Home. Burial in Carter Hills Cemetery. Mr. Posey passed away Saturday afternoon at his residence.

A son of Robert L. and Brenda Louise Posey who survives, he was a native of Lincoln County, employed as a construction worker in Alabama, and of the Baptist faith.

Survivors, in addition to his parents, are his daughters Alaina Marie and Aubrey Nicole Posey, brothers Robert Posey, Jr. and Tyson Dewayne Posey, fiance Tanya Marie Mearse, grandmother Odelle Poteete, aunts and uncles Bobby and Faye Poteete, Joe and Robin Poteete, Linda and Steve Smith and Ronnie and Rachel Spears, and special cousins Britta Spears, Jessica Kinsley, Bobby Poteete, Robbie Joe Poteete, CG Sweeney, BJ Casey, and Sonya Smith.

He was preceded in death by his grandparents Elaine and Frank Posey and Joe Poteete and cousin Wade Allen Casey.
