EDEN CEMETERY, Cashion Rd. MOORE COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Eden Cemetery GPS location as N 35.29939, W -86.38207.
Location: From Highway 55 go north of Lynchburg bear left on Old Lynchburg Road. Take that for 1/4 mile and turn left on Cashion Rd and go to Crest Drive on your right. Look left at the hill above you with trees on the top and that is where the cemetery resides near the front edge of the trees. You will need to climb the sharply steep hill to get there. Have fun.
