CHILDRESS CEMETERY, Boonshill, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Childress Cemetery as N 35.20160 X W -86.72074.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 to Barnes Hollow Road and turn south (left). Immediately turn left again on an old stretch of the Hwy 64 and go to the Old Childress farm (.3 miles and turn right into the home lane) for permission to find the cemetery as shown in the GPS map above. This cemetery is gated and in the midst of a cattle operation and requires the help of the farm operators here.
