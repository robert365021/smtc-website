BAXTER CEMETERY, Cashion Rd., Robertson Hollow. MOORE COUNTY TENNESSEE

From the above map we see the Baxter Cemetery GPS location as N 35.29954, W -86.39128.

 Location: From Highway 55 go north of Lynchburg bear left on Old Lynchburg Road. Take that for 1/4 mile and turn left on Cashion Rd and 1 and 1/10 of a mile to a clump of trees in the corner of a long residential driveway. It is next to the road in the northwest corner of that driveway.

This Aerial Map shows a yellow check to mark the cemetery.
