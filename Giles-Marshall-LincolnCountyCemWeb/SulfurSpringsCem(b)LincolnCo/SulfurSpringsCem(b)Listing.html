This appears to be a people of African American / American Indian / Caucasian heritage. Someone in years past has vandalized the personal photos that at one time graced many of the tombstones of the folks that were proud of their heritage. Most are now fragmented beyond repair. It is impossible for flying rocks to always hit in the center of the picture of the head proving to me this is vandalism. This looks to me like a kid with a pellet rifle. I noticed several other photos have been removed.

This Cemetery presentation is based on the photography of C. Wayne Austin dated 8 Sep 2014. Added here 20 May 2016. Not listed in the book Cemeteries of Lincoln County Tennessee. This is not a complete photo survey as several of the stones were missed.
