SULFUR SPRINGS CEMETERY, Sulfur Springs Road, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

From the above map we see the GPS Coordinates of the Sulfur Springs Cemetery as: N 35.21834 W -86.66631.

Location: From Fayetteville take Hwy 64 aand go west toward Pulaski and turn right on Bradford Road. That road changes to Sulfur Springs Road at the junction of Jaysland Road and Sulfur Springs Road. Go another 3 tenths of a mile and turn right (north) into the road back up to the cemetery. The road comes into the cemetery in the southwest corner.

With this Aerial Map one can see the grave layout.
