REYNOLDS CEMETERY (Hwy 11 R. into Scenic Dr.) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the white check marking the GPS of this Cemetery as N 35.16066 X W -87.09276

Location from the 64 southern Bypass take Minor Hill Hwy (#11) south and go about 3 miles to the Scenic Drive Rd. and turn right. Go a few hundred yards on that road with a grass field on your right until you reach the first thicket or grove of trees spanning away northward from you. Walk (or drive if you want the risk) along that grove of trees on the east side about 2/3 the way to the north end and look left into the thicket for the large Walnut tree that marks this cemetery among many Hackberry trees. Under the Walnut Tree you will find the marked stones. This cemetery is not maintained but the vegetation is somewhat shaded out by trees.
