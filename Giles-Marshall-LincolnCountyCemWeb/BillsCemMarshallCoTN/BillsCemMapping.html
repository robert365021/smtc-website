BILLS CEMETERY, Cheese Road, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

From the above map we are given the Bills Cemetery GPS location as N. 35. 34717 X W -86.66295 Located three miles north west of Petersburg, one-half mile west of Liberty Valley Road.

Location: Go Hwy 431 out of Petersburg a couple of miles to Cheese Road and turn right. Go about one mile and the cemetery is on the right out into a field.

This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly as a square area in an open field. Cheese Road is north of this plot.
