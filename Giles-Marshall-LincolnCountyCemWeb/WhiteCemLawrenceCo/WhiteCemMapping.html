WHITE (or SHANNON) CEMETERY, Box Road, SE, LAWRENCE COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the White Cemetery. This is south east of Lawrenceburg. The GPS as calculated by C. Wayne Austin: N 35.06956 W -87.31129

Location: From Leoma, take the Rabbit Trail Rd. (Hwy. 98) approx. 8 1/2 miles to Day/Mockerson Road on the left. Go approx. 1 1/2 mile to a low water bridge. Go across the bridge and turn left into the driveway. Ask permission and directions to the cemetery. However, facing the house, the cemetery is to the right at the wood line approx. 100 yards behind the old barn near the Shannon Branch Creek.
