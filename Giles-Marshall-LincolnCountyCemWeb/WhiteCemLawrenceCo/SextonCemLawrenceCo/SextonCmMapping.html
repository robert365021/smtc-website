SEXTON CEMETERY, Box Road, SE, LAWRENCE COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Sexton Cemetery. This is south east of Lawrenceburg. The GPS as calculated by C. Wayne Austin: N 35.06063 W -87.29220.

Location: Go Highway 98 (Rabbit Trail Road) to Five Points then turn east (left) and go to Box Road, turn left, go past a dairy barn, at the top of the hill turn left up a lane. The cemetery, is surrounded by a chain link fence, and is on the left side of lane at the bottom of the hill before you get to the home of Jim and Helen Williams, which was once the Sexton/McMasters farm. Directions by Sue Davis.
