GILLIAM CEMETERY, N. OF Minnow Branch Rd), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Gilliam Cemetery is in the center of the map where the red check was placed. I have plotted the GPS to the best of my ability.

The cemetery is located here where I plotted the location with in the center of this road map.
The book Giles County Tennessee Cemeteries says the directions for finding this cemetery are: Go west from Pulaski on Hwy 64 and turn (right) north on Hwy 166 and go past Minnow Branch Road and the cemetery is on the right in a clump of trees. Further they say this is a large cemetery with many fieldstone marked graves among 4 listed marked headstones.
Thanks to Kristi Henson for pointing out this hard to find cemetery and Mr. Childress's patience for allowing me to park in his driveway. I accidentally went up the wrong road. The place is owned by Mr. Edward Henson and permission is necessary to visit.
