ROCKY POINT CEMETERY, (African American, Chicken Creek Rd east side) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the Rocky Point cemetery location GPS coordinates commonly known as the Latitude and Longitude.
Location: Highway 11, left onto Chicken Creek, about 1 mile south on left back. Turn left into a field road. The Cemetery is behind a gate.

This aerial view shows the cemetery in a large rectangle cleared area. There is a field road to this place.
