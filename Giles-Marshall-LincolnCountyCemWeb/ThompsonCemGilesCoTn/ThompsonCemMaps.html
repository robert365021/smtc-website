THOMPSON CEMETERY, (west of Minor Hill 1 Mile) - GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Thompson Cemetery at N 35.06608 X W -87.16978. 

This road map shows the cemetery on the northwest side of Minor Hill on Barber Shop Road. Go south on Hwy 11 to Minor Hill and up to the top of the hill and  turn right on Puncheon Branch Rd and then back right (north) on Jackson Hollow Rd. After less than a mile turn back left on Barber Shop Rd aka Fox Hollow Rd and nearly immediately turn left to cross the concrete bridge over the creek. Drive the 50 yards up to the cemetery slipping and sliding in the wrong weather. HA!. There was a steep drop down to the concrete bridge and that will probably get deeper with time so watch out on that. Please go here with good intentions and honor in your heart. Otherwise there are nearby neighbors watching over this cemetery.
