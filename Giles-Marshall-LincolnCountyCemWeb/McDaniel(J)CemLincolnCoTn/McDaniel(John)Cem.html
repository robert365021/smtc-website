Oct 24, 2014
From: David Birdsong - Cemetery Caretaker.

Hello Mr Austin,
My name is David Birdsong and I live in Nashville, TN. I have just learned of this site and done some looking and found this cemetery listed. I have been maintaining this cemetery for close to 10 years now. My mother was Jean March Birdsong and was born just down the road about 1/2 mile and raised in Delina. I never knew this cemetery existed until Mom asked me to go down there and clear it off. It was a real mess. I believe that it was around 2007 when I covered it with the gravel so that it would be easier to keep cleared of the weeds. It is a labor of love for me. Mom has since passed now and she and dad are buried in the nearby Medium Cemetery.
My contact info is dbirdsong28@gmail.com and my cell is 615-519-4482 if anyone wishes to contact me. David
