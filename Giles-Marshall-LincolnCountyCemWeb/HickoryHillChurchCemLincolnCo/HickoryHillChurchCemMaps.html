HICKORY HILL CHURCH CEMETERY, (Mimosa to Gray Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Hickory Hill Church Cemetery as N 35.35449 X W -86.52895.

Location: From Fayetteville go norht on Hwy 231 and climb the Chestnut Ridge. Before you go back down the other side of Chestnut Ridge turn left on Foster Hollow Road and the cemetery is right there on the left behind the church.
