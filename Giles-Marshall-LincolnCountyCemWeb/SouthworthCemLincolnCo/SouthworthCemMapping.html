SOUTHWORTH CEMETERY, Corders Cross Rd, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Southworth Cemetery as N 35.04795 X W -86.48159.

Location: From Fayetteville go east on Hwy 64 to Kelso. Turn (right) south on Teal Hollow Road and follow that thru and past Kelso. That turns into Corders Cross Roads eventually. After you pass Raby Rd on right (going west) look left at the Walker Ford Rd. junction. Turn left (east) there and the cemetery is immediately on your right under the trees.
