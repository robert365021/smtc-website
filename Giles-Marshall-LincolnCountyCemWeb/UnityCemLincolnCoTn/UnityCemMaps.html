UNITY CEMETERY, (Howell), LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Unity Cemetery. N 35.24451 W 86.60280

Location: From Fayetteville go northwest on Hwy 431  a few miles to Howell and bear right on Old Petersburg Pike. Go abt 3/4 mile and turn right into L. Smith Rd and the cemetery will be on your left after a few hundred yards. Go here only if you have honorable plans to pay homage to the deceased.  Anything else will get you hung high to the nearest snag by the watchful eye of the locals and/or the law.
