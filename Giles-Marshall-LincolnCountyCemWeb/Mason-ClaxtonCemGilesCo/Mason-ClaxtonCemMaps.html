MASON-CLAXTON CEMETERY, Hwy 31 Alt, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Mason-Claxton Cemetery as GPS N 35.251599, W -86.95654. 

Location: Go northeast on Hwy 31 Alt and pass Woodlawn Road on the left. About a 1/2 mile beyond that on the left look for the Driveway that serves three homes and loops around. Cemetery is on the right side of that residential road behind a Modular home in a treed lot.

This aerial map shows the cemetery on the north side of the Lewisburg Hwy (Hwy 31 Alt/Hwy 11) in a treed lot.
