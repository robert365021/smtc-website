The photos for this cemetery are incomplete. The west side of the road was about 90% surveyed and the east side about 35% surveyed. Photos 2553 - 2577 were made on the east side of the road and all other photo #s were made on the west side.

 

Photographed on a sunny Tuesday just after noon on 30 Jun 2015. The inscriptions were updated with exceptions found or known by C. Wayne Austin. This publication culminated from various sources. Listed on page 186-189 of the book Bedford County County Cemeteries by Tim & Helen Marsh, abt 1978. The book separated the east side of the road from the west side of the road and also showed the black section separately. I have combined all of that but left the identify best I could based on the photography number.  Added here 20 Jul 2015.
