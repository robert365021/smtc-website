BUCHANAN-PIGG CEMETERY, Haysland Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Buchanan-Pigg Cemetery as N 35.25190 X W -86.67529. Not listed on the Google Maps.

Location: From Fayetteville go northwest on Hwy 431 to Brown Teal Road in Howell. Turn left and follow that until it terminates into Haysland Rd and turn right (north). Follow that to the Blake Community where the Friendship Church of Christ is located. Turn in left (west) there and go up to the house on the hill for permission and directions to go behind the house northwest about another 200 yards to the cemetery. These folks take wonderful care of the cemetery.
