CAMPBELL CEMETERY (Booneville Rd.) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Campbell Cemetery.
Location: From Fayetteville go east on Highway 64 for a mile and turn north on the Lynchburg Highway (Hwy 50) . Follow that for about 3 & 1/2 miles north and turn north on Booneville Road. Go to just before the junction of Booneville Road and Gimlet Road and look left at a few trees about 140 yards out in the field. The cemetery is under the trees, but there will be no gate from there to visit this spot.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly marked with a red check out in the field across a well built fence under a small clump of trees. There is no access from the nearest spot in the road because no gate is present.
