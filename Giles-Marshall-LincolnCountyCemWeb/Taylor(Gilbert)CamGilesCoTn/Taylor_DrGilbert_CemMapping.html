DR. GILBERT TAYLOR FAMILY CEMETERY, Hwy 31N, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Dr Gilbert Taylor Cemetery as N 35.24597 X W -87.04744.

Location: Hwy 31N, on left about 1/4 mile past Industrial Loop Road, on fence line at the old Craig home (1986 instructions).
This cemetery is near the old Craig Home, but not on the same property. The home is the 1st house on left after passing Guthrie Rd. and is an older white home with one tall chimney.
But the cemetery is actually on the property just south of the house. Before you get to the driveway there is a "curb cut" to the open land just south of the house. That is what can be used to get to the cemetery. Drive as far up the hill (going west) as you can to the tree line and just walk inside the trees along the fence row (on your right) toward the top of the hill until you see the cemetery a little down the hill to the left of the fence line.
If you ask the fellow that owns the Old Craig home it is easier to get there by going up the farm road that heads up the hill off of his driveway. That will allow you to drive closer. by Daniel Vaughn as edited by C. Wayne Austin 4 Jul 2016
