NEAL CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
Location: East on East Jefferson Street in Pulaski continuing on Beech Hill Road if the road changes to that name. Look for the high rock wall on the right as you travel out of town about a mile and one half. Cemetery is just before you get to Mark St. on the right.
Mapping the location

NEAL, James H., died 21 Jul 1825 aged 21 years 7 months and 12 days.

Only one stone was found here in this very large and imposing rock wall enclosure, but there could have been other memorials in past times. the rock enclosure is big enough for 25  to 50 graves. The weeds and brush were so thick that I could not see well. However a nearby neighbor informed me that there were no other memorial stones inside the rock wall enclosure.

Added here 25 Sep 2009 by Wayne Austin. Visited, photographed and copied by Wayne Austin 1 Jul 2008. Not listed in the book Giles County Tennessee Cemeteries.
