KIMBROUGH CEMETERY (Weakley Creek Rd.), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Kimbrough Cemetery. 

This road map shows the cemetery on the north side of Weakley Creek Road about 2/3 of a mile before reaching the Lawrence Co line. It is under a Cedar tree and behind a house about 100 feet. 

This aerial map shows the cemetery accessible from Dry Weakley Creek Rd. Turn into the drive of a house with permission and go behind the house to the cemetery inside a chain link fence.
