ESTES CEMETERY, Sumac Road, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Pulaski Citizen 6 Aug 1896 �Sumac�
One of our oldest people in this part of the county has died very recently. Miss Clarissa Estes, aged about seventy eight years died several days since and was buried at the family burial ground. She was a consistent member of the Methodist Church and a good woman.
