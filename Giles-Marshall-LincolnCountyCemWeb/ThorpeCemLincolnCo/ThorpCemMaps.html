THORPE CEMETERY,  (McBurg to Dellrose Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Thorpe Cemetery as N 35.17080 X W -86.81184.

Location: From Fayetteville go west about 12 miles to the McBurg Community and turn south (left) on the McBurg to Dellrose Road. Follow that for about a mile and turn into a driveway on the left into a residential road with permission. The cemetery is in front of a modern ranch home about 200 yards on the left inside a wooden fence without a gate as shown in the above map.

Here we can see the outline of the wooden fence.
