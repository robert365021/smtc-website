CRAWFORD CEMETERY (March Mill Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

NANCY R. CRAWFORD was born Nancy Gibson, daughter of Patriot John Gibson (1760-1844) and Margaret Joyce (1770-1847). She was the second wife of William Crawford II. Together they had four children: Margaret Ann Elizabeth Crawford Tigert, George Gibson Crawford, Joseph Warren Crawford, and Mary Etta Frances Crawford. Nancy was born May 23, 1814 in Cane Creek (Howell), Lincoln County Tennessee, and died August 6, 1868,  Lincoln County Tennessee. She lived in Cane Creek aka Howell, Lincoln Co., Tennessee all her life and is buried near where she lived.
