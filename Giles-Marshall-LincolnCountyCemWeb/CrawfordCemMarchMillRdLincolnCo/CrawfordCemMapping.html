CRAWFORD CEMETERY (March Mill Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Crawford Cemetery as N 35.22080 X W -86.63237.

 Location: From Fayetteville go northeast on Hwy 431 several miles to Howell and turn left on Shields Rd and when that forks turn left on Brown Teal Rd. Follow that for about a mile and and turn left (south) on March Mill Rd. After a few hundred yards the cemetery is on (your) right of the road in the thicket between two houses. Go in here with heavy boots on in the summer to avoid snakes and chiggers. The cemetery is right on the road so I don't know who owns the surrounding land since a right of way is not necessary.
