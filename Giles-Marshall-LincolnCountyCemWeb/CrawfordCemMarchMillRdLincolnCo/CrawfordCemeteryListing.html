The nearby Howell village at one time was called Cane Creek. You will see that on the old census records.

Really had difficulty photographing this cemetery the first time. It was wet and a rain storm was brewing in the distance with an accompanying lightning storm. Eventually the lens got wet leaving blurry spots on the photo worsening the problem. Many of the photos came out dark and blurred. Digital cameras don't like dark wet environments. Also the hurrying to beat the rain storm probably caused some camera shake which caused most of the blurring. Oh well learn from experience as I probably will not get a repeat experience there. Photographed again on 25 Oct 2014 to eliminate blurred photos.
