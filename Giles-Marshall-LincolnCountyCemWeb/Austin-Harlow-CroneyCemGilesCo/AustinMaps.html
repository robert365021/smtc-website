AUSTIN/HARLOW/CRONEY CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Croney aka Austin/Harlow/Croney Cemetery. The nearest major town is really Ardmore Tennessee about 4 miles as a crow flies southeast. 

This road map shows the route needed from I-65 or Pulaski to get to this cemetery. 
Location: Highway 31 South to Elkton. Right on Prospect Road to Witt Road. Left on Witt Rd. Left on River Bend Rd.. 
More detailed: From Prospect Road turn south on Witt Rd and go to end and turn left on River Bend Road. Follow that until it turns back right 90 degrees. The cemetery will be north west of there in the edge of the field inside a chain link fence. Cemetery on what was the old Harlow Place. This area was at one time Old Elkton as the first settlement of Elkton in the bend of the Elk River for this rich river bottom land.

This aerial map shows the sharp bend of the River Bend road. The cemetery is located northwest of there 200 yards. We have an arrow pointing to the cemetery in the edge of the field.
