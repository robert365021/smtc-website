HELTON CEMETERY, (Campbellsville), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This photo shows the Cemetery sign you will see from Rose Hill Road. Follow that field road and go thru the gate closing it to keep the cattle secure.

This is the sign at the gate into the Helton Cemetery. The wooden sign is failing so I restored the image graphically. However, there is a street sign just to the left of this that says: "HELTON CEMETERY". The memorials are back and to the right of this image. The photographer was pointing the camera at an odd angle to dodge the rays of the sun which can destroy the photo visibility.
 Photos dated 29 Dec 2009, Information by Wayne Austin 11 Feb 2010.
