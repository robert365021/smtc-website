NEW BEAR CREEK CHURCH CEMETERY, BEAR CREEK, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE, near Mooresville.

Bear Creek Church & Cemetery, Map from MS Terra, modified for use here.

From Culleoka: Take the Lewisburg Rd. (Hwy 373) and go one mile beyond the county line. Turn south and go one and one half mile to the historic Bear Creek Church on the left. The cemetery is in front of church near the road on your left. 
From Interstate 65: From I-65 south go Exit 32 (Hwy 373 Culleoka to Lewisburg Tn.) west toward Culleoka or Columbia Tn. Go thru the sleepy historic village of Mooresville and turn left or south on East Bear Creek Road. Go past Whitsell/Demastus Rd. Cemetery as about 1.5 miles south of where you turned off Highway 373 on your left.
To find this cemetery from Culleoka take Lewisburg highway and go one mile beyond the county line. Turn south on Bear Creek Rd and go one and one half mile to the historic Bear Creek Church. The cemetery is in front of the church near the road on your left.

 Information by Wayne Austin, 26 Feb 2008.
