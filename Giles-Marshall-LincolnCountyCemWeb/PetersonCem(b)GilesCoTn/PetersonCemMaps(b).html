PETERSON CEMETERY,(African American), Johnson Rd., GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Peterson Cemetery. N35.22872 X W 87.02968.

 Location: Go north on North 1st St and take Bledsoe Road to the right. Climb up to the top of the hill. When the road forks take the Johnson Rd left another 1/2 mile. Cemetery in clearing on bank of road on right.

This aerial map shows the cemetery marked with red check on the right side of the road.
