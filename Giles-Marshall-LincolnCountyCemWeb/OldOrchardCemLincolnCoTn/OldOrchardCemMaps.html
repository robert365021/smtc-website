OLD ORCHARD CEMETERY, Hwy 129 Petersburg to Chestnut Ridge Rd), LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Old Orchard Cemetery as N 35.31687 X W -86.62713.

Location: From Fayetteville go northwest on Hwy 431 and follow the signs into Petersburg turning right on Hwy 129 (Petersburg to Chestnut Ridge Hwy) After you go about a 1/2 mile out of Petersburg look for the Orchard Cemetery Road and turn left and you will see the cemetery.

This view of the Cemetery is a Google close up for your help in finding the stones you are interested in.
