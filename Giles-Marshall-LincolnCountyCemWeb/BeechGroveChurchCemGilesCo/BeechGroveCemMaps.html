BEECH GROVE CUMBERLAND PRESBYTERIAN CHURCH CEMETERY (Dog Branch Road), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Beech Grove Cemetery GPS location.
Location: Take Highway 11 out of Pulaski and turn left at Anthony Hill onto Oak Grove Road. Turn left at fork in road which is Oak Grove & Dog Branch Roads. Travel down Dog Branch Road about 1 2/3 miles & you will come to a flat area on the left which goes steeply up a slope after the flat area. The flat area is the site of an old road into the CP Church site. The Cemetery is upon that slope above about 50 feet higher than the road above the flat area & deep into the forest.
This Aerial Map shows the forest and the slope where the Beech Grove Cemetery is located above the road. Be prepared to climb a steep rocky area to reach this cemetery.
