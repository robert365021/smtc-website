BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
GOSNELL, Martha Ellen Estep, 13 Feb 1925 - 4 Dec 2007, Columbia, Maury Co., Tn. d/o Charlie Harrison Estep (1890 - 1941) & Lillie Pearl Swinney Estep (1894 - 1965); w/o Kelly Douglas Gosnell (1916 - 2002)
GOSNELL, Kelly Douglass, 12 Jul 1916 - 28 Feb 2002, h/o Martha Ellen Estep Gosnell, Pfc US Army

OBITUARY - "Marshall County Tribune", published Dec. 7, 2007 -- Martha Ellen Estep Gosnell, 82, died Dec. 4, 2007 at Maury Regional Hospital. Preceded in death by parents, Charles Harrison Estep & Lillie Pearl Swinney; husband Kelly Douglas Gosnell; 1 son; 2 sisters; and 4 brothers. Survived by 5 sisters; 2 brothers; a sister in law; 1 granddaughter; 1 grandson; several nieces & nephews. Services & burial were at Brick Church Cemetery.
