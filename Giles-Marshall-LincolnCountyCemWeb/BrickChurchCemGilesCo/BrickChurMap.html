BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

From Nashville take I-65 South to Exit 22. Go left or southwest on Highway 31 Alt toward Pulaski for about 1 mile. Pass the Brick Church Community sign and find the road that veers left off Highway 31 Alt called Clock Creek Road. Take that and just down the road on your left is the church and cemetery. That is also the road to the historic Lairdland Farm Bed & Breakfast about 1/4 miles further, a good place to stay.
