BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

BARLAR, Lee G. (Gusta), 1856 - (3 Feb) 1932. (School teacher in the area of Brick Church at one time. Son of William Henry & Susan Frances Phillips Barlar.)
BARLAR, Johnnie Lee Rutledge, (22 Dec) 1864 - (24 Aug) 1955, wife of Lee Gusta Barlar, dau. of John & Mary Wood Rutledge. Lee & Johnnie married 20 Dec 1883. They had no children.
