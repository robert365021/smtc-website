WORSHAM, Katholean Wheeler (5/11/1913 - 1/15/2006)

Mrs. Katholean Wheeler Chapman Worsham, 92, of the Sumac community, died Jan. 15, 2006, at her residence. A member of the Providence Church of Christ, Mrs. Worsham was preceded in death by her parents, Homer and Mattie Gowan Wheeler; her husbands, Clifford Chapman and W. E. Worsham; son, Glenn Chapman; brother, Gorden Wheeler; and sisters, Evelyn Worsham and June Haws. Funeral services were held Jan. 18 in the chapel of Giles County Funeral Home with Robert Hatfield and Mack Wheeler officiating. Burial was in the Brick Church Cemetery. She is survived by her daughter, Faye Bennett of Pulaski, sister, Floree Arnold of Russellville, Ala.; grandchildren, Carol Resnick, Marsha Dunavant, Lisa Alden, Debbie Holley, Bobby Holley, and Susan Fitzgibbon and nine great grandchildren.
Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home 15 Jan 2006
