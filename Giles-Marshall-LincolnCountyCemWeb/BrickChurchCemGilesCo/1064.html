BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

The stone of Jane L. McMahon.  

McMAHON, Jane L., wife of J. F. McMahon 1 Jun 1812 - 4 Jan 1875. Mother. "Our mother rest in quiet Sleep while friends in sorrow oer thee weep" (broken stone).
This memorial was set back up temporarily for photography. It is broken across the center and would not stand long term without the correct epoxy glue. Never place a broken memorial back on the ground where the weight of the stone sits across the inscription as it will wear it away.
