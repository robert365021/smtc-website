BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

SMITH, Nannie, 1875 - 1924. (I think this is a black lady.) 
That is a hunch based on where she is buried and the where the places are located for the other black ladies interred in similarly marked locations on the back or side of the church. This is the back side of the churchyard whereas Ms Whitfield and Ms Gordon were interred in the right side of the building where few marked graves are located.
