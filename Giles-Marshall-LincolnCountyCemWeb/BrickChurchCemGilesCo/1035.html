BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Martha A. West Memorial.

WEST, Martha A., wife of James W. West, 10 Jan 1824 - 19 Aug 1854. James W. West is believed to be the son of Walter West. James is also believed to be the brother of Samuel A. West b. 1809. Died in Giles County buried on Blue Branch Road on a high hill above the road.
