BRICK CHURCH CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Fallen and crumbling James Mabe memoial.

MABE, James, 7 Dec 1814 - 29 Sep 1849. A strong chance the birth date above is 1844 instead of 1814. This stone is crumbling and this may be the last photo that is made of enough of it to read. The lower section is broken into many fractures which are falling away. Inscription IN -- MEMORY OF JAMES MABE -- born Dec 7th 1814 (or possibly 1844). -- Died Sep 29th 1849.
