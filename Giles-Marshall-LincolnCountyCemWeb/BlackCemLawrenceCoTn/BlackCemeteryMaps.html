BLACK CEMETERY, Weakley Creek Rd., LAWRENCE COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Black Cemetery. 

 Location: Go North on Hwy 43 to Weakley Creek Rd and turn right (east) and go about 2 miles and when the road begins to bend northward look in the bend of the road on the right next to the road at the cemetery.

This aerial map shows the cemetery as outlined by the yellow border.
