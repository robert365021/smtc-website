SHORES BAPTIST CHURCH CEMETERY, (Fall River Rd to Shores Mill Rd) - GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Shores Baptist Church Cemetery as N 35.13501 X W -87.15398. 

From Pulaski on the bypass (64) take Hwy 11 south to Anthony Hill and turn right on Fall River Road. Go 2 & 1/2 to 3 miles and turn left on Shores Mill Road. Then go 1 mile and the cemetery is on the right behind the Shores Baptist Church parking lot. Unless you have respect, admiration and love for the folks who lie here do not go to this place. The local watchman will have your number if you have malice or devious aims in your heart.

This aerial map shows the cemetery well maintained behind the church parking lot. You can see a part of the parking lot in the lower part of this map image. This is a new cemetery, no more than 20 to 40 years old. There were only 20 burials in 1985.
