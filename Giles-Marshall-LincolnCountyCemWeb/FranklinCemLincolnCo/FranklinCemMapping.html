FRANKLIN CEMETERY,  Asa Smith Road,  LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Franklin Cemetery.
Location: From Fayetteville go southwest on Hay 110 Ardmore Hwy several miles passing Skinem, Taft and Blanche then when you come into the village of Cash Point turn left or south on Asa Smith Rd. and go about a mile and the cemetery is on your right under the trees beside the road.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly in a grove of trees next to Asa Smith Road.
