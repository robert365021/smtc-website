HEREFORD CEMETERY-LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
The center of the map marks the location of the Hereford Cemetery. This cemetery is detailed on the USGS records. I have no reason to believe the GPS coordinates are not correct as shown above.


 To get to this cemetery from Interstate 65. Take Exit 6 Bryson Road (Hwy 273) and go east. In Bryson take Bunker Hill Road north (left) to Bee Springs Road and turn back (right) east again. Go past Bee Springs Church and Cemetery and continue northeast. About 3/4 mile past Bee Springs Cemetery you will cross into Lincoln County continuing on Bee Springs Road. Continue something less than a mile and the cemetery is on your left with the new Story Cemetery on the right as shown in the aerial view below.
