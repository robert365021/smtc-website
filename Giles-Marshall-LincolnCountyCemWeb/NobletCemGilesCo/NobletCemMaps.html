NOBLIT CEMETERY, Sugar Creek Road, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Noblit Cemetery as GPS N 35.07726, W -87.21138. 
Location: Take Hwy 11 from Pulaski going southwest past Minor Hill go a few more miles and turn right into Sugar Creek Road before crossing over Sugar Creek Bridge. After about one mile on Sugar Creek Rd turn left up the slope into the cemetery road and under the Noblit Cemetery sign.

This aerial map shows a closer view of the cemetery with the cemetery among the trees, but no trees are inside the cemetery.
