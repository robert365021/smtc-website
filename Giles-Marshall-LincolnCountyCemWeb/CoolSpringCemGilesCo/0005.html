COOL SPRING CEMETERY, (Minor Hill Hwy on west side of Hwy) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
Mrs. Mary Anna Campbell formerly of Pulaski, passed away in Mayfield, Ky. on February 14, 2009.

She was a member of the Northside Church of Christ in Ky., a homemaker and 83 years old.
Visitation was in Pulaski, Wednesday, February 18th from 12:00 noon until 2:00pm at Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home.
Funeral Service will follow after visitation Wednesday at 2:00pm in the Chapel of Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home with William Greenleaf officiating.

Burial followed in Coolspring Cemetery.
Memorials are suggested for, New Pathways for Children Churches of Christ Disaster Relief Effort at P.O. Box 10, Melber, Ky. 42069 or 410 Allied Drive, Nashville, Tn 37211.
She was preceded in death by her, Husband, Robert Neal Campbell Parents, Wilson and Georgia Davenport Cole Granddaughter, Amanda Tuggle Brothers, George Cole, and Clarence Reed Cole.

Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home in charge of arrangements for Mrs. Mary Anna Campbell.
