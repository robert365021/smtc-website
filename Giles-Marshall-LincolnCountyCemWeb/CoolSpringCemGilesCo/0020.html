COOL SPRING CEMETERY, (Minor Hill Hwy on west side of Hwy) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Pauline Vincent, age 93, died on Saturday February 16, 2013 at Meadowbrook Nursing Home in Pulaski.

She was born in Limestone County Alabama on June 24, 1920. She was a retired production worker at Le Mar Shirt company.

She was preceded in death by her parents Christopher Columbus and Mary Elizabeth Rogers Musgrove, husbands Henry Thompson, and Dewey Vincent, 1 son Billy Wayne Thompson, 3 brothers Clyde, Charles, and Logan Musgrove, 1 sister Lila May Patterson.

Visitation will be on Monday November 18, 2013 from 5pm to 8pm at the Carr And Erwin Funeral Home. Funeral service will be held Tuesday 11-19-2013 at 2:30pm at the Carr And Erwin Chapel, with burial in Cool Spring cemetery.

Survivors are: daughter Maudie Newton of Frankewing, TN, brother Orvil Musgrove of Columbia, TN, sister Mildred Patterson of Illinois daughter-in-law Polly Thompson of Athens, Al, 10 grandchildren, 22 great grandchildren, and several nieces and nephews
