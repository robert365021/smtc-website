COOL SPRING CEMETERY, (Minor Hill Hwy on west side of Hwy) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Obituary: LOCKE, Ones Elmore Giles Free Press 22 Dec 2005
Mr. Ones Elmore Locke, 78, of Pulaski died Dec. 19, 2005, at St. Thomas Hospital in Nashville. He was the son of the late Daniel and Eva Ward Locke, was retired from Murray Ohio and was a member of Cool Springs Church of Christ. Funeral services were to have been held today (Thursday) at Carr and Erwin Funeral Home. Burial was to have been in Cool Springs Cemetery. Memorial donations may be made to Cool Springs Church of Christ. Survivors include his wife, Mary Morse Miller Locke of Pulaski; stepson Tim Miller of Pulaski; stepdaughter Vivian Coleman of Minor Hill; two sisters, Pauline Fox of Goodspring and Lois Locke of Nashville; four grandchildren and one great-grandchild.
