ALLSUP CEMETERY, Chicken Creek Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Allsup Cemetery as N 35.20118 X W -86.81751.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 for about 17 miles and pass the first north (right) turn off into the Chicken Creek Road. Pass on to the McBurg community and turn north on the 2nd Chicken Creek Rd. Chicken Creek Rd is a loop road. Go on that about 1 mile and with permission the cemetery is on the See property. You will need permission to visit this cemetery. It is on private land next to private assets.
