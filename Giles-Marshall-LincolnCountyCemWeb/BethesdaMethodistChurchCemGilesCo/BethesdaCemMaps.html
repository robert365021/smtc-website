BETHESDA METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY (Hardy Road) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Bethesda Methodist Church Cemetery. 

Go south from Pulaski on Hwy 31 and pass the Ephesus Church of Christ. Immediately past that turn left or east on Hardy Road. Go a couple of miles almost to the Tarpley Shop aka Bunker Hill aka Old Stage Coach Road and look right to find the cemetery. The church building is on the left.

This aerial map shows the cemetery well maintained on the right across from the church building. This should be a public cemetery, but you may be scrutinized by the neighbors who rightly and regularly watch over the cemetery.
