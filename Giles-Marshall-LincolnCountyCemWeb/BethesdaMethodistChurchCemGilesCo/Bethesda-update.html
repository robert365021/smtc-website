BETHESDA METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY (Hardy Road) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
Sarah Elizabeth Nipp, 1844 - 1917.

 NIPP, Sarah Elizabeth, 28 Apr 1844 - 24 Feb 1917, Dearest Mother thou hast left us and thy loss we deeply feel. (Daughter of William Calvin Hollis & Martha (Patty) Deloach; Wife of James Andrew Nipp

 Mrs. J. A. Nipp died Friday at her home in the Bethesda locality and was buried Saturday at the Bethesda graveyard after services by Rev. J. L. Kellum at the church.
Mrs. Nipp had been a consistent member of the Methodist Church for a number of years. She is survived by her husband and three children.
Published in The Pulaski Citizen, 1917 - bio information shared by R. S. Birdsong as sent by Mary Bob Richardson 9 Jun 2012)
