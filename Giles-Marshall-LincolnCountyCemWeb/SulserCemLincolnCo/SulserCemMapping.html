SULSER CEMETERY, Henry Thompson Rd., Taft, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Sulser Cemetery as N 35.03192 X W 86.71862.

Location: From Taft Tennessee go north on Old Railroad Bed Road 1/4 mile and turn right (east) on Henry Thompson Road. Follow that thru the first bend in the road left and then back right. After the right bend (now going northeast) go about 300 yards, but before crossing over the creek stop at the last house on the left and ask permission to use his driveway to park and walk the 1/4 mile back to the cemetery in the distant tree line as mapped above. In other words follow the tree line on your right along on your right side for about 1/4 mile and the cemetery is in the tree line, but bulges wider than the average hedgerow as you will notice.
