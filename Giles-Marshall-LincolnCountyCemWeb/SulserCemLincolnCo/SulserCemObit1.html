SULSER CEMETERY, Henry Thompson Rd., Taft, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

Daughter of Henry Bryant, Age 8 years.

The Fayetteville Observer

January 24, 1907

Lizzie B., daughter of Mr. Henry Bryant, died of heart trouble at Dellrose on Saturday, January 19, 1907, aged 8 years. The funeral was conducted by Rev. W. J. Malone; burial at Sulser grave yard.
