TILLERY CEMETERY, Industrial Loop Rd Pulaski, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Tillery Cemetery along the south side of Industrial Loop Road as N 35.23577 X W -87.04663. Location: Hwy 31N, at Industrial Park Road, cemetery on bluff above spring.  This cemetery is not mapped.

Location: From Pulaski go north on Hwy 31 to Industrial Loop Road on your left. Turn left there and after a few hundred yards there will appear a bluff & stream below that on your left. The cemetery is upon that bluff in back next to the open field. When you come to a house on the left there will be a short drive leading up a dead end before the next driveway to the house. Park in that 1st drive and climb the slope on your left. When you encounter a fence follow it (left) north a short distance toward the road until you can go around the corner of the fence without crossing it. Careful it is steep at that place. After that follow the fence on your right along the edge of the woodland staying in the woodland but traveling eastward. Be careful not to fall over the bluff on your left. After a hundred yards or so you will begin to see the stones as marked on the aerial view above. The cemetery is inside the woodland, but close to the open field to the south. The woodland there juts out into the field at the cemetery.
