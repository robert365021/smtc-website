SCOTTS HILL CEMETERY (Scotts Hill Tn), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Scotts Hill Cemetery. The nearest major town is Pulaski, Tennessee about 6+ miles northeast, as a crow flies. 

Location: Go west of Hwy 64 out of Pulaski and turn south or left on Agnew Rd. Follow that for about 3 miles and turn right on Myrick Branch Rd. Go about 2 miles on that and it will terminate into Johnson Branch Rd. Turn left there and follow that for a mile to the cemetery & church on your left.

This aerial map shows the cemetery adjacent to the Scotts Hill Baptist Church at the junction of Johnson Branch and Lee Chapmen Roads.
