ELKRIDGE CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE, GILES COUNTY TN.

WELLS, Annie L., 27 August 1900 - 6 October 1900

WELLS, Mrs. Annie, 25 September 1879 - 6 September 1900 "In remembrance of Mrs. Annie Wells, born September 25, 1879, died Sep 6, 1900. Tis hard to break the tender cord when love has bound the heart. Tis hard to speak the words we must forever part dearest loved one. We must lay thee in the peaceful grave's embrace but thy memory will be cherished till we see thy heavenly face." (spelling & grammar  altered some to today's English)
