ELKRIDGE CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE, GILES COUNTY TN.

The 5th & fourth memorials from the left of the drawing.


The memorial for Elizabeth Parker Crockett, wife of Archibald.


CROCKETT, Archibald, 10 January 1839 age 64 yrs, 11 mos, 16 days, (son of Robert & Rachel Kerr Crockett buried about 30 feet north from here.)
CROCKETT, Elizabeth Parker, d. 10 December 1857 in her 79th year (wife of Archibald Crockett).
