This is the signage for the cemetery. It lies to the south of highway 129. The black section lies to the north of this photo upon the bank.

This view looks east out over the cemetery. We are looking at the newest area in the cemetery with the old historic area lying further out to the east beyond the ability of this photo to capture all the memorials. Hwy 129 is on our left. Roberson Fork Creek lies on our right hear the edge of the woods. It was the source of water for the old settlers interred here at one time supplying vital water for the livestock, cooking & drinking needs.
