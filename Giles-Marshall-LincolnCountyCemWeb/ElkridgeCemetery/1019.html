ELKRIDGE CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE, GILES COUNTY TN.


WILLIAMSON, Lottie E., 13 Oct. 1881 - 1 January 1901, married F. D. Wells 16 Jan. 1898.Transcription: "Lottie E. Williamson, Born Oct 13 1881 married to F.D. Wells Jan 16, 1898, died Jan 1, 1901, Darling Lottie she has left us, yes left us forever more, but we hope to meet our loved one on that bright and happy shore."
