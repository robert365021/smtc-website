WHITE-LAWRENCE CEMETERY (Hagon or Shoals Bluff Rd)  GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
The center of the map shows the location of the White-Lawrence Cemetery shown as the White Cemetery on the above mapping. This cemetery is detailed in the USGS records. I have provided this map to show the GPS coordinates. The GPS Latitude & Longitude are correct as shown above.


 This map shows the White-Lawrence Cemetery located on the Hagan Road, but that is not labeled on this map. 
Directions: Take Hwy 166 south from the center of Pulaski about 11 miles and go to Hagan Road and turn right (west). Go abt 2 1/4 miles to the second right turn. Turn into that and follow along for 1 mile to the Shoals Bluff Church of Christ on your left. Go past there for another 300 yards and the cemetery is on the right side of the road. 
This Aerial view shows the roads and landscape you will encounter as you approach the cemetery. I am unaware if the names of the roads shown on this map are correct. Lots of errors in these maps now days, but the detail is plentiful enough to find this location irrespective of incorrect road names.
