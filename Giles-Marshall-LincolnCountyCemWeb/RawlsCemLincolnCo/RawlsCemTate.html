RAWLS CEMETERY, Taft # 5, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

Memorial service for Michael Gene Tate, age 53 of Fayetteville, will be conducted Friday at
1 p.m. at Higgins Funeral Home with Bro. Tim Shields officiating. Burial will follow in the Tate Family Cemetery. Mr. Tate passed away Monday at his residence.
He was a native of Dade County, FL and was the son of the late Patricia Mosher Tate and of Fred (Sharron) Tate who survives . He was a sales representative at Howard Bentley and a member of the Brown Teal Road Baptist Church.
Survivors in addition to his father include four brothers Rick (Diane) Tate, John (Kathy) Tate, Heath (Cecilia) Tate and Bill (Tonya) Tate all of Fayetteville and several aunts, uncles, nieces and nephews. He was preceded in death by a brother Sam Tate.
