HARWELL (Hartwell) CEMETERY,  Solomon RD., GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Hartwell Sr. Harwell Cemetery as GPS: N 35.14335 X W -87.06970. 

Go south on Hwy 31 from Pulaski on Mill St./Hwy 166. Continue south passing the Airfield then the Rock Crusher on the right. Just after the rock Crusher (on the right) turn right on Donahue Rd and take the first right (north) on what is mapped as Story Lane or Fry Lane on the above map. After a few hundred yards turn back left (west) on Solomon Road. Follow that about 500 yards and bare left. You are now on a private driveway. It then turns left 90 degrees continuing on the residential road leading up the hill to a private home. Follow that residential road as it bends to the left then back right. After the right bend notice a grassy field road under the woodland on the right that will take you the last 400 yards up to the cemetery. Not certain if the home at the end can grant you permission. These stones may not be visible in the summertime because of high weeds, but at least visible in the winter time when the vegetation is low.
