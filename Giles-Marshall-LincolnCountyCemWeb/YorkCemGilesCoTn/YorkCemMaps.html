YORK CEMETERY, (Minor Hill Hwy to York Hollow Road) - GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the York Cemetery as N 35.14704 X -87.09289.

Location: From the Bypass take Hwy 11 less than 5 miles and turn left on to York Hollow Road. Go about 1/4 mile and the stone can be seen on the bank of the road on the left of a driveway.
