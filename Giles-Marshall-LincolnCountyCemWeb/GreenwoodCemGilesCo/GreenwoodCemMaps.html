GREENWOOD CEMETERY (Weakley Creek Road) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
The center of the map marks the location of the Greenwood Cemetery. This cemetery is detailed on the USGS records and I have no reason to believe the GPS coordinates are not correct as shown above.


 This map shows the location of the Greenwood Cemetery which can be found by going west on Highway 64 from Pulaski several miles and turning north on Weakley Creek Road. Follow that for about 3 miles and the cemetery and Greenwood Church of Christ will be on your right upon the side of a hill.
This (Google) Aerial view shows the cemetery named in white text with the church facility a few yards southeast of the cemetery.
