OLD BEAR CREEK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.

This is the Dr. G. A. Brown memorial. It is fallen or been knocked over by livestock. I was able to turn it over. Cleaning it was a different story.

BROWN, Dr. G. A., died 2 Feb 1913. Age about 81 yrs. 
Inscription: "Dr. G. A. BROWN -- DIED FEB. 2 -- ABOUT 81 -- YEARS -- DIED 1913." 
Photos, commentary and transcription by Wayne Austin, 26 Feb 2008.
