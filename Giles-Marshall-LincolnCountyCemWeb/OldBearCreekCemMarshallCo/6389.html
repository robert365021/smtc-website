OLD BEAR CREEK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.

Our focus is on the last tall stone in the upper center of the photo just before the large cedar tree trunk. Since the Keneda stone is misplaced then the other 4 stones between us and the cedar trunk is for James W. Lowrance and his three wives. That is going toward the tree; Lucy; Evie; James W.; & Julie.

Julie C. Lowrance lower right of photo.


LOWRANCE, Julia C., 26 Jun 1849 - 21 Feb 1885. 'Wife of Jas W. Lowrance.".
