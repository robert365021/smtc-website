OLD BEAR CREEK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.

Our focus is on the stone second south from the Cedar tree trunk.

LOWRANCE, Sarah, died 31 Aug 1848. aged 45 Years & 3 Months. "Consort of J. N. Lowrance." Inscription is not plain. Looks like "Consort of J.sN Lowrance." That may be abbr. or a corruption of Joseph N. Lowrance.
