OLD BEAR CREEK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.

Old Bear Creek Cemetery, on the left of the road up the steep hill.

To find this cemetery from Culleoka take Lewisburg highway and go one mile beyond the county line. Turn south on Bear Creek Rd. and go one and one half mile to the historic Bear Creek Church. The cemetery is on your right upon the hill in a grove of trees across the road from the church. Standing in front of the church building on the road go 400 yards further on the road and there will be a gate that goes into a field road up the hill. Go up that hill in a northwesterly direction to the cemetery about 300 yards from the road.
