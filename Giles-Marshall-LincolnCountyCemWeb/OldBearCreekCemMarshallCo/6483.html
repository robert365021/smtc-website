OLD BEAR CREEK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.

The blue gray stone just to our left of the tree trunk. The footstone is in front of us shown as the smaller of the two stones in front & all under the tree, Andrew Jackson Moore.

Andrew Jackson Moore memorial.

MOORE, Andrew Jackson, 4 Apr 1842 - 16 Dec 1859. 17-10-12. Inscription: "SACRED -- To the memory of -- ANDREW JACKSON MOORE -- who was born April the 4th in the year of -- our Lord 1841 Departed -- this life February the -- 16th 1859 aged 17 years -- 10 Months and 12 days."
