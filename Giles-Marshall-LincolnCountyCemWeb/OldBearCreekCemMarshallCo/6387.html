OLD BEAR CREEK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.

Our focus is on the stone leaning on the square stone two memorials back from the large cedar trunk.


KENEDA, Elizabeth, 25 Nov 1832 - 13 May 1853. "Consort of J. S. Keneda; dau. J. N. & Sarh Lowrance." (The spelling is probably corrupted and the surname should be Kennedy.)
Another broken and crumbling batman ears style memorial with a death date between 1851 and 1858. This stone is sitting on the base for J.W. Lowrance. This one probably belongs in the row just east of here.
