OLD BEAR CREEK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.

An unknown stack of carved stones with no inscription on our left and the fallen stone of Mary King Orr on our right, and next past the Mary King Orr is the box tomb of Wm Orr. Emily J. Orr memorial here.

ORR, Emily J., 5 Sep 1838 - 16 Sep 1839.
