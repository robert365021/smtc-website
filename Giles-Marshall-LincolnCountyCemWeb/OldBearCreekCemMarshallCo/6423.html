OLD BEAR CREEK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.

Many of the Samuel K Orr family memorials are tumbled, thanks to livestock or vandals. I stood the stone on the left up (Rev Robert J. Orr, probably a son) to photograph it and left it up. It has a solid wide base and should stand up.

Rev. Robert J. Orr.

ORR, Rev. Robert J., 25 Dec 1847 - 15 Jan 1894. Mason.
Photo, commentary and transcription by Wayne Austin, 26 Feb 2008.
