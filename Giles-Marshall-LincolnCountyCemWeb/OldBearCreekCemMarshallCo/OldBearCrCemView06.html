View looking west up the hill at Lowrance and other family memorials.


From this view we see the iron rails of the broken gate that once separated the livestock from the cemetery. I believe sometime in the early 1980s a group came up here and put the Gate & Chain link fence in place. Over time with trees falling on it and livestock trampling across it the fence no longer stands in places. 

Standing in the middle of the cemetery in an open area we see thru the weeds some of the memorials on our left. The stones do not stand out very well since they and the surrounding brush are brown to gray in color.
