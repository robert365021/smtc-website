OLD BEAR CREEK CEMETERY, MAURY COUNTY 1807-1836, MARSHALL COUNTY Aft. 1836.

Our focus is on the big tall dark gray stone in the center right and back. It belongs to James P. & Elizabeth J. Orr. 

ORR, James P., 4 Apr 1817 - 13 May 1895. Father. Mason.
ORR, Elizabeth J., 9 Jun 1822 - 19 Sep 1898. "Wife of James P. Orr." Mother
