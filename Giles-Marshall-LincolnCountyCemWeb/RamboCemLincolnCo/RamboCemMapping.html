RAMBO CEMETERY, Old Hwy 64, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Rambo Cemetery as N 35.18559 X W -86.81842.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 several miles to the McBurg Community and turn left on the Old Hwy 64. Go past the McBurg Methodist Church 500 yards and the graves are on the right up the slope behind a home between two out buildings or sheds as seen in the above map.
