SAMUEL (Anthony) CEMETERY, Buford Station Rd, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Samuel (Anthony) Cemetery as North 35.31625, West -86.95507.

This is a more detailed view zooming in a little further than the above map.

 The stone slab can be seen marked with a yellow cross in the yard east of the home.

Anthony is interred in the Anthony Samuel Graveyard on the Buford Station Road. Directions are:
Go out Highway 31 North to Buford station Road. Turn right (east) there and go about 4 miles passing the Lane Cemetery (unseen under the trees) and the Blue Creek Road on the left. Go another 1/2 mile and look up the slope to a white home that was until 1970 The John Yokley Farm & home. It still has his name on the mailbox today at 3709 Buford Station Road. I do not know if the mailbox belongs to a son named John or they left it from the original John's ownership.
