WALKER-HARDEN CEMETERY, (Fayetteville to Lynchburg Hwy), LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Walker-Harden Cemetery GPS location as N 35.19188, W -86.51564.
Location: From the junction of Highway 50/55 & Hwy 64 go north about 2 miles and look to your left for C.A. Boaz road. Turn left there and double back almost opposite the direction you were going on Hwy 50. That directin is about due west. Follow that to the next to the last house and with permission turn right (north) into that residence lane and the cemetery will be found on their property. They are gracious host and will likely accompany you to the cemetery if their time permits. Horses are found in this premises so you are at your own risk, but I found them very friendly.
This Aerial Map shows a yellow check marking the cemetery inside a tree lined area. Stones are all down from trees falling on them and maybe livestock.
