One area which is about 1/3 of the cemetery the northwest part was covered in Poison Ivy. I tried my best not to touch it as it highly poisonous to some folks. I did everything Icould to secure the photos and still avoid the stuff and succeeded in avoiding the Ivy and yet I missed a few gravestones I ordinarily would have seen & photographed, ones which had fallen and requires scrounging around to find. Because of this I also skipped the King Cemetery across the way from this one, but will go back sometime in the winter time for that one. Since cattle roam freely there then if that is true then the poison Ivy is likely trampled down. See Findagrave.com for more photos here on this cemetery.
