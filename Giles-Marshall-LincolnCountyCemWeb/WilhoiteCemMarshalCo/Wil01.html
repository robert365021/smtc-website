WILHOITE CEMETERY, CHAPEL HILL, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

Wilhoite Cemetery as photographed in 1992 with a Canon Film camera.
Photo by Kim Shockey, 2005

ROBINSON, Anna Adeline Warner Wilhoite, 6 Jan 1814 - 16 Mar 1876, daughter of John & Eunice Warner wife of 1st William Wilhoite, 2nd Mr. James Robinson. She helped her son John Benton Wilhoite found the Wilhoite Mills, a thriving grain and lumber milling operation in 1845.
