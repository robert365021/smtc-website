WILHOITE CEMETERY, CHAPEL HILL, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
The Cemetery is located on the premises of Henry Horton State Park across Highway 31A from the Golf Course Parking lot. many recreational amenities can be seen and enjoyed in this park.

This location is south of Chapel Hill Tennessee a couple of miles on Hwy 31A.
