LUCY-COLVETT-ROPER CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

From Nashville take I-65 South to Exit 22. Go left or southwest on Highway 31 Alt to Pulaski. Take the main highway 64 west from Pulaski for a couple of miles and turn (right) north on Highway 166. Go thru Campbellsville and continue on 166 to Factory Creek Road. Turn left (or west) and go 2/3 of a mile and turn north on Anderson Creek Road. The road to the cemetery on the right is just after one turns into Anderson creek Rd.
