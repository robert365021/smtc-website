YARBROUGH CEMETERY, (Weakley Creek Rd., near county line) LAWRENCE COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Yarbrough Cemetery. This is about half-way between Pulaski & Lawrenceburg Tennessee just inside the county Lawrence Line.

This road map shows the route needed from Pulaski to get to this cemetery. 
Location: Highway 64 west to Weakley Creek road. North or left there and follow Weakley Creek Road as it winds its way up to and finally into Lawrence County. Then after another 1/3 of a mile turn north (right) into the private drive. Back about 100 yards prior to reaching the house turn northeast across the open field directly toward and up the slope to the far northeast corner of that open field. Park there and the cemetery is across the fence into the woods with gravestones clustered by family over about a 1/4 acre tract.

This aerial map shows the cemetery inside the edge of the woods on the side of the hill. Using this map about the "6" of CR-936 turn north into the private drive and ask the precious elderly lady there if it is OK, then back about 100 yards prior to the house turn northeast across the field directly toward and up the slope to the northeast corner of that open field. The cemetery is across the fence into the woods occupying about 1/4 of an acre of land. Strangely there is a north/south fence that divides the cemetery in half. Do not go onto this private property if your aims are less than honorable as the neighbors will surely interrupt your aims.
