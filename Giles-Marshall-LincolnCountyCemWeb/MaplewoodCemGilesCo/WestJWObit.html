MAPLEHILL CEMETERY, Pulaski, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
 

WEST, John W. The Pulaski Citizen 15 Oct 1924
John W. West, aged a few weeks past 90 years died Sunday at his home in the eastern suburbs of Pulaski of the infirmities of age, after an honorable and useful career. His burial was at Maplewood Cemetery with the rites of the Masonic fraternity with which he had long been identified. He also was a member of the Cumberland Presbyterian Church.
Mr. West was at one time a Justice of the Peace here. He was a fine mechanic and worked on until a few years ago when advancing age forced him to give up the activities of life.
His wife died a number of years ago. Surviving him are his sons, Sam and Carson West, and a daughter, Mrs. A. M. Weatherford.
