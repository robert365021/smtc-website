BALLENTINE, Paisley Nhyira Danielle, 04 Jun 2016 - 23 Jun 2016, d/o Whitney Ballentine & Deron Garrett, NM-761-Babyland
BALLENTINE, Paisley Nhyira Danielle Bennett-May-Giles County FH 23 Jun 2016

Miss Paisley Nhyira Danielle Ballentine, the infant daughter of Whitney Ballentine and Deron Garrett, passed away peacefully Thursday morning, June 23, 2016, at St. Thomas Midtown in Nashville. Paisley was born on June 4, 2016 and departed this life surrounded by her loving family. A graveside service will be 10:00 Tuesday, June 28, 2016, at Maplewood Cemetery. She is survived by mother Whitney Ballentine of Pulaski, father Deron Garrett of Pulaski; 3 brothers and 2 sisters; loving grandparents, Ricky and Melody Ballentine of Pulaski. Ralph and Tonya Garrett of Pulaski, Pam Williamson of Pulaski; great-grandmothers, Mary Young and Mattie Garrett both of Pulaski. Many other family, friends, and loved ones also survive. Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home is in charge of arrangements for Paisley Nhyira Ballentine.
