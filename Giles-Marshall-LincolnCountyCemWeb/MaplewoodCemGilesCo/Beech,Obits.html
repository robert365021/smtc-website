MAPLEWOOD CEMETERY, Pulaski, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

BEECH, Joe Hardy Pulaski Citizen 28 Mar 2006

Joe Hardy Beech, 86, died of Kingston Springs died March 23, 2006, at the Alvin C. York Veterans Hospital in Murfreesboro. He was a Giles County native and retired from the Tennessee Air National Guard. He was preceded in death by his parents, Branch and Russie Cardin Beech; three brothers, Cornelius Beech, John Baugh Beech and William Beech; and sister, Katherine Beech. Funeral services were held March 26 in the chapel of Carr and Erwin Funeral Home. Burial was in Maplewood Cemetery. Survivors include his wife, Helen Stevenson Beech of Kingston Springs, Tenn.; three sons, John Beech of Dickson Tennessee, Steve Beech of Kingston Springs, and Jim Beech of Burns, Tenn.; daughter, Debbie Beech of Montana; sister, Ruth Gordon of Pulaski.; six grandchildren and six great grandchildren also survive.

BEECH, Kathryn Pulaski Citizen 12 Apr 2005

Mrs. Kathryn Beech, 83, of Pulaski died April 10, 2005, at Sun Bridge Health-Care and Rehab of Ardmore. Funeral services were to have been held today (Tuesday) at Carr and Erwin Funeral Home. Burial was to have been in Maplewood Cemetery. Survivors include son Johnny Neal of Pulaski; daughter Renee Vasallo of Nevada; brother Joe Beech of Nashville; sister Ruth Gordon of Pulaski; six grandchildren and six great-grandchildren.

She had been married before but divorced. I know she had a son by John David Neal.
