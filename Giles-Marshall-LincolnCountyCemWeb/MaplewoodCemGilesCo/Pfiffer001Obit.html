MAPLEHILL CEMETERY, Pulaski, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

PFEIFFER, Mary Louise [Kendrick], 31 Jul 1923 - 16 Jun 2016, w/o Daniel Richard Pfeiffer, d/o Charles and Mary Howley Kendrick Serenity-70C-2
PFEIFFER, Mary Louise Kendrick Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home 16 Jun 2016

Mary Louise Kendrick Pfeiffer of Dellrose passed away on Thursday, June 16, 2016 at NHC Healthcare in Pulaski. She was born in Los Angeles, California on July 31, 1923 and was 92 years old. She was a loving wife, mother, grandmother and sister, a homemaker, member of DAR, collected antiques for 40 years and was interested in genealogy. Visitation will be on Monday, June 20th from 10:00am Until 11:00am at Imaculate Conception Catholic Church. A mass will be held following visitation at the Imaculate Conception Catholic Church with Rev. Father George Panthananickal, CMI Pastor officiating followed by a graveside service at Maplewood Cemetery. Memorial donations are suggested to Alzheimer’s Association, 201 W. Lincoln Street, Tullahoma, Tn 37388 in memory of Daniel Richard Pfeiffer, Sr.She was preceded in death by her husband, Daniel Richard Pfeiffer, Sr. son, Robert “Bob” Pfeiffer, daughter, Mary Catherine Pfeiffer, parents, Charles and Mary Howley Kendrick, and a brother, Jack Kendrick. She is survived by her sons, Daniel (Laurel) Pfeiffer, Jr. of Elkmont, AL, Charles “Bill” (Kristen) Pfeiffer of Dellrose; grandchildren, Matthew Pfeiffer, Jake Pfeiffer, Conrad Pfeiffer and Gately Pfeiffer; 5 great-grandchildren. Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home in Charge of arrangements for Mary Louise Kendrick Pfeiffer.
