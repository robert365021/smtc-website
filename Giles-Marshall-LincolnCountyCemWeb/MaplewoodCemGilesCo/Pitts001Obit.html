MAPLEHILL CEMETERY, Pulaski, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

PITTS, Louise [Crawford], 03 Aug 1921 - 02 Jun 2016,w/o Rufus Amuel Pitts, d/o Sidney Johnson & Grace Jeans Crawford, NM-1134-5
PITTS, Louise Crawford Pulaski Citizen 07 Jun 2016
Mrs. Louise Crawford Pitts, 94, of Pulaski died June 2, 2016, at Meadowbrook Health and Rehabilitation Center. Mrs. Pitts was born Aug. 3, 1921, in Fayetteville and was a member of First Baptist Church, Pulaski. The loving wife, mother, grandmother, great-grandmother, great-great-grandmother and sister worked for Morris Harwell & Son for many years. Mrs. Pitts is preceded in death by husband of 60 years Amuel Pitts; parents, Sidney Johnson and Grace Jeans Crawford; brothers, William Crawford, John Crawford; and sisters, Virginia McClanahan and Mildred Dunn. Graveside services were June 4 at Maplewood Cemetery with Mark Johnson officiating. Memorial donations may be made to First Baptist Church building fund, 409 S. First St., Pulaski, TN 38478. Survivors include sons, Larry Pitts and wife Peggy of Cunningham, Mike Pitts and wife Amy of Pulaski; grandchildren, Kim McLean, Chrisie Brown, Mellisa Waskiewicz, Megen Reynolds, Katie Pitts, Shane Pitts, Ryan Pitts; seven great-grandchildren; one great-great grandchild; sisters, Lillian Posey, Patricia Moyers, both of Fayetteville, Carolyn McInnis of Texas; brothers, David Crawford of Fayetteville, Raymond Crawford of Louisiana; and several nieces and nephews.
