PHILLIPS-TAYLOR, (African American), Bledsoe Rd., GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Phillips-Taylor Cemetery. 

 Location: Go north on North 1st St and take Bledsoe Road to the right to the top of the hill. The cemetery is on the right along a long driveway on your right in what is now a mowed strip.

This aerial map shows the cemetery marked with yellow highlight borders which mainly is the mowed area and not necessarily all a part of the cemetery.
 Mapping from ACME Mapper 2.0, MS Streets & Trips, & Google mapping sites. Modified and added here 12 May 2013 by C. Wayne Austin.
