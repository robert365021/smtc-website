WAID CEMETERY (Stoneboro Rd, north) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Waid/Wade Cemetery GPS location.
Location: Take Highway 231 north out of Fayetteville and go several miles and turn east on (right) on Stoneboro l Road. Go about 2 miles and the cemetery is on your right behind a rock wall upon the ledge of the road.

This Aerial Map shows the plot where the cemetery resides next to Stoneboro Road.
