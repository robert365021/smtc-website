WARNER CEMETERY, (Warner Bridge Road, 2 miles w. of Shelbyville), BEDFORD COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Shown as the Warner Cemetery on USGS mapping. The GPS coordinates are corrects as far as I can tell, but I have not proven them out with an actual on-site visit.

From Shelbyville go out West Lane St which becomes either Fishing Ford Pike or Warner Bridge Road. Follow that as it winds northwest toward the Duck River for about 3 miles. After passing the junction of Troupe Rd and  Fishing Ford Pike aka Warner Bridge Road go about a half of a mile and look right at a pond on the right. With permission, turn right into the private drive and go up the hill 200 yards to the top and the cemetery is on the left (30 feet) in a small round tree thicket as shown on these maps below. The cemetery in the past has been reported in bad shape, but may have been cleared some since, but the stones are probably not being maintained.

 This aerial view shows the various assets needed to get to this cemetery. From the maps this appears to be exclusive & highly private land so account for that in your visit with permission as it may have gated locks.

This view from MS Bing - Birds Eye view shows the faint view of the few trees under (marked with a red check) which the cemetery resides. This entrance is private and may be gated. I am unaware of the owner's name at this time.
