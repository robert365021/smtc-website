GIBSONVILLE CEMETERY, (Hwy 245, aka Columbia to Campbellsville Rd) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

From the above map we see the Gibsonville GPS location as N 35.41924, W -87.10223.

Location: From Pulaski take the highway 64 West a mile or so and turn right (north) on Highway 166. Follow that for about 14 miles and turn right on Highway 245 toward Columbia Tennessee. Follow Hwy 245 for more than 5 miles and after you pass Waco Road the cemetery is just up the road on the right before you come to Blue Branch Road upon a hill above the road under Cedar Trees. Cemetery can be seen from the road. The Cemetery is fenced and signs maintained. Keep the gate closed to prevent cattle from entering the cemetery if you go here.

 This aerial view from Google shows with a red cross the fenced light green rectangular plot of land whereby the cemetery is located.
