STEWART, L., 1 Aug 1840 - (28 Aug 1920). "Co. C. 48th Tenn. Inf. C.S.A. 1861-'5." (Giles Co. native; s/o William Pemberton Stewart, Sr. and Martha Green Stewart; h/o and shares stone with Elizabeth A. Bailey Stewart, mar. 1 Jul 1866 in Lawrence Co. Tn.. Ref: e-mail to Mary Bob from Wayne Goates FAG member 46795365) 8434

John Littleton Stewart was the son of William Pemberton Stewart, Sr. and Martha Green. He was born August 1, 1849 in Giles, Tennessee and died August 28, 1920 in Maury, Tennessee. He was married to Elizabeth A. Bailey on July 1, 1866 in Lawrence County, Tennessee. Bond was issued in Lawrence, Tennessee on July 1, 1866 by W. F. Bumpass, BM and they were married by W. C. Davis, JP. Their children were:

John Bailey Stewart
Unnamed Female Child Stewart
Martha J. Stewart Willbanks
James Thomas Stewart
Lucy Meredith Stewart Isbell
Thomas L. "Tom" Stewart
William Morgan "Bill" Stewart
Note: TS stone clearly has dob 1 Aug 1840. [Mary Bob McClain Richardson]
