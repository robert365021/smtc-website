EDWARDS FAMILY CEMETERY, (Lewisburg Reservoir Rd) MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Edwards Family Cemetery. 

This road map shows the cemetery on the east side of Old Campbell Road. This is about 7 miles north of Pulaski.

This aerial map shows the cemetery well maintained. It is fenced with electrical shock protection and the ability to disable it safely. The road to the cemetery also doubles as the road to the residence and also to the farm operation so be sure you go here with deliberate upscale intentions and secure permission if needed.
