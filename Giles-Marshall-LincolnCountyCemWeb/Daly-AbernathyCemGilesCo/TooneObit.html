DECATUR DAILY - Decatur, Alabama - September 2, 1935 -  Obituary

MRS. EMMA TOONE IS LAID TO REST

Funeral Services In Prospect, Tenn., Sunday

Mrs. Emma Toone, of Tanner, who died Saturday afternoon at 4:15 o'clock, was laid to rest Sunday in Prospect, Tenn.

Mrs. Toone, who was 60 years of age, is survived by four sons, E. V., W. P., and J. T., all of Tanner, (Limestone County Al.) and L. R. of Pulaski, Tenn; three sisters, Mrs. Essie White of Athens, Mrs. May Magingson and Miss Zoe Hargrove of Prospect, Tenn.; five brothers, T. G. and A. B. Hargrove of Nashville, W. C. Hargrove of Prospect, H. H. Hargrove of Decatur and L. L. Hargrove of Ardmore.

Funeral services were conducted Sunday afternoon at three o'clock at Prospect, interment being made there, Drinkard and Howell, of Hartselle, directing.

Pallbearers were John Toone, Jessie Toone, Marvin Toone, Milton Toone, Clifford Hargrove and Laureen Hargrove.

Obit sent in by Mary Bob Richardson, 15 Jul 2011.
