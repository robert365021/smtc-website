DALY-ABERNATHY CEMETERY, (Pleasant Hill Rd) S.E. GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
The GPS coordinates for the Daly-Abernathy Cemetery are shown on the above map. 
Location: From I-65 south take Exit 1 and go across Hwy 31 onto Pleasant Hill Road and travel about 2 miles and look to the right. The cemetery will be next to the road inside a low rock wall.

In this aerial view we see the Daly-Abernathy Cemetery adjacent to the Pleasant Hill Road. It is named on this Google map.
