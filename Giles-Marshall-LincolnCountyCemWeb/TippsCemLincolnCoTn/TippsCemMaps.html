TIPPS CEMETERY, (Stephens Creek Rd.) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Tipps Cemetery as 35.19906 X -86.41390.

 Location: From Fayetteville go Hwy 64 east to Hwy 50 then on Past Mulberry to Crystal Ridge Rd and turn right. Follow that for more than 2 miles to Stephens Creek Rd and when that bends left (or southwest) look out in a pasture for the cemetery behind a fence abt 30 yards from the road on the left. You will have to look over a fence row and hedge row to see the cemetery from the road. Get permission from the land owner to get into this cemetery.  

This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly in a grove of trees next to the road.
