NEW ZION BAPT. CHURCH CEMETERY, NEW ZION, Hwy 31 Alt. GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE.

MOORE, Sarah Ann, wife of N. S. Moore, 15 Apr 1839 - 7 Nov 1891. "Behold ye stranger passing by. As you are now so once was I. As I am now so must you be. Prepare for death and follow me."
We are in the old section, on the south side, of the old church building.
