NEW ZION BAPT. CHURCH CEMETERY, NEW ZION, Hwy 31 Alt. GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE.
 
CRUNK, Delia Crunk Tinnon 18 Jan 1861 - 11 Aug 1929.
This stone could be listed under the surname Tinnon. It appears she was 1st the wife of J. J. Crunk who died years before and perhaps Delia remarried to --- Tinnon. Tinnon does not have a tombstone in this cemetery if he was laid to rest here. 
We are in the old section, on the south side, of the old church building.
