Obituary:
PARTIN, Patcie The Pulaski Citizen 16 Jun 1904
Miss Patcie Parton, aged about 22, died suddenly Tuesday morning (b. abt 1882 d. 14 Jun 1904, ) at the home of Walter Williams near Sumac. She was a daughter of Charles Partin and related to Forrest Partin, the blacksmith. The burial occurred at New Zion yesterday.

Walter Lafayette Williams (the son of Tempy Louise PARTIN and Green Harrison Williams) would have been the cousin of Patcie PARTIN since her father Charles PARTIN was the brother of Tempy Louise PARTIN. Forrest was the half brother of Tempy Louise PARTIN Williams and Charles PARTIN.
