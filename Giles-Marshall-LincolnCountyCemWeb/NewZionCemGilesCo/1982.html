NEW ZION BAPT. CHURCH CEMETERY, NEW ZION, Hwy 31 Alt. GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE.

DOSS, Thomas J., 7 Jan 1837 - 26 Jan 1925. Company A, 32nd Tennessee Infantry, Confederates States Army. "Cross of Honor" (steel), showing Thomas remained loyal until surrender in 1865. (Soldier's Pension #S3453 32nd Inf.)
