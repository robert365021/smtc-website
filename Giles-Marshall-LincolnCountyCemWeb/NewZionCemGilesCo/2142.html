NEW ZION BAPT. CHURCH CEMETERY, NEW ZION, Hwy 31 Alt. GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE.

WILLIAMS, Newbern W., 25 Aug 1873 - 20 Jul 1958. (Giles Co. native; s/o Green Harrrison and Temple Louise Partin Williams; lst Ida Farrington Hamlett in 1897, 2nd mar. Martha D. McGinnis in 1922, 3rd mar. Velma Downey in 1929; circuit minister.)
We are now on the east side of the road, in front of the old church building.
