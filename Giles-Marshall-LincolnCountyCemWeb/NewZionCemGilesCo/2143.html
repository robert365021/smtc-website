NEW ZION BAPT. CHURCH CEMETERY, NEW ZION, Hwy 31 Alt. GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE.


WILLIAMS, Martha (D.) McGinness, 7 Sep 1883 - 12 Jan 1926. "Mother." (Smithville DeKalb Co. Tn. native; d/o Richard Wallace & Mary Elizabeth Clark McGinnis; 19 Nov 1922 mar. Newbern Winfield William, he was the circuit minister to Dekalb Co. church she went to.) We are now on the east side of the road, in front of the old church building.
