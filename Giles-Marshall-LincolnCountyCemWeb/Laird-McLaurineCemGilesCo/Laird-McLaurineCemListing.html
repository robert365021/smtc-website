Transcribed from Photos taken by C Wayne Austin 24 Oct 2012. This cemetery is presented in the book Cemeteries of Giles County Tennessee by the Historical Society 1986. Page 193-195. Findagrave has this cemetery split between the John Laird Cemetery (54) listings & McLaurine Cemetery with just a few more listings. There are 63 known listings above with many graves marked with fieldstones by standing them on their end and burying them facing east in a row of graves.
