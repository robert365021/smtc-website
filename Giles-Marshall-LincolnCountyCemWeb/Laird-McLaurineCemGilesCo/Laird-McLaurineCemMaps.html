LAIRD-McLAURINE CEMETERY, (Fry Branch Rd, Old Lynnville) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS location of the Laird-McLaurine Cemetery GPS: N 35.37936 X W 87.03139.
From Pulaski go north on Highway 31 toward Columbia. Enter Old Lynnville and turn left on Fry Branch Road. Go about 500 yards and the long road to the cemetery up the slope is on the right. It is marked by a sign.

This is a blow up of the cemetery showing a wooden fence surrounding it. Graves are as in all old cemeteries scattered around the premises with most of them being in the center.
Do not go here if your aims are not to honor the deceased. The sheriff and neighbors do not take kindly to vandals, devil worshippers, and other such evils.
