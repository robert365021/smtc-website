PLEASANT RIDGE CEMETERY, Pleasant Ridge Road, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Pleasant Ridge Church Cemetery as GPS N 35.10222, W -87.16322. 

 Location: Go southwest Hwy 11 (Minor Hill Rd) to Anthony Hill (turn right on Fall River Road. Follow that about 2 1/2 miles and turn left on Pleasant Ridge Road. Go about 2 miles and the church and cemetery are on your left.

This aerial map shows the cemetery on the left of the Road on the northeast side of the church.
