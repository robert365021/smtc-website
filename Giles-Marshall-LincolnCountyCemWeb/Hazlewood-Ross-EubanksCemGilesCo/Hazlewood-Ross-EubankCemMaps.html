HAZLEWOOD-ROSS-EUBANK CEMETERY, (Fiducia Rd off of Hwy 166) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Hazlewood-Ross-Eubank Cemetery. 

Directions: From the Hwy 64 (Pulaski bypass) go south on Hwy 166 (Bethel Road), turn left on Hurd Road, and then back south (right) on Fiducia Road. Go just over 1/2 mile and look right and a locked gate will be present. With permission go thru that closing it behind you since cattle is in there. Follow that for 30 yards and turn 90 degrees right and you will be looking north up a clearing. Follow the clearing to the cemetery about 200 yards away. It is fenced with the gate on the south west corner of the  fence. It is labeled the Ross Cemetery on GNIS Maps.

This aerial map shows the cemetery across the Fiducia road from a house from a residential house. The gate is south of this image.
