WELLS-SMITH (Bunker Hill & Mitchell Rd Junction) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Wells-Smith Cemetery at GPS N 35.12897 X W -86.87223. 

 Location: Go south on Hwy 31 for 12 or so miles and turn east on Bryson Road. Follow that under I-65 and continue north on Bunker Hill/Newman Roads then bear or turn right on Bunker Hill Road (north) and go about 2 miles and at the junction of Mitchell Road the cemetery will be under some trees on the right behind an old barn in someone's storage lot for old cars & equipment and other things of a private property nature.

This aerial map shows the cemetery marked with a red check inside the clump of trees on private property. Livestock was on the premises of the cemetery trampling the fence that once surrounded the cemetery.
