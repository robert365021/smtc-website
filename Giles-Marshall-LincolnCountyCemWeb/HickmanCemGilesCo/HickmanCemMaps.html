HICKMAN CEMETERY, west side Hwy 166,  GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Sugar Valley Farm     Map #2, 1,227 Acres


The center of the map  with the yellow check marks the location of the Hickman Cemetery. This cemetery is not detailed on the USGS records and I have no reason to believe the GPS coordinates are not correct as shown above.


The Hickman Cemetery is small thicket in a field on the old Luther Morrow farm, known, in 1996, as the Sugar Valley Farm. Take Hwy 64 W. then go north (right) on Highway 166, go more than 10 miles and pass thru Campbellsville, Giles County, Tennessee and the cemetery is past Campbellsville along Hwy 166 aka Mt Pleasant Road and about a mile further along Hwy 166 past the Hwy 245 turn off. This is a large 1,227 acre farm.
