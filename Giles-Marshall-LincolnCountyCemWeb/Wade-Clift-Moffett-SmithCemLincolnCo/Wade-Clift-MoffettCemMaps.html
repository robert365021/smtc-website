WADE-CLIFT-MOFFETT CEMETERY, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Wade-Clift-Moffett-Smith Cemetery as N 35.22585 X W -86.59406.

 Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 431 several miles to Daves Hollow Road and turn right. Follow that about 1.5 miles and the farm road to the cemetery is next to a barn on the south side. The cemetery is southwest of there up the slope about 400 yards. It is due west of the house, but the house faces northeast.

In this view the cemetery shown as I surrounded with yellow lines about the area of the cemetery enclosure. This is not to be taken as a legal boundary but a rough estimate of the area where graves obviously appear.
