We did the best we could to classify between the black & white sections. Generally the old section is white and the new area of the cemetery is black, but if we misclassified your ancestor or kin let us know and we will change it. The confusion stems partly because we did not do a complete photographic survey of the monuments of this cemetery. [WA 31 May 2010.]

This cemetery was photographed in a visit 5 Dec 2005, by Wayne Austin & again 3 Feb 2010 to get area photos. Typing and organization by Faye Bradford. Programming to this site by Wayne Austin 1 Jun 2010. It is found in both the Marshall and Giles County Cemetery books. Some of the facts come from those records.
