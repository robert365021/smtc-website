ROBERTSON FORK CEMETERY, Hwy 129 Robertson Fork Community, MARSHALL COUNTY,  TENNESSEE.    

 The GPS coordinates for the Roberson Fork just inside the Marshall County line from Giles County are shown on the above map.

Robertson Fork Cemetery shown above Highway 129 on this topography map off of Wilkes Hollow Road, beside a residence.
Located two miles west of I-65 out Highway 129 Exit 27, and one-fourth mile north of Highway 129 on Wilkes Hollow Road. This is the site of the old Robertson Fork Baptist Church that existed here at one time.

In this aerial view we see the cemetery outlined in blue. The Americans of African descendent graves are located in the northwest area of the cemetery along the edge generally.  The above outline does not represent a legal boundary to this cemetery.
