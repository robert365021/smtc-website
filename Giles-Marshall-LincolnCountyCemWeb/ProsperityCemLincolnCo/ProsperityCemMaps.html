PROSPERITY CHURCH CEMETERY, Yukon, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Prosperity Cemetery. Since it is a big cemetery the coordinates given are near the center of the biggest area.
Location: From Fayetteville go southeast on Hwy 110 go past Skinem and turn right on Carmago Road. Follow that to the dead end and turn left on McDeal Rd and go 1/2 mile and turn left on Yukon Rd and that crosses over Red Hill Rd and becomes the church and cemetery circular road. Unless you honor those who have gone on or have official business stay out of here.
