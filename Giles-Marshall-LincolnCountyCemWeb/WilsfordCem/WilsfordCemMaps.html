WILSFORD CEMETERY-(Kerr Hill Road), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Wilsford Cemetery. The nearest major town is Old Lynnville formally Waco, Tennessee about 5+ miles as a crow flies northeast. 

Location Go Hwy 31 north from Pulaski to Kerr Hill Road and turn left. Follow that for about 2 miles and two tenths and look to the left 100 yards into a field for a grove of trees that contains the cemetery. There is a gate into the pasture that is usable to visit this cemetery. Be sure to close it behind you because there are cattle in this field. Otherwise I think the right of way to this cemetery is clear.

This aerial map shows the cemetery out in a field just before the road bends back toward the south marked by a red check. The outline of the white picket fence can be seen on this map.
