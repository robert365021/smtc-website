BATES CEMETERY (Camargo Rd, N. of Kirkland) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Bates Cemetery as N 35.07820 X W -86.63417.

 Location: Go south on Hwy 231 from Fayetteville and turn right on the Ardmore Highway (Hwy 110). Follow that more than 5 miles to Kirkland and turn north on Camargo Rd. Go about a mile and the cemetery can be seen northeast of the junction of McMillan & Camargo Roads - on the right - out in a cultivated field - in a clump of trees.

This aerial map shows the cemetery under an old clump of trees, out in a field.
