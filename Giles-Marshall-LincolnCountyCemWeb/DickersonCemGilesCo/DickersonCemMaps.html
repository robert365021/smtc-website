DICKERSON CEMETERY, (Hwy 31, 2 Mi. S. of Old Lynnville), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the Longitude and Latitude or GPS, as it is commonly known, for the Dickerson Cemetery.

Location: Travel north out of Pulaski on Highway 31 for several miles. After passing Buford Station Road on your right go nearly a mile and look to your right for the massive rock fence that encloses this cemetery. When you find it you are about 2 miles back from Waco (or recently renamed to Old Lynnville.) This is private land - permission is a must and parking is not available on Highway 31 so be careful here.

In this aerial view we have placed a red check at the site of the cemetery.
