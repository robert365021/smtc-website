This cemetery is inside a rock wall along the east side of Highway 31 between Pulaski and the Maury County line. The grounds had tall grass covering it so a grave analysis was not possible for the short time I was there. I cannot verify any of the counts of individuals who are actually interred here, but am relying on the memorial for accuracy. The listing originally came from the book Giles County Tennessee Cemeteries by the Historical Society, 1985, but the new granite monument showed five new listings. They were Mary Etta Dickerson, Dickerson Keeling, Catherine Royal Bills, Sarah Garland Royall, William Richard Stone.
