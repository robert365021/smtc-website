McDANIEL-PARKS CEMETERY,  (Providence Rd) - LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the McDaniel-Parks Cemetery. 

From Hwy 50 & Hwy 64 (Lynchburg Hwy) take Hwy 50 for a 1/2 mile and turn left on Badenhop Blvd. Go four tenths of a mile and turn left on Providence Road. Follow that for about two miles and the cemetery will be on the left about 70 yards across the pasture from a rock wall that lines the road on the north side. From the road you should notice the iron fence if the rotten stump is gone.

This aerial map shows with a red check the cemetery north of the road. The road along there is lined with a rock wall.
