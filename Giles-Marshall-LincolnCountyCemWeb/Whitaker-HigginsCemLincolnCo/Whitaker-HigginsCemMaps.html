WHITAKER-HIGGINS CEMETERY, HWY 231, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Buchanan (David) Cemetery as N 35.22936 X W -86.56342.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 several miles and look to your right into a field where the cemetery is located inside a rock wall. Now the cemetery is visible from the road under the large oak trees.
