WILKERSON CEMETERY, (Hwy 166) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

William Puryear Wilkerson, 1822 - 1882 & Rachel Hughes Wilkerson, 1837 - 1924. Their footstones will reside behind the photographer down next to the ground covered in debris. Coming off of a long day of photographing hundreds of stone I missed that thought at the time.
