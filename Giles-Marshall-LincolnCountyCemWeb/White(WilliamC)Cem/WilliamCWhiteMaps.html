WHITE (William Clark) CEMETERY (Shoals Bluff)GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the William Clark White Family Cemetery. GPS: N 35.00814 X W 87.11137. 

 Location: Cemetery south of Shoals Bluff church.
Go south on the Hwy 166 (Bethel Highway) from Pulaski and turn right on Bethel Road. Travel that for more then two miles and turn right on Shoal Creek Rd. Follow that for about 1/2 mile and turn left into Liberty Hill Rd. The cemetery is in a pasture southeast from a lake and and east of the cemetery are hills. There is no easy access to this cemetery so get help from the land owner to get there.

This aerial map shows the cemetery just up the slope from the lake on the southeast side. On the east side of the cemetery are high hills. There is a new wrought iron fence surrounding the monuments installed recently by Lloyd Jackson.
