BUCHANAN CEMETERY, Howell, LINCOLN COUNY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Buchanan (David) Cemetery as N 35.22192 X W -86.61194.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 431 and go to the Howell community. Turn left on Shields Road and after a few hundred yards turn left (or south) on Sawyer Road adn follow that to the crest of the hill and the cemetery is on your left about 100 yards down in a wooded area & northeast of an old barn.

This image attempts to show the outline of the cemetery outlined in yellow as on the northern edge of a garden.
