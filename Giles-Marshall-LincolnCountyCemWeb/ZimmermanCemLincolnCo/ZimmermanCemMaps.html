ZIMMERMAN CEMETERY, Hwy 273, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Zimmerman Cemetery as N 35.14820 X W -86.73718.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 and turn left (south) on Hwy 273 aka old Elkton Pike. Follow that for about 7 miles and turn right on Cunningham Rd and go about 500 yards or past the turn off and dead end. The cemetery is on your left behind an old rusty top barn which may or may not be there on your visit. There is a gate into the pasture on your left you will use for access to the cemetery and horse pasture. See the owner in the nearby home for permission/notification.
