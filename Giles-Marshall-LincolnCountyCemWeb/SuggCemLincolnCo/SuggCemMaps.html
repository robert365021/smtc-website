SUGG CEMETERY, Boonshill, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Sugg Cemetery as N 35.15771 X W -86.72326 along Old Elkton Pike. 

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 several miles to Hwy 273 aka Old Elkton Pike and turn left. Follow that to Swan Creek Road on your right and go behind the first house after you pass Swan Creek Road. The inhabitants of that home lease the property which actually belongs to the Wilson family who lives nearby.

This pictorial view from Google shows the cemetery on the north side of the road marked with a yellow pointer. How bout that Chic-fil-lay advertisement specimen on our left.
