GILBERT CEMETERY, (Poor Grab Road), LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Gilbert Cemetery as N 35.33368 X W -86.56607.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 431 toward Lewisburg to Petersburg. Continue N on S. High Street turn right on Water Street. That becomes (Hwy 129) Petersburg to Chestnut Ridge Road. Go less than 3 miles and turn north (left) on Bledsoe Road. Follow that for more than mile and turn right (east) on Poor Grab Chestnut Ridge Road. When the road bends back north the cemetery is straight ahead up the ridge under the trees as marked on the GPS map.
