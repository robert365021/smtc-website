EATON CEMETERY, Hwy 50 sw of Lynchburg, MOORE COUNTY TENNESSEE

From the above map we see the Eaton Cemetery GPS location as N 35.26484, W -86.39199.
Location: From the center of Lynchburg take Highway 50 southwest out of Lynchburg, (Moore County) and go about a mile and 1/4 and pass over the East Fork of Mulberry Creek. Just before crossing over the creek look left at the new signage Eaton Cemetery. Turn around at a convenient place and go back across the creek again viewing the cemetery the 2nd time being careful of the busy traffic. About a 150 years after you cross over the Creek turn right into the Old Fayetteville Hwy as shown on the map below and park on the right side of the highway. Walk back to the right-of-way for Hwy 50 and cross and follow down the mowed area of the right of way to the cemetery. Have a small light aluminum ladder with you to cross the fence without damaging it. If you don't have a ladder continue on nearly to the creek and the fence fizzles out and you can cross over and return (U-turn) to the cemetery. (Though I give you these instructions I take no responsibility for your safely in applying them and neither your general safety either because this is a bit of a rough entrance. However, any other entrance to this cemetery will get you scratched from the prolific black berry vines. Do not climb on the fences either or "Snuffy Smiff" the farmer will get his "shooting armaments" after you.
