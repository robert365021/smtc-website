Unknown graves covered and/or surrounded by stone, some rough cut, others, raw limestone: 3999/4000/4001/4002/4003
Elizabeth Adkins was omitted from the Moore County Cemetery book mentioned below. Also there are large piles of rocks and graves obviously outlined with stone in the back (western) side of this cemetery. William & Nancy Spencer and Susan Eaton at the (eastern) side of the cemetery toward the highway have stones outlining the graves along with a wrought iron fence surrounding. Elizabeth Adkins toward the western side of the cemetery has un-carved stones outlining her grave and also stones piled up to protect the grave. Sometimes since the outlining rocks shift and fall that is not clear.

Added here by C. Wayne Austin. 11 Feb 2015 using photos dated 4 Feb 2015. This cemetery is also presented in the book Moore County Tennessee Cemeteries on page 15.
