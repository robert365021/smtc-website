FOX-WHEATLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 31 Alt, Cornersville Rd) MARSHALL COUNTY

The GPS coordinates for the Fox-Wheatly Cemetery are shown on the above map. 
Located on Highway 31Alt/11 the cemetery is on shown on the above map on the east side of the road south of Lewisburg and north of Cornersville. The Cemetery is behind an immaculate Rock wall.
Official description of the location: Two miles south of Lewisburg, on the Cornersville Road on west side (right) of road inside a large rock wall.
