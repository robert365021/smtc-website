BOYET CEMETERY, (Hwy 431, near Ellington Airport) MARSHALL COUNTY
The GPS coordinates for the Boyet Cemetery are shown on the above map. 
Located on Highway 431 a couple of miles north of Lewisburg. Turn into the Boyet Cemetery Road and follow that to the cemetery.

With this aerial view one can see the cemetery under a grove of trees marked with a red check. The road on this Google map is incorrect. The road shown north of there, but more faintly, is the correct road to take.
