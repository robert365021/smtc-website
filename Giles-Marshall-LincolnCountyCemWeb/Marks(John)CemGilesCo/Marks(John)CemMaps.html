MARKS (John) CEMETERY, (Chicken Creek Road) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This topographical map from Topozone shows the GPS coordinates of the Marks Cemetery. I believe the GPS coordinates to be accurate.
Take the southern (Hwy 64) Bypass for Pulaski going west Go past Hwy 31 past Hwy 166 & Hwy 11. Next go about 1 & 1/2 miles further and turn left (west) on Chicken Creek Road. Go 7 tenths of a mile and look left at a field road into the Rocky Point Cemetery. Go 75 yards to the gate and with permission thru it into the open field and before you reach the Rocky Point cemetery gate across the way bear south (right) and go up the slope along the outside fence line of the Rocky Point Cemetery to the end of that cemetery. Next continuing in the same direction go another 50 yards further and turn 90 degrees and face the tree line. In the wintertime the cemetery can be seen under the trees with the few stones that are still standing.
