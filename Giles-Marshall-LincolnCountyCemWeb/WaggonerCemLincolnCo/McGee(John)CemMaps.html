McGEE (John) CEMETERY, Gimlet Road West side, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the John McGee grave GPS location as N 35.25889 X W -86.50290
Location: From Fayetteville take the Bypass to Hwy 64 and and go east toward Chattanooga. After a mile or so turn (left) north on the Lynchburg Hwy (Hwy 55) and go about 3 and 1/2 miles to the Booneville Road and turn left (or north). Go 1 mile and turn left or northwest on Gimlet Road. Go about 1 3/4 miles and the cemetery is on your right as marked above at the edge of a tree lined hedgerow out in a field.
