Previously Elizabeth Sherrell who died 7 Oct 1848 at age 29 years was listed in this cemetery, but when updated surveys were made her memorial was found in the Sharp Cemetery further down the road on the east side.

This little cemetery is beside the road on the property of a homeowner in his side yard at 570 Shelbyville Highway 231. The owner will be happy for your visit, but watch out for his dogs.
