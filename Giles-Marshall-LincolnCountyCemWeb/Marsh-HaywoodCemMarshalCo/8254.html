MARSH-HAYWOOD CEMETERY, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

Francis Ferguson memorial, boxtomb under the fallen cherry tree on the left.
2005

The above photo taken 17 Jul 2008, after most of the tree decayed.
 
FERGESON, Francis, Died Jan 9, 1854, "In the 64 years of his age". Boxtomb broken and covered with fallen & rotting trees. The name is spelled Fergeson on the above stone but the spelling is known to be both ways in this graveyard. (This boxtomb is broken and in danger of completely caving into itself.
Photos dated 29 Nov 2005 & 17 Jul 2008, information by Wayne Austin, 14 Jul & 23 Jul 2008.
