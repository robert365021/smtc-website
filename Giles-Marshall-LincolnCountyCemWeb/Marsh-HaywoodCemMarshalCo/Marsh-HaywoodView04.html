MARSH-HAYWOOD CEMETERY, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

 

<-->


 Looking into the cemetery from the north side we see the damage from the cattle roaming freely and also damage from falling trees. Back to the right of the fenced area are two tall memorials that have been pushed over since 2005. They are lying face down and the inscriptions cannot now be read.

 The edge of this cemetery is being used as a feed lot and the cattle come in an out of the cemetery at will. I understand the needs and hardships of farming but this is bit ridiculous.
Photos by Wayne Austin 17 Jul 2008.
