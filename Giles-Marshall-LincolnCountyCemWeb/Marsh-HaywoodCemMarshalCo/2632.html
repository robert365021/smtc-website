MARSH-HAYWOOD CEMETERY, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
Benjamine Fergeson. This is where I found this stone still lost under the large fallen log in 2008. The footstone is on the left. It is in the row with the Francis & Mary F. Fergeson boxtombs.

Benjamine Fergeson memorial. It is peeling away and soon will there will be no inscription to read.

FERGESON, Benjamine, son of N. P. Fergeson, Aug 18, 1851 - Aug 1, 1852. Aged 11 Months and 12 days.
Inscription: "In -- memory of -- Benjamine -- Fergeson -- son of -- N.P. Fergeson. -- was born -- Aug 18, 1851 -- and died -- August 1, 1852. -- Aged 11 months and -- 12 days.
(-- double dashes indicate a line break in the inscription)
