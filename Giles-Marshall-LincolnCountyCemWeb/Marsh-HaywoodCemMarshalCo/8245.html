MARSH-HAYWOOD CEMETERY, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

R. W. Collins. Notice the scattered boulders next to the tree. I suppose that is some of the 55 fieldstones that marked graves when this cemetery was surveyed in 1958.
