PROSSER-GABBERT CEMETERY, Cheese Rd., MARSHALL COUNTY, TENNESSEE

From the above map we are given the Prosser-Gabbert Cemetery GPS location as N. 35. 35403 X W -86.66420, Located one and one-half miles north of Cane Creek Church of Christ, and one mile east of Talley  Station.
Location: Go Hwy 431 out of Petersburg a couple of miles to Cheese Road and turn right. Go about one mile and the cemetery is behind a home on a farm & Cemetery road about 1/4 mile back west of the house on the left side of the road. Permission can be given at the home of Mr. Pigg.

This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly as a square area next to the gravel road. Cheese Road is north of this plot.
