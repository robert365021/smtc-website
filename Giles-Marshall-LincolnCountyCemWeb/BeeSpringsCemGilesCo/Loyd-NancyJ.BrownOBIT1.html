BEE SPRINGS CEMETERY (N.E. of Elkton), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE


 Nancy J. Brown Loyd

 

OBITUARY:  Pulaski Citizen, Thursday, April 6, 1899
  
We regret to announce the death of Mrs. John H. Loyd, which occurred at her home near Pisgah at 4 o'clock Monday morning.  Her death was due to a hemorrhage, one of several which she had had recently.  The burial of this good woman, who died standing on the promises of the Lord, took place at Bee Spring Tuesday at 11:00 a.m. with services by Rev. Wood Bouldin ....

Snuff Branch - We are sorry to hear of the death of Mrs. John Loyd of Bee Spring . . . ..
