BEE SPRINGS CEMETERY (N.E. of Elkton), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Mrs Samantha Phelps
 
 OBITUARY: Pulaski Citizen, Thursday, February 15, 1872
      
   Died on Leatherwood Creek on last Sunday of heart disease complicated by pneumonia, Mrs. Samantha Phelps, wife of T. Benton Phelps and daughter of John H. Loyd, Esquire, age about 30 years.
