BEE SPRINGS CEMETERY (N.E. of Elkton), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

From the above map we see the Bee Springs Cemetery GPS location. It is marked as across the road from Bee Spring (Methodist) Church 

Location: Take Highway 64 east out of Pulaski and take Interstate 65 South to Bryson Road Exit (6) Go east up Bryson Road and turn north on Bunker Hill Road. After about 3/4 a mile turn back right or east on Bee Springs Road. Travel Bee Springs Road about 2 miles and see the church building on the right and the cemetery on the left behind a rock wall.
This Aerial Map shows the plot where the cemetery resides next the Bee Springs Road. The Church & spring are on the right. This is a very old cemetery and contains many fragile stones easily damaged by overzealous people, so be gentle on these soft monuments if you have business here. Touch them only if you have experience with old monuments.
