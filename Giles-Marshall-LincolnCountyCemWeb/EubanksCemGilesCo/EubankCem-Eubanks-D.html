EUBANK CEMETERY, (Hanna Branch Road, west off of Hwy 166) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
EUBANK, Denton, 12 Oct 1877 - 6 Dec 1898 (s/o Azariah Jackson and Henrietta Anthony Eubank)

The Pulaski Citizen 8 Dec 1898

Denton Eubank died of typhoid fever at home near Aspen Hill, Tuesday morning, Dec. 6, and was buried at the family graveyard yesterday. Deceased was an excellent
young man, son of A. J. Eubanks and brother to Nathan Eubanks of Heron.
