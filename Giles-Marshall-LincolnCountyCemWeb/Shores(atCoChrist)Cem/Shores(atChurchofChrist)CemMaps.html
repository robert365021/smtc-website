SHORES CEMETERY, at Shores Church of Christ, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

 This map shows the Shores (at the Church of Christ) Cemetery GPS locations known also as the Latitude and Longitude. There are actually 4 Shores Cemeteries in Giles County Tennessee so identify them specifically to differentiate.
This road map will guide you to the Shores Cemetery on Shores Road at the junction of Fall River Road and Shores Road behind the Church of Christ building. That is west of Highway 11. The other Shores cemetery south of here shown on this map is known as the Shores on the Hill or Old Shores Cemetery.
