KINCAID CEMETERY, BRICK CHURCH, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
Elizabeth M. Wife of D. W. Kincaid. Memorial just back this way and to our right of the gray granite memorial of the Jones. Calling this Row 10, 01

Elizabeth M. Kincaid.

 KINCAID, Elizabeth M. Jones., wife of D. W. (David Washington) Kincaid, 18 Nov 1840 - 28 Aug 1909 (Row 10, 02)
 Census data has a David W. Kincaid born about 1838 in Giles County Tennessee as the son of David M. Kincaid. Since his wife Mary E. West was living until 1845. the David W. was the son of David M. & Mary West Kincaid and not the 2nd wife of David M. Kincaid, b. 1802.
