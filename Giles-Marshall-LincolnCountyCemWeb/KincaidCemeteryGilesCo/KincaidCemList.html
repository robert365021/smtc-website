KINCAID CEMETERY, BRICK CHURCH, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
    
This graveyard was surveyed starting on the east side and progressing westward within the north/south rows. There is a large area in the middle of the cemetery with no clear indication of graves. I was unable to count the rows in that area so I chose to skip up to Row 10 as the next row after the Row 2 (early David M. Kincaid family memorials.) We photographed the cemetery from north to south so grave 01 is north of grave 02.
