KINCAID CEMETERY, BRICK CHURCH, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
It is a mystery to me what the two stones here were used for. Both are common Yellow Chirt Stones, but the one on the left is ground out in the center. May have been an old Corn Meal grounding stone that fell obsolete and was brought down to the graveyard and set up as a memorial stone for a burial. I do not know if these stones mark graves.
Photos & information by Wayne Austin, 1 Jul 2008.
