KINCAID CEMETERY, BRICK CHURCH, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
It is obvious the fieldstone marks a grave. It stands on its side and it is in line with the marked stones above. I would bet it belongs to the Kincaid family. Probably a small child. Sometimes this can be proven if there was a fieldstone set at the foot of the grave because the space from head to foot indicates a child or adult. I did not look for that.
