KINCAID CEMETERY, BRICK CHURCH, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
The light colored subject stone next to the far left.

David M. Kincaid

KINCAID, D. (David) M. (Marion), 15 Jan 1802 - 6 Jul 1877 (Row 2 03) "Blessed are the pure in heart for they shall see my God." During the 1850 census Elizabeth the second wife was already in the household of David M.. There was also a David Kincaid Sr. living in the next household over from David M.'s home. That was in the home of Benjamin (age 21) & Martha (age 23) Williams. David Sr. was born in Pennsylvania according to this record.

David Marion Kincaid, 15 Jan 1802 - 6 Jul 1877, buried in Kincaid Cemetery, Giles County Tennessee, married to (1) Mary A. West who was born 15 Jun 1807, died 8 Nov 1845
Children of David & Mary A.: Nancy J. Kincaid; David W. Kincaid; John Kincaid

David Marion married again to (2) Elizabeth Diana Griffis, had more children: Polk Kincaid, Emily Kincaid, Thomas, Franklin (Bud) Anderson, Sarah L. Sarah Lewina Alice, Donna and Joseph E.

This Mary A West would be the sister to Samuel A. West, born 1809 interred in the West Family Cemetery over in the Blue Branch area of Giles Co. Dist 14.
