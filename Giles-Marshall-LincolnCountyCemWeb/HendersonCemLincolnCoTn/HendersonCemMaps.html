HENDERSON CEMETERY, Old Huntsville Road,  LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Henderson Cemetery.
Location: From Fayetteville go south on Hwy 231 to Lincoln Road and turn west (or right). Follow that for a mile until it dead ends into Old Huntsville Road and turn left (south). Follow that for 1/4 mile and the cemetery is on the (right) bank of the road. It is just past a house being and is maintained as a part of the owners maintenance of their yard.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly marked with a red check next to Old Huntsville Road on the west side.
