HARRIS CEMETERY, (near) LEWISBURG, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE.

This cemetery is located on the Batten Road, near the Clay Hill area. south east of Henry Horton Park. It is on property today owned by Batte family, Michelle and her husband.  The land owners has spent time cleaning and restoring this cemetery.
