HARRIS CEMETERY, (near) LEWISBURG, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map includes the location of the Harris Cemetery detailed on this web site. However, it has not been placed on this map. It is somewhere around the red "+" on the Batten Road, near the Clay Hill area. Originally, this cemetery was located in Bedford County but when the county lines where changed in the mid 1800s, it ended up in Marshall County.
 Information by Michelle Batte, 2 Jan 2008. Map from TOPOZONE modified for use here.
