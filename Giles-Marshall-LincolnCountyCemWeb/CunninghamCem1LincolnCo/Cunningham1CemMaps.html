CUNNINGHAM CEMETERY, (Chenault Ford Rd. East of Fayetteville, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

 This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Cunningham Cem #1 as N 35.14887 X W -86.51791.

 Location: From Fayetteville go east on Hwy 64 about 4 miles and turn left on Industrial Blvd. Follow that for about a mile after the road bends back west from south go about 300 yards and the cemetery is on your left (south)  back in a field under a clump of trees near an old extinct barn lot. You can see the grove of trees from the road aka Industrial Blvd.. The cemetery is on the west end of that grove of trees.
