EZELL/ABEL/MENAFEE CEMETERY, near Ardmore, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE.

One of two standing memorials in this cemetery. The red arrow points it out.

Martha Corpier.

CORPIER, Martha, daughter of Abel & Sarah Ezell, 15 May 1823 - 15 Dec 1862. "A good wife, an affectionate mother, a loving sister." Stone inscription error spelled Ezell as "EZLL"
This was the only stone remaining standing, but I stood up and packed another of them while there.
