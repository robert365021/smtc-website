EZELL/ABEL/MENAFEE CEMETERY, near Ardmore, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE.

The fallen memorial of Mary J. Jones, in spite of its heavy concrete base.

JONES, Mary J. Consort of M. S. Jones, departed this life 29 April 1840 Aged 27 years, 7 months & 9 days
This stone has been pushed over by cattle in spite of its concrete base.
