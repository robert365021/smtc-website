EZELL/ABEL/MENAFEE CEMETERY, near Ardmore, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE.

The broken & fallen memorial of Sarah Ann Ezell. The base is in the bottom of the photo. Epoxy glue is needed to restore this stone, but also removal from the company of cattle to keep it standing.

EZELL, Sarah Ann, 25 Feb 1828 - 7 Jan 1870. "Sister, daughter of Abel & Sarah Ezell." (d/o Abel & Sarah Palmer Ezell.)
