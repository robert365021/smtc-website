EZELL/ABEL/MENAFEE CEMETERY, near Ardmore, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE.

The fallen stone next from the left. The top with the inscription lies between the two large bases. The William S. Ezell memorial is in the bottom of the photo and his wife's memorial lies between the two bases. Obviously both are tumbled by the rubbing up against them that livestock do.

The fallen top of N. A. Ezell wife of William S. Ezell, Its base is to our right, not the base you see here.

EZELL, N.A. (Nancy Ann Kerr), Wife of W.S. Ezell, 4 Dec 1824 - 3 Feb 1902. (wife of William S. Ezell; daughter of Henry & Mary Ford Kerr.))
