EZELL/ABEL/MENAFEE CEMETERY, near Ardmore, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE.

The big mid-section of the memorial to Abel & Sarah Ezell the original progenitors in this Ezell Cemetery. We are actually looking at Abel's inscription on the west side. 

The fallen memorial for Abel & Sarah Ezell.


EZELL, Sarah, wife of Abel Ezell, 15 Oct 1786 - 12 Dec 1868. "In memory of Mrs SARAH EZELL, wife of ABEL EZELL, born Oct 15th 1786 and Departed this life Decmy 12th 1868."
