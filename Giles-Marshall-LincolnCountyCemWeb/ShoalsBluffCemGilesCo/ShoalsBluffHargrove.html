 HARGROVE, Charles Brandon, 9 Sep 1980 - 20 Nov 2009. U. Marker: "Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home." 5128
OBITUARY: WKSR Radio, November 22, 2009.

Charles Brandon Hargrove, age 29 of Minor Hill, passed away Friday at Hillside Hospital.Visitation will be from 4 - 8pm today (Sunday) at Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home.
Funeral services will be at 1pm tomorrow (Monday) in the chapel of Bennett-May-Giles County Funeral Home with Brother Mark Johnson officiating. Burial will follow in the Shoal Bluff Cemetery in Minor Hill.
He is survived by his wife Brandy Penley Hargrove, daughters Chelsey Brooke and Cadence Nichole Hargrove, parents Marcella Hayes and Charles Randal Hargrove, sisters Katrina and Britney Hargrove, brother Samuel Hobock, grandparents Katherine Benefield, Jimmy and Pat Hayes, and mother-in-law Ginger Colwell.
