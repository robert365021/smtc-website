HARWELL, (William Fielder) CEMETERY, Donahue Road, Left then across field, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This This map shows the GPS Coordinates of the Harwell (William Fielder) Cemetery as GPS: N 35.13613 X W -87.08082 in Garner Hollow. Road to this house no longer marked as the Cemetery road in spite of what maps indicates.

Go south on Hwy 31 from Pulaski on Mill St./Hwy 166. Continue south passing the Airfield then the Rock Crusher on the right. Just after the rock Crusher turn right on Donahue Rd and take the first left (south) on what is mapped as Story Lane or Fry Lane or it seems to continue as Donahue Road. This road immediately bends back westward. After about 2/3 a mile the road forks to Garner Hollow Road (left) on the south fork, but turn right on the right fork to continue on Donahue Road as shown on the above map. After about 6 tenths of a mile take the road left which two serves two homes. When confronted with a fork take the right driveway and go to the A-Frame home. With permission cross the field to the northwest of the home to the cemetery which sits just back inside the tree line under a chain link fence. This  is only about 300 yards across the field from the home as shown in the next map. The home and (I think) the land also is today owned by the Hopper family.

This map shows the location of the cemetery across the field from the home. Please do not disturb anything private while here. This cemetery is threatened by Privet Hedge and will be impassable in a few years if this (invasive, rain loving) stuff is allowed to grow unchecked.

View looking out from the cemetery & across the field back toward the home. The photographer is shooting in a southeasterly direction. The gate can be seen in the photo.
