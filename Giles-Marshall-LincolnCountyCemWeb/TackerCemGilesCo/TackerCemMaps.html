TACKER CEMETERY, (Hwy 31) GILES COUNTY TN

This map shows the GPS coordinates that I have calculated for the Tacker Cemetery
11 miles north of Pulaski on left side of Hwy 31, in back of Blackburn place.
Location: Take Highway 31 north out of Pulaski and go about 11 miles and look left onto the old Blackburn farm. 
This Aerial Map shows the clump of trees where the cemetery is located behind the barn on the old Blackburn place. It is my understanding that the owners here are fussy about permission. So far he has managed to elude me.

This aerial view shows the clump of trees where the cemetery can be found behind the private shed on the right.
