CHAPMAN CEMETERY, (Little Dry Creek Rd, s. of Campbellsville) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

 The GPS coordinates for the Chapman Cemetery are shown on the above map. 
Location: Highway 64 West; right into Campbellsville Road. Left on Big Dry Creek Road; go about 1 mile and take the first road to the left. At top of hill, turn right; cemetery is on the right. Alternate instructions is to turn south off of Hwy 166 onto Little Dry Creek Road and go about 2 1/4 miles and the cemetery can be seen under the trees mentioned above. 

In this aerial view we see the Chapman Cemetery under an old Magnolia & Evergreen Tree that shelters many of the graves from the hot summer sun. It is pointed out above with a yellow pointer.
