DOBBINS CEMETERY, (south east Lawrence Co.), LAWRENCE COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Dobbins Cemetery, near Appleton Community. GPS as calculated by C. Wayne Austin is: N 35.02574 W 87.22642

Go down Hwy 43 a few miles to Hwy 98 Rabbit Trail Road and turn left (east). Follow that road many miles to Hood Hollow aka Gamble Road aka Appleton road and turn left east. Follow that for about 6 miles and after passing thru Appleton the road bends back north (left) and the Dobbins Cemetery is beside the road on the right.
