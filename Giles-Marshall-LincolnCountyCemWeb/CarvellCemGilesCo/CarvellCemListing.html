This cemetery has a Barb Wire fence surrounding it, but is grown up in tall weeds. The small stones were mostly in the south-end area of the cemetery. A few others in the north section were hard to spot due to the tall weeds. It was the usual weed whacking task to clear enough area to make the memorial photographs show up.
