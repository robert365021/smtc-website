STONE CEMETERY, Union Hollow Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Stone Cemetery as N 35.28813 X W -86.57880. This is plotted in the center of the cemetery.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 several miles. Cross Union Branch Creek and turn left on Cold Springs Rd. Follow that a couple miles and turn right into Union Hollow Road. Go less than one mile and a field road turns into the cemetery road and crosses Union Branch on your right. The cemetery is on your left (after turning off Union Hollow Road) up a road improved for access to this cemetery. The Yell family has built a beautiful stone entrance and is taking an interest in reviving this cemetery for future burials. 

In this image I have drawn a yellow border which approximates the boundaries of the cemetery.
