BILLINGTON CEMETERY, (Caney Springs, Hwy 99) MARSHALL COUNTY TN

This map shows the GPS coordinates needed to find this cemetery as 35.6136 N X 86.7916 W

From Lewisburg go several miles north on Verona-Caney Springs Road and turn left on Highway 99 and go 2 miles and the cemetery is on the south side of the road on Mr. Frank Osteen's place.
Since I have not visited this cemetery, the map may be off by as much as 500 yards.
