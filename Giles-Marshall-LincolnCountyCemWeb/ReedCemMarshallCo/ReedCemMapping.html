REED CEMETERY, Verona Road Lewisburg, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the Whitesell Cemetery GPS location at Lat. 35.511692, Long -86.788486

 Location: In Lewisburg take N. Ellington Parkway to Hwy 31/11 Nashville Highway and turn right. Immediately turn back left or north on Verona Road and the cemetery is a few hundred yards up the road on your right.
Do not go to this place if your intentions are not honorable. Respect must be shown at all times for those gone before us by not disturbing their monuments & graves.
