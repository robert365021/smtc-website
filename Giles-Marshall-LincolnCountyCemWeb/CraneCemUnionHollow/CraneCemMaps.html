CRANE CEMETERY, Union Hollow Rd, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Crane Cemetery as N 35.29861 X W -86.56270.

 Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 several miles to Cold Spring Road and turn left or northwest. Travel that to Union Hollow Road and turn right and go about 2.5 miles to the top of the hill at a residence/farm operation on the right. The cemetery is in the field next to a home & barns.

I have applied a yellow highlighter to outline the fenced perimeter of this cemetery where you will find stones. I do not have any idea if this cemetery has a separate lot line.
