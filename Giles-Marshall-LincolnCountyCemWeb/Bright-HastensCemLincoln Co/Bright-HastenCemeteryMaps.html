BRIGHT-HASTINGS CEMETERY, John Campbell Rd, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Bright-Hastens Cemetery as N 35.12074 X W -86.69732.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 and turn south (left) on Old Elkton Pike (273). Follow that to Old Molino Road and turn left and follow that to Luttrell Rd and turn right. After that road takes a northward bend go another 4 tenths of a mile and turn left (east). That road dead ends on John Campbell Road and turn left (south) there and after the road bends back west go another 1/2 mile to the bend in the road back south and you will see large farm silos on the right. the cemetery is in a field to the west of those silos under a giant irrigation system. Follow the 3rd irrigation machine tire track from the road to the cemetery to avoid disturbing the crops. When wheat is growing in the winter follow the tracks to the cemetery and there will be no brush in the cemetery. Since the machines indivertibly fertilize the cemetery in the summer the weeds grow giant and it will be impossible to penetrate those or even to cut them down to view the cemetery, so yes this is presently a winter time visit. Corn is grown in the summer and is very tall making it hard even to find the cemetery.
I have attempted to outline the cemetery with a yellow highlighter in the above map. Take this as only an estimation of the area.
