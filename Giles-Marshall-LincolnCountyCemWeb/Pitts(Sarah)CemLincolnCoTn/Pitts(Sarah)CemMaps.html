PITTS (Sarah) CEMETERY, (Mimosa Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Baggerly Cemetery as N 35.03457 X W 86.70119.

 Location: From Fayetteville go out Mimosa Rd several miles and when the road bends back westward go another 2 miles and the cemetery is on the right upon a slope in a pasture among thin trees about 60 yards from the Road. There is no gate here, so find the access to this field.

This aerial map shows the cemetery on the right after one passes the thick woods on the right. The cemetery is behind a failing fence. There are only two stones here but several fieldstones are located here.
