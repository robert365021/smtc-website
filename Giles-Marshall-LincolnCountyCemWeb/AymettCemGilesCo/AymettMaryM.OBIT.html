AYMETT CEMETERY (Hwy 64 East, North side of Road, past Leatherman Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
 

AYMETT, Mary M. Harwell 27 Sep 1840 - 20 Sep 1920 (w/o Henry Clay Aymett; d/o Wesley and Melvina Caruthers Harwell)

Obituary: The Pulaski Citizen 23 Sep 1920
Mrs. H. C. Aymett, aged about 84 years, died Monday at her home in the Pisgah locality of the infirmities of age. Her burial was at the Aymett graveyard on John M. Aymett's farm Tuesday, the Rev. W. M. Cook conducting funeral services. Mrs. Aymett was an estimable Christian woman whose husband died many years ago. She is survived by two sons, Dr. R. E. Aymett and Wesley Aymett, and two half-sisters, Mrs. John Keltner and Miss Mat Harwell and a half-brother, Thomas Harwell.
