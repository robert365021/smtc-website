AYMETT, Thomas White (Veno) 15 Oct 1932 29 Oct 2002 (h/o Lois Harwell Aymett)(s/o Fred Fields and Katie Butcher Aymett)

Obituary: Citizen Press 31 Oct 2002

Mr. Thomas W. (Veno) Aymett, 70, of Puaski died Oct. 29, 2002, at the Gulf Coast Medical Center in Panama City, Fla. Mr. Aymett was born Oct. 15, 1932, in Giles County and was the son of the late Fred Fields Aymett. He was retired from Brindley Construction Company and a veteran of the Korean Conflict. Funeral services will be held at 2 p.m., Friday, Nov. 1, at Carr and Erwin Funeral Home. Burial will be in the Aymett Family Cemetery. Visitation will be 5-9 p.m., tonight (Thursday) at the funeral home. Memorials may be made to First United Methodist Church. Survivors include his mother, Katie Aymett of Pulaski; wife Lois Aymett of Pulaski; son Fred Harwell Aymett of Pulaski; daughter Dr. Angela Aymett Tucker of Pulaski; brother Carl Aymett of Nashville; sisters, Carolyn A. Merrill and Betty Murray, both of Pulaski; and three grandchildren.
