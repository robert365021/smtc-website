PIONEER CEMETERY, Hwy 55, MOORE COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Old Pioneer Cemetery GPS location as N 35.29756, W -86.36413 upon a hill above the Road and Water Treatment Plant.
Location: From Highway 55 in Downtown Lynchburg go northeast on Hwy 55 (main Road out of town) about a mile and when you almost get to the water treatment plant and see the historical marker for the cemetery on your right the cemetery is past the first hedge row about 100 yards upon the slope on your right but under a stand of trees. You will need to cross the hedgerow lining the road. Not sure what kind of fence is there nor do I know who owns the surrounding property. Check along Good Branch Road for permission to cross the land.
