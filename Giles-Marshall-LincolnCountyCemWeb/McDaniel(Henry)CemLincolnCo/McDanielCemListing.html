McDANIEL, Henry, 12 Feb 1833 - 18 Jun 1888. 8234/8235/Henry McDaniel's inscribed grave cover:8236/8238/footstone H.Mc.8240/8241
McDANIEL, Angeline, 17 Aug 1832 - 1 Aug 1916. 8233/8235/8238/footstone A.M.:8237/8239

Both Graves are enclosed within an Iron Fence that has been compromised by the tree limbs falling on it. There are three large trees in position to fall on these grave stones sometimes in the near future. A cemetery restorer should go there, removed the stones and cut the trees. Then return the stones to their exact places. If not we will go here in the future and only find broken stones.
 

Photographed on a sunny 19 Mar 2013. The inscriptions were updated with exceptions found or known by C. Wayne Austin. This publication culminated from various sources. Listed on page 334 of the book Lincoln County County Cemeteries by Tim & Helen Marsh abt 1978. Added here 10 Sep 2013.
