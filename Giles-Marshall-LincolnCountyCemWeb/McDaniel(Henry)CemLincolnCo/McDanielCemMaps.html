McDANIEL CEMETERY (McAnally Rd, S. of Kirkland) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the McDaniel Family Cemetery. 

 Location: Go south on Hwy 231 from Fayetteville and turn right on W. Lincoln Rd and when that terminates turn right and go to McAnally Rd. Just before this road bend north (right) stop at the house there for permission to get back to the cemetery in the fence row on the back side of the property about 300 yards. You can only walk to this place. From the barn or house walk northwest to the fence road and follow along that until you locate the gravestones. The owners are cordial but will question your purpose.

This aerial map shows the cemetery in the fence row under a huge oak tree that will some day create problems as it dies when it starts dropping its limbs on the tombstones.
