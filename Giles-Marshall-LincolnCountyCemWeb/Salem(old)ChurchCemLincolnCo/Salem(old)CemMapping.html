SALEM (old) CHURCH CEMETERY, Red Oak Road, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Old Salem Church Cemetery as about N 35.21279 X W -86.75045. This is an estimate only and may contain as much as 200 yards error or about 185 meters error.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64w to Boonshill and turn north and at the dead end turn left on Red Oak Road. Go past the Boonshill Church & Cemetery on your left when you are just back from the Boonshill black CP Church building abandoned several years ago. The cemetery is southeast of there just south of a small pond about 400 yards from the Red Oak Road . This was located on Phillip Thomason's farm in (1990), just to the south of a small pond on the hill, but to the south-west of
his house.
