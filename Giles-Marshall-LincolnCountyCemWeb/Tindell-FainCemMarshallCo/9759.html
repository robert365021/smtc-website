This depiction of the location of the stones of Martha & James Old is probably accurate but may be switched up.

The broken Martha Old memorial.

We are looking at the James Old memorial. On the back side is this memorial lies the broken parts of the memorial of his wife Martha Old along with their footstones. His misplaced stone is leaning against her memorial and is not standing in its base.
