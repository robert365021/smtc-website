TINDELL-FAIN CEMETERY, HWY 431, MARSHALL COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This is a distant view of the cemetery. We are looking westward from near the fence. This photo was cropped to eliminate some of the open grass land between us and the cemetery. Some of the stones are not visible because of the tall grasses.
This view captures the complete cemetery with its scattered stones. As Mr Fred Hawkins alluded to this cemetery is almost two separate cemeteries with the Hardison & Tindell memorials on our left and the Stewart & Fain stones on our right further back and out.
