Old Mt Hermon Cemetery

MOUNT HERMON (old) CEMETERY (Hart Road) BEDFORD COUNTY, TENNESSEE

From the above map we are given the Old Mt. Hermon Cemetery GPS location.

Location: From Shelbyville go south on Hwy 231 several miles to the Hawthorne Hill Road and turn left. Follow that road a couple of miles and it will terminate into Mt Hermon Road. Turn left (east) on Mt Hermon Road and when Hart Road junctions to the left take that and the old cemetery will be there on your left. The new cemetery is a hundred yards further and is on the right.
This Aerial Map from MS Bing shows the Mt Hermon Community including the two cemeteries. Here we see labeled in yellow text the Old and New Cemeteries.
