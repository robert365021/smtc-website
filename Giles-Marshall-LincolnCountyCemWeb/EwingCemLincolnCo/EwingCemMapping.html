EWING CEMETERY, Kelly Creek Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Ewing Cemetery as N 35.05081 X W -86.79712

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 110 (Ardmore Hwy) past Taft to Blanche Rd and turn north (left). Go 3 tenths of a mile and turn left (west) on McLemore Rd Follow that until it terminates into Kelly Creek Rd. Follow Kelly Creek Rd past Holland Rd and Philpot Rd to the first house past Philpot Rd far up a slope. The cemetery is behind this house which is of log construction. Permission is required to use the private facilities to gain access to the cemetery. You must use their driveway and their private assets are around you while in the cemetery. Please show respect for their privacy here. The cemetery is slightly more to the west than the above road map shows as that was mapped incorrectly.
