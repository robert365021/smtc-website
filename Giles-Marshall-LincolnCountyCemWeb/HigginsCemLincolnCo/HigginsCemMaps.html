HIGGINS CEMETERY (Hwy 231 N.) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE

From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Higgins Cemetery.
Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 about 3 miles and look left at a rock wall and first notice the antebullem home so lovingly kept up. The cemetery is down next to the highway beside the rock wall with the Kennedy monument above the rock wall.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly marked with a red check in front of what is probably the original home place of the Higgins famly.
