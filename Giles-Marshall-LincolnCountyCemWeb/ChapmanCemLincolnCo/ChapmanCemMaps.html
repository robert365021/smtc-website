CHAPMAN CEMETERY, Fishing Ford Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Chapman Cemetery as N 35.28483 X W -86.65433. USGS  & Google maps has this cemetery wrongly mapped on the east side of South Fishing Ford Rd.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 431 and just the other side of Howell turn left on S. Fishing Ford Road. Follow that for about 5 miles and look left (west) into the thicket for the Chapman Cemetery sign. The entrance is an old gate that can still be opened.

This above map is an attempt to outline in yellow the cemetery which approximates its size. There is an open area on the north section and a fallen rock wall on the south portion of the cemetery.
