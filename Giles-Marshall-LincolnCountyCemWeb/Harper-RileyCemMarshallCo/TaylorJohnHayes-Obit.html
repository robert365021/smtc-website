John Hayes Taylor - Birth: Sep. 24, 1932, Anes, Marshall Co., Tenn. - Death: Feb. 24, 2008, Lewisburg, Marshall Co., Tenn.

Wednesday, February 27, 2008 - Marshall County Tribune  John Hayes Taylor

Mr. John Hayes Taylor, 75, of Anes Station, died Sunday, Feb. 24, 2008, at NHC Oakwood. Mr. Taylor was a native of Maury County. He was retired from Century Cable TV of Wise, Va., served in the Army during the Korean conflict, and was a member of the Methodist Church.
He was preceded in death by his parents, Grady A. and Addie Lunn Taylor; wife, Norma Jean Wiley Taylor; son, John Dorman Taylor; four brothers, Ivy, Horace, and Harold Taylor and Billy Vanhooser.
He is survived by his daughter-in-law, Pat Taylor of Anes Station; sister, Louise Whitsett of Lewisburg; brothers, Bill Taylor of Columbia, Charles Vanhooser of Lewisburg, and Jack Vanhooser of Nashville; and grandchildren, Derek (Holly) Taylor of Lewisburg, and Seth (Jennifer) Taylor of Chillicotha, Ohio.
The family will be at London Funeral Home Wednesday, Feb. 27, until the funeral services at 1 p.m. Burial will be in the Harper-Taylor Cemetery. by: Ray Harris

Burial: Harper-Riley-Taylor Cemetery, Marshall Co., Tenn.
