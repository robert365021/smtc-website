TAYLOR-HARPER CEMETERY, Caney Springs, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

LYNCH, James Larry "Pee Wee", Feb 4, 1948 - Apr 26, 2012

James Larry "Pee Wee" Lynch, age 64 of Hillsboro, Tennessee died Thursday
April 26, 2012 in Decherd, Tennessee. Mr. Lynch was a native of Marshall County, Tennessee and son of the late James Reeves "J.R." and Alta B. Haynes Lynch. He was employed with Holiday Express Motel in maintenance.
In addition to his parents, he was preceded in death by a sister, Jean Blalock. He is survived by his wife, Sarah Estella Whitsett Lynch of Hillsboro, daughter, Carrie Addie Thomas of Manchester, son, Jonathan Dwayne Thomas of Lewisburg, brother, Jerry Lynch of Belfast, sister, Linda Cordum of Lewisburg, and three grandchildren.
