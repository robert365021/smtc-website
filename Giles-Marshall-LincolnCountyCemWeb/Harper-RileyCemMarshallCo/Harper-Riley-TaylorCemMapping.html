HARPER-TAYLOR-RILEY CEMETERY, Caney Springs, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Harper-Taylor-Riley Cemetery of Marshall County, Tennessee. My GPS marker is N 35.64465 X W -86.76545.
Location: From the Lewisburg go north on Hwy 272 past the Duck River and turn left on Hwy 99 and back right (north) again on Lumms store road. Follow that for more than 2 miles and turn right (east) on Harper Cemetery Road where the cemetery is less than 1/2 mile on the left inside a chain link fence.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly. The above yellow outline is a rough area approximating the cemetery space and bears no legal resemblance to the property lines of this cemetery.
