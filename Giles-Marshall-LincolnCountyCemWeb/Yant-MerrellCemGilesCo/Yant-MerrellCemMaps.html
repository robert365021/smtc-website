YANT-MERRELL CEMETERY (Youngs Chapel Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Yant-Merrell Cemetery as N 35.169322 X W -86.86356. 

Location: From I-65 Exit 14 at Highway 64 go west toward Pulaski to Bunker Hill Rd and turn left (south). Continue on that until you go under I-65 and turn left (east) onto Young Chapel Rd. Follow that for something over a mile and the Young Chapel Church & Cemetery is on your right. Next go another tenth of a mile and turn right into the road that may be marked Minitra Road r it may look like a private drive. With permission go by the barns to a gate between the last two barns and thru that into the cattle pasture land behind the last barn. This barn or shed furtherest west of the other barns. The cemetery is under an old dying Cedar tree with a wagon wheel hanging from it. Otherwise the cemetery is west of the back of that last long north/south length shed. The center of the cemetery being about 25 feet west of the gate also.

This aerial map shows the cemetery behind the barn or equipment building. This road may not be public now and may not be marked as Minitra Rd as the road that spans from here southwest is called Minatra Rd.
