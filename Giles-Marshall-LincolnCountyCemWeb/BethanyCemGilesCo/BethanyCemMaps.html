BETHANY CEMETERY, (Newman Rd, just E. of I-65, but no exit) - GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Bethany Cemetery GPS location. 

Location: Take Highway 31 South from Pulaski and go more than 14 miles and turn left on Newman Road. After you go under Interstate 65 the cemetery is on your right in the bend of the road behind a rock wall and gated fence.
This Aerial Map shows the area where the cemetery resides next the Newman Road. This is a very old cemetery and contains many fragile stones easily damaged by overzealous people. Be careful around these soft monuments if you have business here. Touch them only if you have the proper experience with brittle old monuments.
