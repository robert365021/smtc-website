MT. CARMEL CEMETERY, Lewisburg, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the Mt Carmel Cemetery GPS location as N 35.43600 X W -86.80275.

Location: From Hwy 11 inside Lewisburg go west on Fox land to Barron Rod and turn north (right). Follow that to the long Mt Carmel Cemetery Road and turn right.

This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly under a few trees surrounded by my yellow graphic line. The entrance is from the upper left where the road comes in.
