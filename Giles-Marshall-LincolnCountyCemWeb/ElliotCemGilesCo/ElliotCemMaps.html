ELLIOTT CEMETERY (Ardmore), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Elliot Cemetery GPS location. This is across (east of) from Exit 1 of I-65 to Drivers Road.

Location: Going South on I-65 take Exit 1 and turn left on Hwy 31. After crossing over I-65 turn left on Drivers Lane and go 3 tenths of a mile to cemetery on hill on right overlooking I-65.
Here we see the various roads needed to get to this cemetery and also the Odd Fellows Cemetery (African American) Some maps label the Odd Fellows Cemetery as Elliot Cemetery #2. No one we know in the black community agrees with this. There is an Odd Fellows Hall Community in Giles County and there is a Black Odd Fellows Hall Cemetery there with that name.
