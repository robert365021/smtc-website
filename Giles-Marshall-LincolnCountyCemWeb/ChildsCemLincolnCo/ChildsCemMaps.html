CHILDS CEMETERY, Sulfur Springs Rd, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Childs Cemetery as N 35.22520 X W -86.66852. This is far back in a field next to and across a fence row.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on hwy 64 and turn right on Bradford Rd crossing Hughey Road and eventually Haysland Rd. Go a total of about 2 & 7/10 miles to the Sulfur Springs Cemetery on your right. Pass on by that another 150 yards and with permission turn right into the middle of three residential driveways. This will be the one that goes by the right (east) side of the pond. This will be the northern most home far back down the road. They keep horses and dogs so you will need instructions and safety knowledge. Be prepared for a 400 yard walk across the pasture land.

This is a rough outline in yellow also showing a fence row which cannot be damaged because of livestock needing its security. Carry a small aluminum ladder with you to get over the fence.
