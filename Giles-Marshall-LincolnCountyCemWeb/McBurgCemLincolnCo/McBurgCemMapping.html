McBURG CHURCH OF CHRIST & CEMETERY, Old Highway 64, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the McBurg Church of Christ Cemetery as N 35.18730 X W -86.81195.

 Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 to McBurg and turn left (south) on the old Hwy 64.Follow that for a mile or so and look to your right for the road back to the church and cemetery which is on the right just before you get to Chicken Creek Road which goes north.
