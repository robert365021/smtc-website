BEECH HILL CEMETERY (Beech Hill), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Beech Hill Cemetery GPS location. It is across the road from the extinct Beech Hill High School, 1917 - 1978. A monument is in place to remind us of that.

Location: Take Highway 31 Alternate northeast out of Pulaski for about 8 miles and turn right at Hopkins Rd which almost immediately merges into Blackburn Road at Brick Church. Turn south on Blackburn Road. At the Blackburn Road and Clock Creek Road merge right and go about 3 more miles. When the Blackburn Road terminates at Beech Hill Road (aka Old Hwy 15) turn left and after another 2/3 of a mile the Beech Hill Church of Christ will be on your right. The entrance up the hill to the cemetery will be on your left.
This Aerial Map shows the plot where the cemetery resides next to Interstate 65. Beech Hill/Bradshaw/Centerpoint Road goes under Interstate Highway 65 nearby. The cemetery access road is across the road in front of the Beech Hill Church of Christ. Be prepared for your vehicle to climb a steep road up to the cemetery. The Church of Christ and old school site is in the valley of the Little Bradshaw Creek.
