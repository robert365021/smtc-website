JONES CEMETERY, Beech Hill Road near Friendship, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the A.H. Jones Family Cemetery as N 35.22486 X W 86.92130.

Location: Go east out old Hwy 15 aka Beech Hill Rd several miles to the Friendship Church Community. When you see a large Trailer trailer with a Martin Industries sign you have just passed this little cemetery. TAs stated the cemetery is just back from that a couple of hundred yards and about 30 yards from the road on the left (north) under and inside a clump of hackberry trees is the cemetery. The cemetery is being swallowed up by the clumping of several tree trunks.

The Tree out in the field has the graves under and inside the trunk and the Martin Trailer that marks this spot today is behind the trees in the right of this photo.
