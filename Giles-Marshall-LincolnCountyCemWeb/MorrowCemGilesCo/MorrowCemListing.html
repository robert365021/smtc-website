 Judy King Sanders (the source author) and visitor of this cemetery in 1996 found 5 tombstones, but she believes that at one time, there were more. Further she says the cemetery is not in good condition. It is an open cemetery and cows have been allowed to roam over it. Some of the tombstones were lying on the ground. The inscriptions on the tombstones were hard to read. The last person's tombstone she found was buried over 100 years ago. This publication was added to Familytreemaker by a Morrow researcher: Barbara Mandersheid. Another source is the Giles County Cemetery Historical Society book.  The GSC book say also that Elizabeth Hickman and husband J.B. Hickman (or Morrow) is interred here without headstones. They offered no listings for any other burials. P 374, Morrow Cemetery. Cemetery & Tombstone Photos by John Colvett who visited here recently. Revised to add John's photos in May 2015
