ROGERS CEMETERY (Old Huntsville Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Rogers Family Cemetery as N 35.02326 X W -86.59770.
Location: From Fayetteville go south on Hwy 231 to W. Lincoln Rd and turn west for a mile and then turn south and then into the parking lot of the auto salvage yard. Stop for permission to visit the cemetery, otherwise it is behind a locked gate. In dry weather it can be driven along the southern route leading thru the salvage yard to the clump of trees where the cemetery resides.
This Aerial Map shows, marked with a yellow outline the cemetery among the auto salvage yard operation.
