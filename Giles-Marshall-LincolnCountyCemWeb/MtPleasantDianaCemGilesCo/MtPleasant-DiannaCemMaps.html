MT. PLEASANT/DIANA CEMETERY, (Hannah Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This topographical map from Topozone shows the GPS coordinates of the Mt Pleasant-Dianna Cemetery. I believe the GPS coordinates to be accurate.
Location: From I-65 take Exit 22 toward Pulaski and go about a mile. Turn east or left on Dianna Road. Follow Dianna Rd back under I-65 and thru the main part of Dianna and bend back south another mile and the cemetery is on your right. Look for the large overhead sign "Dianna Cemetery" on the right. The cemetery is upon a long high flat hill top area.
This aerial map shows the cemetery just west of Dianna Road between the Rail Road Tracks and Dianna Road marked by Google and a red check.
