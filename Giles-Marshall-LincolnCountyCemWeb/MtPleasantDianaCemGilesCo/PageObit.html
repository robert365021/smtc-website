 PAGE, M.A. (Martha Ann Hopkins, 7 Dec 1819 - 20 Sep 1872, daughter of Francis Hopkins and Temperence "Tempie. Marth Ann Hopkins married Benjamin Nelson (or Nathan) Page on 26 Nov 1837 in Benton County, Alabama. Benjamin N. Page. (hand in scripted fieldstone.) 3762

Biography

Martha Ann Hopkins born 7 Dec 1819 in South Carolina and died 10 Sept 1872 in Diana, Giles County, Tennessee is the daughter of Francis Hopkins and Temperence "Tempie. Marth Ann Hopkins married Benjamin Nelson (or Nathan) Page on 26 Nov 1837 in Benton County, Alabama. Benjamin N Page born 20 Dec 1817 in Georgia and died 19 Aug 1864 in Petersburg, Dinwiddie, Virginia during the Civil War is the son of William Nelson Page and Mary Landers.
