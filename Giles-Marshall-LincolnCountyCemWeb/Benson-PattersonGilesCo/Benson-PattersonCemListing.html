Notes from from Flournoy Rivers about this cemetery in the 1890s.
1 Early Benson married Obedience Foster, dau. of Elizabeth Foster.
2 Presley Ward Phelps was the Brother-in-law of J. B. Morrell of Bryson and uncle of Ward Phelps of Bunker Hill.
3 Martha Phelps was the daughter of Maj. E. Benson and wife of Dr. Phelps.
4 Eleanor P. Patterson was the dau. of Maj. E. Benson and wife of Jno. C. Patterson.
5 Eleanor S. McCallum was dau. of Jno. C. Patterson and wife of L. N. McCallum.
