BENSON-PATTERSON CEMETERY,King Road, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Benson-Patterson Cemetery as GPS N 35.09574 X W -86.89082.

Go south on Hwy 31 from Pulaski and turn east (left) on Bunker Hill Road. Follow that several miles and turn right (south) on Old Stage Coach Road. Old Stage Coach Road will terminate at junction of King & Newman Roads. You will find yourself at a gate with multiple no-trespassing signs present into (old) King Road which evidently has been reverted to private land over time. Your journey probably ends here. The owner sadly has made it known he allows no visitors on this land.

This aerial map shows the cemetery behind a rock wall. I cannot tell here if there are invasive trees or brush present in this cemetery. If you get permission please respect the crop being grown here and do not cross into the cemetery when crops are green and subject to being trampled upon.

From this Google view we see the cemetery in the distance looking west out from I-65. The dark spot in the field is probably the rock wall. This is about 600 yards from I-65.
