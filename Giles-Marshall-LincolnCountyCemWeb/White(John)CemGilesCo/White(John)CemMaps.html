WHITE (John) CEMETERY, Bledsoe Road, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This topographical map from ACME Mapper 2.1 shows the GPS coordinates of the John White Cemetery. The GPS coordinates are N 35.22920 X W -87.01645.
From the inside of Pulaski go north on Hwy 31 1 mile to Bledsoe Road (Old Pigeon Roost Rd). Turn right when the Johnson Road/Bledsoe Rd branch comes up bear right onto the Bledsoe Road. After 1/2 mile turn right into a long private drive (with permission) and find the cemetery inside a rock wall enclosure behind the house about 100 yards up the slope. This was or still is the Floyd Christopher place.
