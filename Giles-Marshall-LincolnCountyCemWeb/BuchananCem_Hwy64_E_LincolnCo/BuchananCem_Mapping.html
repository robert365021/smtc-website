BUCHANAN CEMETERY, Hwy 64 East, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Buchanan Cemetery as N 35.15380 X W -86.49726.

Location: From Fayetteville at the bypass & Hwy 64 go east on Hwy 64 just over 4 miles and look to your left (north) at a clump of trees about 250 yards from the highway. Its an easy walk otherwise, but the the cemetery is fenced.
