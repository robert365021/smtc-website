BUCHANAN CEMETERY Hwy 64 East, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
 

WILLIAMS, Jennie Simmons, Jul, 1878 - Apr 27, 1907, Age not given

Virginia "Jennie" Simmons was the daughter of J. L. Simmons, a farmer, of Moore County, TN. Jennie's parents had another daughter documented on the 1880 census.
Jennie was married to George P. Williams in Moore County 6 Aug 1893. The 1900 Lincoln County Census documented she and George had one deceased child and one living child, Edna.
