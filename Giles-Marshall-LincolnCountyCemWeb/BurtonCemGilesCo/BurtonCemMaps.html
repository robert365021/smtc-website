BURTON CEMETERY, (Hwy 31A North side) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
 
This map shows the Burton cemetery location GPS coordinates commonly known as the Latitude and Longitude.
Location: From Pulaski Tennessee take Highway 31A out northeast about 9 miles and turn left on the old Turnpike Road (labeled as the Hopkins Road on the above map). The cemetery is near the first house on right on the east (or your right) side of the house.

This aerial view shows the top of the house and a tree lined area adjacent to the cemetery. Actually the cemetery is just outside that tree lined area to the northeast.
