This is a small family cemetery located in the field beside the yard of the family living at the junction of Hwy 31 Alt and the old Turnpike (Hopkins) Road. It is well cared for being fenced and maintained.

Publication based on a visit and photography session which took place 16 Jun 2008 by Wayne Austin. Added here by W.A. 12 May 2010.

Parenthetical Information from Julia B. Gregory, 6 March 1985. published on page 65 in the book Giles County Tennessee Cemeteries, by the Giles County Historical Society.
