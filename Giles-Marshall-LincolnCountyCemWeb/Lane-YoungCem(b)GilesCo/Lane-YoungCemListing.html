LANE-YOUNG CEMETERY, (Wheelerton), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
(African American)
I could not find this cemetery named anywhere so gave it the name above after the two known military men who are buried here. It is just barely inside the Giles County line from Lincoln County.
