LANE-YOUNG CEMETERY, (Wheelerton), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Lane-Young Cemetery marked as Cem above.

From Exit 6 on I-65 go east toward Fayetteville on Dellrose Rd. about three miles and turn south on Wheelerton Rd. Go about 3 tenths of a mile and look left up the slope behind a house and the cemetery is on the ridge at the top of the slope. It seems to be maintained as part of someone's back yard but is north and slightly east from the house. It is reported for the other Young Cemetery shown southeast on the above map (1/2 mile) reports no formal headstones.

This aerial map shows the cemetery starting at the southeast corner of a picket fence.
