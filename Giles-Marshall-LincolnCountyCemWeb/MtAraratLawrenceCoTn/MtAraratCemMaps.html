MOUNT ARARAT CEMETERY, LAWRENCE COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Mt Ararat Cemetery. This is north of Lawrenceburg. GPS: N 35.28636 X W 87.33500. This is actually the white section. The black section is northwest of this - north of Beasley Rd & west side of the Mt Ararat Road whereas this is the east side of the road and if coming up Mt Ararat the first you will come to.

Location: From Lawrenceburg go north on Liberty Ave and that becomes Mt Ararat Road. Just about 2 miles out you will see the white section on the right of the road. Go a little further and the black section is on the west and north of Beasley Road.
