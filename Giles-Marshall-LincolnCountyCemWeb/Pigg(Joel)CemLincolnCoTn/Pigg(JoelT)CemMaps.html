PIGG CEMETERY (Haysland Road) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Joel T. Pigg Family Cemetery at N 35.25526 X W -86.67306.

 Location: From Fayetteville go west on Highway 64 and turn north (right) on Boonshill to Petersburg Rd.. Follow that for several miles to Blakeville. Veer right into Haysland Rd (or fail to turn left) and go about a half mile and with permission turn right into the drive of the home that has the cemetery on the left side of the driveway. Cemetery about 1/2 way down the driveway to the home. If the Haysland Road bends back left you have passed by the cemetery. A little further on off the road on the right is the Buchanan-Pigg Cemetery.
