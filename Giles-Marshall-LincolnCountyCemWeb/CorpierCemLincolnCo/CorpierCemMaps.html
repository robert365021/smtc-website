CORPIER CEMETERY, (SE of Junction of Henry Bayless & Elliott Roads) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Corpier Cemetery.
Location: From Ardmore to Fayetteville Highway go north on Henry Bayless Road to the junction of Elliott Road and turn right. The field road to the cemetery is just before the first house about 200 yards after you turn right.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly in a clump of trees behind a private home. There is a pond west of the cemetery.
