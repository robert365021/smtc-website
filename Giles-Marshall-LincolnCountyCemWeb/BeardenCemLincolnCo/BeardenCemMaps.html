BEARDEN CEMETERY, (Hwy 110), LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE

From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Bearden Cemetery.
Location: From Fayetteville go southwest on Hwy 110 (Ardmore Highway) about 7 miles. Go past Skinem, Kirkland Chapel Baptist Church & Mary's Grove Community. On your left at the entrance road to the large pond and Farming operation turn in and the cemetery will be on the left about 100 yards down that road behind a house.
This Aerial Map shows, marked with a red check, the cemetery more clearly as in front of a large pond behind a house, but you will not have to go into the driveway for the home even though this is still private property. This is also in front of a large Lawn Grass farming operation.
