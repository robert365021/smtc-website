STELLA CEMETERY (aka Pleasant Hill), Stella, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

 I have a copy of Emma P. Reagan's application where she states Thomas C. Reagin enlisted in Capt Hanna's Co 32 regiment. Later in answer to question 9 "When and Where did Thomas Caleb Reagan enlist in the war between the States, and in what Company and Regiment did he enlist, and how do you know this?"
She answers "In this State and County Oct 1861 and in Capt Hannas Co 32 Tenn Reg and detailed as a blacksmith to another brigade and served as such to the end of the war.

Marilyn Hare
