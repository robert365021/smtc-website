SMITH, Jennie B.(Barnes), 20 Jan 1865 - 23 Aug 1958. "Not dead but sleeping." (d/o Robert T. & Eliza J. Douthitt Barnes; w/o William Turner Smith.) 9552/9571

Athens (AL) Limestone Democrat, August 26, 1958, p. 7

Rites Held Sunday For Mrs. W. T. Smith, 93.
Funeral services were held Sunday afternoon at the Stella Church of Christ for Mrs. W. T. Smith, 93, of Rt. I, Minor Hill, Tenn., who died Saturday. Burial was in the Stella cemetery, E. F. Bullington officiated.

Mrs. Smith is survived by three sons, James R., Nashville; Harry L. and Herbert F. Smith, Elkmont; five daughters. Mrs. Elsie Graves and Mrs. Blanche W. Ellis, Minor Hill, Mrs. Mary Bass, Nashville, Mrs Bessie Hester, Dothan, Mrs. Verna Jackson.

Lester; 28 grandchildren, 56 great-grandchildren, and seven great - great-grandchildren.

Grandsons were pallbearers.
