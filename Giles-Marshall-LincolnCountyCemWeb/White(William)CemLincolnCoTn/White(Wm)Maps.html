WHITE (William) CEMETERY (Stephens Creek Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the William White Family Cemetery. GPS: N 35.18110 X W 86.43610. 

 Location: Cemetery south of mulberry. Take Back Street out of Mulberry which becomes Kelso Mulberry Rd to Cooper Branch Rd and turn left. Go to Lloyd Chapel Rd and turn right. When that dead ends on Stephens Creek Rd turn left and the cemetery is up the slope on the left behind an old barn under the edge of the trees. This cemetery must be visited with the help of the current land owner Dr. Akin who has certain times of the year where the premises is open only to (private) turkey hunters.

This topographical map shows the cemetery just up the slope from the Stephens Creek Rd about 200 yards. Park at the old Barn (which was at one time a store) next to the road where there is a gate and thru the gate and follow along the tree line 150 yards. Cross the tree line at the old  (torn out) gate on the left and proceed up the slope another 100 yards north west veering right to stay in the clearing and then back left, due west, toward the next tree line where the one large obelisk monument can be found. The Cemetery was recently cleared and now is more easily found.
