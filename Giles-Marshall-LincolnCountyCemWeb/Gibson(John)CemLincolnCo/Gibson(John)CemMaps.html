GIBSON (John) CEMETERY, Clayton Lane (far end), LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the John Gibson Cemetery as N 35.21245 X W -86.62043.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 431 and before you get to the Howell community turn left on Clayton Lane. Go to the end of Clayton Lane and turn right (north) and go up that road and on into the private residential road. The cemetery is past a locked gate next to a modern home & family at the crest of the hill. You will need permission and directions as this cemetery is hard to see in the summer time even if you stand next to it, because of having only one standing stone.

This map shows the Clayton Ln you will need to gain access to this cemetery, at the John Crawford Cemetery on your right travel north until that road ends and turn right and follow the residential road to the home on top of the hill and the Cemetery is in the side yard and with the brush covering it.
