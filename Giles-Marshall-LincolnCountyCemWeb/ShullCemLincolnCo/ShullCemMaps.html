SHULL CEMETERY (Off of Hwy 231 N.) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Shull Cemetery.
Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 a two or three miles turn left into a private drive. Travel down the drive 300 yards and the Cemetery is in the southwest corner of the home in a flower bed. Obviously you cannot go here without permission from the home owner.
This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly at the corner of the private residential home.
