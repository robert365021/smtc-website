OLD (surname) CEMETERY, Bledsoe Road, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the John Old Cemetery as N 35.32774 X W -86.58209.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 to Chestnut Ridge and turn left on Hwy 129 (Petersburg Chestnut Ridge Rd. Go about 2 miles and turn north (right) on Bledsoe Rd and go a few hundred yards and bear right passing 3 hundred Dollar Rd on the left and continue on about a half of a mile and the house on the left will belong to Mr. Gibson (2015). The Cemetery is behind his home about 70 yards under a partial fence. You will pass thru two gates. Go into the cemetery from the east side where there is no fence now. This is a winter time visit. I found the whole place high in weeds and the old fallen tree lying across the center of the cemetery and covering many graves and no doubt smashing many of the tombstones as you can see from the missing number I did not find.
