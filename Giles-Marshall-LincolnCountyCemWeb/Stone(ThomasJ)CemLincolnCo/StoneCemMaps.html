STONE  (Thomas J.) CEMETERY, (Hwy 273, Cemetery Ridge Lane [also a residential road]) - LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the Stone Cemetery.
Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 and turn left on Hwy 273 (Old Elkton Pike). Follow that nearly 15 miles and turn into Cemetery Ridge Road which doubles as a residential road and go until you almost come to the house and look left across the fence at the cemetery. There seems to be an open attitude about this cemetery from the land owner but notification of your business is still a good idea.
This Aerial Map shows where I highlighted the cemetery in yellow including separate yellow outlines for the George Cemetery section. This is all next to a private residence so do nothing to disturb the living neighbors or the deceased inhabitants. I think that river seen here is the Elk River.
