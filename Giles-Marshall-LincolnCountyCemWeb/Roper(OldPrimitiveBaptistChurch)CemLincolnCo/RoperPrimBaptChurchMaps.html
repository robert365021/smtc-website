ROPER (Primitive Baptist Church) CEMETERY (Hwy 110 Fayetteville to Ardmore Hwy) LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Roper Cemetery GPS location. 

Location: Take Highway 110 (Fayetteville to Ardmore) and go several miles, past Skinem and past Taft and go about 2 miles past Taft to the Church on the left side of the highway. The Roper Cemetery (new section) is in the church yard to the east of the building while the Roper Predestined Primitive Baptist (old section) is on the west side and allowed to grow up in weeds. The highway bends there to avoid running into the cemetery and church property.

This Aerial Map shows the plot where the cemetery resides next the old Roper Primitive Baptist Church marked with the Google map pointer. Some of the stones are very old and fragile and therefore easily damaged by overzealous people so be gentle on these soft monuments if you have business here.
