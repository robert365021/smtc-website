EZELL CEMETERY (Pulaski, Old Hwy 15 behind home), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Ezell Cemetery at Sumac as GPS: N 35.25309 X W -86.92803. 

Location: Go out Hwy 31/11 Alt to Krapp Springs Road and turn right. When that dead ends into Sumac Road turn left. Go to the last house on the right before reaching New Zion Church & Cemetery for permission. The cemetery is behind that house and behind the barns on the old Worsham place, but inside the white fence. This landowner values their privacy so permission will probably be denied. If you are a descendent of this cemetery use the legal & law community for visitation here. If you go here without accompaniment you will be denied entrance by a locked gate.  However,  you may be lucky enough to find someone who is in good graces of this landowner for securing permission. Good luck. The landowner seems to be taking relatively good care of this cemetery. However, there are some stones that are fallen and in need of being set back up or repaired The William & Sarah Samuel stone is face down. This used to be the location instructions: Hwy 31A, near New Zion Church, behind Mrs. Katholine Worshams House.
