CROSS CEMETERY, Off of Ardmore/Fayetteville Hwy, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Cross Cemetery as N 35.03052 X W -86.77677.

 Location: From Fayetteville go west on the Ardmore Hwy aka Hwy 110 several miles and past Taft and past another community called Blanche. After passing Blanche go about another 1 & 1/2 mile or so and turn north (right) into the Cross Cemetery Road. Follow that to the end of the road and it will bend back to the right after which the cemetery will be in front of you.
