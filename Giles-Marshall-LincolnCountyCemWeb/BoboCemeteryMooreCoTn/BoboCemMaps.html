BOBO FAMILY CEMETERY, Hwy 50/55, MOORE COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Bobo Cemetery GPS location as 35.31542, -86.35959. It is on the East Fork of Mulberry Creek. 

 Location: From Lynchburg go north on Hwy 50/55 to the junction of Hwy 82 branching left to the northeast and turn in and the cemetery is on your left in that junction Alternate description: This Cemetery is located about 2 1/2 miles North-East of Lynchburg on Highway 50/55 toward Tullahoma, just east of the junction of Hwy 82 and Hwy 50/55. The gentleman who owns this farm is very protective of this cemetery. Get permission to visit here.
This Aerial Map shows the area I have roughly outlined as the Bobo Cemetery area where the cemetery resides. One can see the faint outline of the stones under the trees here. The caretaker is doing a great job of keeping this cemetery maintained. This in no way represents a legal boundary.
