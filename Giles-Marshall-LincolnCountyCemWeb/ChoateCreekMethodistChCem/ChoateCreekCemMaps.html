CHOATES CREEK METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, (Old Hwy 64, Bodenham) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Choate Creed Cemetery on old Hwy 64 at the intersection of Choate Creek Road. 

 Location: Go west on Highway 64 (toward Lawrenceburg) from Pulaski several miles and branch off left (or south) onto the old Hwy 64 and the Church will be on your right between the new and old Hwy 64. The cemetery is across Choate Creek Rd. from the church.

This aerial map shows the cemetery just on the edge of the new highway 64 under construction here. It is shown in Yellow text and so is the Church across the Choate Creek Road.

This aerial map shows in more detail the cemetery. The streaks facing east/west are the faint images of the memorial stones.
