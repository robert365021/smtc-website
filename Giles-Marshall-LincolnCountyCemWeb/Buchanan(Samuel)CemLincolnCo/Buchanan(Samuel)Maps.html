BUCHANAN (Samuel) CEMETERY (Boonshill-Petersburg Rd E. side, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Samuel Buchanan Cemetery as N 35.26411 X W -86.67282.

Location: From Fayetteville west on Hwy 64 and turn north (right) on the Boonshill-Petersburg Road. Go about 6 miles and the road bends back north for the last time and after that bend there is the locked gate with double pad lock on it. Notification is required to get past that, but after doing so bend northeast on the field road and go about 200 yards from the gate and the cemetery is in a thicket on the right of that field road, just before a fence/hedge row.

This view shows the roads more clearly and the cemetery marked with a yellow check.
