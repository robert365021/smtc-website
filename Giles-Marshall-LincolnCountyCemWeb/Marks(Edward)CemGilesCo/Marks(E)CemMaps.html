MARKS, (Edward) Blooming Grove Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Edward Marks Cemetery. 

Turn right and go northeast on E. Grigsby St (aka Hwy 31 Alt) a couple of miles to Blooming Grove Road. Go nearly a mile and the cemetery will be on your right down a shallow bank next to the road.

This aerial map shows the cemetery well maintained. The trees in this view above hides the size of it. You can see one of the fence enclosures in this map under the "w" in Edward.
