GARRETT/PINSON CEMETERY (Dianna Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS location of the Garrett Cemetery.  This cemetery is detailed on the USGS records. It is my belief the GPS Latitude & Longitude are correct as stated.


 Location: Old Highway 15 to Beech Hill; go under I-65 underpass; Pass the Bradshaw Center Point Cemetery on the right and go another mile and the cemetery is in a field in the northwest corner of the junction of Dianna Road and Center Point Rd. (left) enclosed in a Rock wall. This is between Bradshaw and Diana in the eastern edge of the county. The dotted yellow line above is the Lincoln/Giles County line.  
This Aerial view shows the cemetery under the clump of trees 250 yards north of Center Point Rd. The folks living in the house in this corner are the surrounding land owners.
Maps from USGS Topographical, MS Streets & Trips 2004 & MS Bing Maps modified for this site by Wayne Austin, 24 May 2010.
