CHARITY CEMETERY, (off Charity Road on Steelman Loop Rd) MOORE COUNTY TENNESSEE

From the above map we see the Charity Cemetery GPS location. It is on the West Fork of Mulberry Creek. 

Location: From Shelbyville go south on Hwy 231 several miles up the hill into Moore County Tennessee and turn left on Charity Road (Hwy 129). Follow Charity Road about 3 miles to Steelman/(Stellman) Loop Road and turn left. Go less than a mile or nearly until that road terminates and look left inside a very large rock wall where the cemetery is located.
This Aerial Map shows the area where the cemetery resides next to what is labeled Stellman Loop Road. I am left wondering if at one time the Charity Baptist Church facility was located closer to the cemetery, though it is just around the corner now on Bagley Hollow Road. Part of the facility now can be seen on the bottom right edge of this map.
