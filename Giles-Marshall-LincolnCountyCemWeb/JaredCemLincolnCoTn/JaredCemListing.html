The folks on Findagrave and otherwise in Lincoln Co history have made an attempt to keep records of who is buried here. This is evidenced by the large number of grave listings without headstones. Some stones may still be there in the deep vegetation, but many others represent a record of a death and burial here whereby no one ever added an inscribed tombstone. Many of the above have the fieldstones shown below but I also noticed many grave sinks without as much as a fieldstone marking the head of the grave. Periwinkle was knee deep in this cemetery.
