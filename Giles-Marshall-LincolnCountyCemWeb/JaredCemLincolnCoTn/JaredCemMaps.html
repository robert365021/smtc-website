JARED CEMETERY (Crystal Ridge Road) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Jared Cemetery as 35.18582 X 86.41884. Crystal Ridge Rd is very steep so go here in dry weather. 

Location: From Fayetteville go Hwy 64 east to Hwy 50 then left and on Past Mulberry to Crystal Ridge Rd and turn right. Follow that for more than 2 miles to Stephens Creek Rd and turn right. Follow that for about 3 1/2 miles and it becomes Buntley Hill Rd. The cemetery is on out Crystal Ridge Rd. Turn right on Crystal Ridge Rd and go down a steep hill nd back up the other side following the road and go thru a gate and the cemetery is in a fence in front of you.  Get permission from the land owner/manager who lives nearby to get into this cemetery.
