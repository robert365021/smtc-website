MOORE CEMETERY, Hwy 130, N of Petersburg, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the Moore Cemetery GPS location as Lat. N. 35.532623, Long. W. -86.63933.
Location: Go north from Petersburg about 1/4 mile on Hwy 130 and turn left into a residential road and the cemetery is on the left back next to a hedgerow behind a new fence.

This photo shows the inside of the cemetery. Do not go to this place if your intentions are not honorable. Respect must be shown at all times for those gone before us by not disturbing (harming) their monuments & graves.
