This cemetery was visited and the memorial stones initially photographed by C. Wayne Austin 27 Jan 2011 without identifying any inscriptions. On 2 Apr 2012 David Lacy and others of his family visited the site and found & identified three inscribed stones by investigative work. This is two more than the book Bedford Co Cemeteries. Findagrave.com has this cemetery presented by David Lacy at:
