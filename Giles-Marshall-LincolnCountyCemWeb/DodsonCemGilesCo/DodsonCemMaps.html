Location: Highway 31N; right (east) into Lynnville Road, In Lynnville, go left (north) in front of
stores on Mill Street. Mill Street turns into Dodson Gap Rd. Cross overpass; cemetery on left between RR tracks and road. After a northeastward distance the road will bend due east and when it bends back north go another 3 tenths of a mile and look left at the thicket or cemetery by the tracks.
