McFERRIN CEMETERY, (Yukon, Yukon Rd), LINCOLN COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the GPS location of the McFerrin Cemetery as N 35.08415 X W -86.64865. This map above has the road set too far to our left.

Location: From Fayetteville go southwest on Hwy 110 (Ardmore Highway) about 7 miles. Go past Skinem, almost to Kirkland and turn right into the Camargo Rd. Follow that a couple of miles and turn left on Yukon Rd. Go 6 tenths of a mile and the road into the residence and back to the cemetery on the right. The road doubles as a farm road, cemetery road & residential road. However there is a locked gate just to the back of the house to insure one gets permission from the caretaker who lives on the north side of Yukon Rd as you will pass right by his/her garage.

This Aerial Map shows, clearly the fenced outline of the cemetery. Road is shown on the left.
