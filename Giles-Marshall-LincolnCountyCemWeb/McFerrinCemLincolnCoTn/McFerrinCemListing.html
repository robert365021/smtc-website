Several stones spelled the surname as McFerran indicating there was some confusion or disagreement as to how to spell the surname. I have found this to be a common problem, but I stayed with the most common spelling as McFerrin. This is all a part of the same extended family.

This presentation is based on the photography of C. Wayne Austin 19 Mar 2013. Material was presented on Page 516 Lincoln Co Cemeteries (book.) Added here 17 Sep 2013. Findagrave contributors have done an outstanding job presenting this cemetery.
