BOONSHILL CEMETERY (Boonshill) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

From the above map we see the Boonshill Cemetery GPS location. Actually Fayetteville is the nearest Town about 10 miles east GPS N 35.21155 X W -86.74305. 

Location: Take Highway 64 out of Fayetteville and go several miles and turn north (right) on Boonshill School Road. when that dead ends turn left and the church and cemetery will be on your left right away.

This Aerial Map shows the plot where the cemetery resides along with the premises of the church. Some of the stones here are very old and fragile cemetery and easily damaged by overzealous people so be gentle on these soft monuments if you have business here and basically hands off.
