MAXEY GRAVES (Gimlet Creek & Morgan Hollow Road) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Isaiah Thomas Maxey Cemetery in that cleared place north of he house shown in the aerial view. 

 Location: Go west on Highway 64 (toward Lawrenceburg) from Pulaski and turn north on Weakley Creek Road. Go more than a mile and turn left on Gimlet Creek Road. Go about 2 miles and turn left into the Morgan Hollow Road, cross Gimlet Creek and the graves are on the right above the road on the north of the house on your right.

This aerial map shows the cemetery on the north of the house. It is marked here with a red check. This is just an open space with the owners adding a building on-site back from where the graves are supposed to be located.
