GOOCH CEMETERY, TACKETT BRANCH ROAD, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

 Gooch & Mt Carmel Cemetery are not far apart as shown on the above map. Both are on the north side of Tacketts Branch Road.
This topographical map shows the elevations well and captures Gooch Cemetery high above the north side of the road and Mt Carmel on sloping up the hill to the east.
