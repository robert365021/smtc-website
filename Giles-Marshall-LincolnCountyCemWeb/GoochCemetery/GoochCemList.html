GOOCH CEMETERY, TACKETT BRANCH ROAD, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Location: Highway 11; left on Bethel Road, then right at Stella Sign. On Tackett Branch Road high upon the bank above the road on your left (going east) in a Cedar Tree thicket.
