GOOCH CEMETERY, TACKETT BRANCH ROAD, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

 Looking west at the Gooch Cemetery memorials lined up north to south. Notice the dying Cedar Trees all around.
This view captures about all of the memorials, looking northeast across the stones aligned north to south.

In spite of the small brush and cattle that can roam freely this cemetery is maintained. The memorials are kept standing upright even if the latest restoration processes are not in use.

Moving over to the north a few feet from the above photo will yield this image. The shade and dead Polk Berry weeds made the photography difficult.
