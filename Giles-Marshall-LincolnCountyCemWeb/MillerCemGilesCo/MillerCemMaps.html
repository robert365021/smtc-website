MILLER CEMETERY, (Hannah Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This topographical map from Topozone shows the GPS coordinates of the Miller Cemetery. I believe the GPS coordinates to be accurate.
Here we have a road map which shows the cemetery off of Highway 166 on Hannah Road or called Hannah Hollow Rd on some maps.  
Location: Highway 64 West, right into Campbellsville Road, proceed about 3 miles, turn left into Hannah Road, Cemetery on left toward the end of the dead end road.
This aerial map shows the cemetery just to the left of the Hannah Road which becomes a residential road, toward the end, before the last house. If your intentions are honorable you will be welcomed to visit this cemetery by the local land owner.
