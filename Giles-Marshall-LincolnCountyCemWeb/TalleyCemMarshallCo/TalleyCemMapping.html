TALLEY CEMETERY, Hwy 129, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Talley Cemetery as N 35.32813 X W -86.68239 as about the center of the cemetery.

Location: From Lewisburg go southeast on Highway 431 about 8+ miles and turn right (west on Delina Road (Hwy 129). Follow that for a couple of miles and look to your left at the cemetery along and back from the road. This is an old cemetery expanded with modern granite monuments also.

This map shows the four sections of this cemetery as A-D with the oldest being next to the road or section A. The cemetery has generally expanded south from the road over time with road access to all sections.
