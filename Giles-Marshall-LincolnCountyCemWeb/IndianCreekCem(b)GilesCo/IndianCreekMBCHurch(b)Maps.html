INDIAN CREEK (BLACK) CEMETERY (Newman Road) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Indian Creek Missionary Baptist Church Cemetery. 

Location: Highway 64 E; right on Pisgah Road; bear right to Indian Creek Road. Pass Bunker Hill, right on Newman Road and cross concrete bridge over Indian Creek. Cemetery on hill on right all around the Indian Creek Missionary Baptist Church building. This is in or near the community of Bryson.
.

This aerial map shows the cemetery marked with a red check on the premises of the Church yard along a slope. Be sure to go here with upscale intentions and secure permission. The road is labeled Newman Hollow Road and the road on the right is Bunker Hill Road.
