PRATT CEMETERY, Talley Moore Road, MAURY COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates needed to find this cemetery as 35.68106 N X -86.821017 W. I am not aware of the exact location behind that house, but the likely area is under the trees behind the home.

From Lewisburg north on Hwy 431 to Talley Moore Road and turn right. Go past Moore Cemetery to a sharp curve right and go instead straight ahead across a cattle gap. Next to left behind a home. Folks  in the home keep the graves cleared of brush. If my maps are correct and this is the right cemetery it is in Maury County instead of Marshall County Tennessee. The faint dotted line on the above map represents the county line. So I am placing this cemetery in Maury County.
