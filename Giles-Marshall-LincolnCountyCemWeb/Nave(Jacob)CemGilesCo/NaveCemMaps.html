NAVE-PHELPS CEMETERY, (Bunker Hill Rd) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

The red check shows the location of the Nave-Phelps Cemetery. This cemetery is detailed on the USGS records and I have provided this map to show the coordinates. The GPS Latitude & Longitude is correct as shown above.
