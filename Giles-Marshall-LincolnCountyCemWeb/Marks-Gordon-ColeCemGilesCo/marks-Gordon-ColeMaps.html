MARKS-GORDON-COLE CEMETERY, (Tarpley to Prospect Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Marks-Gordon-Cole Cemetery.

Go south on Hwy 31 to Tarpley and turn right on the Prospect Road and go about a mile and look left at a grove of trees in a pasture. You will need permission from the folks that live in the next house on the right of the cemetery.

This aerial map shows the cemetery in a clump of trees marked with a red check. You cannot get there by going due east from the road without damaging a fence, so permission is needed from the next house south.
