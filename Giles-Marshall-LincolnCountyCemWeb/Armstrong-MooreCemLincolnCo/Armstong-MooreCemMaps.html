ARMSTRONG-MOORE CEMETERY Hwy 231, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Armstrong-Moore Cemetery as N 35.24388 X W -86.56527.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Highway 231 about 6 miles and the highway will bend to the left to go around a steep hill ahead. Just after that look for house at the bottom of the hill and the cemetery is behind that home in a grove of trees at the top of the hill. The old Stage Coach road went thru the cemetery and did not go around the hill. You will find graves on both sides of the ruts that were made by the carriages cutting into the ground. The Whitaker Cemetery is highlighted here but look just north of there about a mile for the Moore Cemetery.
