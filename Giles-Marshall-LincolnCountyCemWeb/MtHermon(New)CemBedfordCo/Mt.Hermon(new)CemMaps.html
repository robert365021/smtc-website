MT. HERMON (NEW) (Mt. Hermon), BEDFORD COUNTY, TENNESSEE
The center of the map shows the location of the New Mount Hermon Cemetery. This cemetery is detailed on the USGS records and the GPS coordinates are correct as shown above.


From Shelbyville go south several miles to Mt Hermon Road and turn left. Follow that as it journeys eastward about 4 miles to the cemetery. Mt Hermon - old cemetery is on the left and Mt Hermon - new cemetery is on the right a little further along as shown on the maps here.
This Aerial view shows the New Mt Hermon Cemetery clearly on Hart Road where the red check is placed with the yellow label.
