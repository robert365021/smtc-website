PILLOW CEMETERY, (Agnew Rd) GILES, COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Pillow Cemetery. 

Location, Go out Hwy 64 west 2 to 3 miles to Agnew Creek Road. Turn left (or south) on Agnew road and go more than 2 miles down road on right to the Herbert Mize house. The cemetery is behind/next to an old barn about 75 yards west of the road on the far (west) side of the barn under a stand of hackberry trees.

This shows the cemetery on the left of the barn. Do not confuse the object seen just north of the red check as a stone. That is an old abandoned propane tank.
