LEWTER CEMETERY (Stephenson Rd, near Ardmore) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Lewters Cemetery  as N 35.01786 X W 86.84056 on the backside of the farm. 

 Location: From Ardmore go north on Ardmore Ridge Road and turn right on Stephenson Road. Just before that road bends back south find the cemetery behind the old home on the left up the slope past several fences. You cannot get in there without permission from the landowner to open the gates. Also he will inform you how to drive there to keep from damaging his crops.

This aerial map shows the cemetery right of way but the land owner plants certain crops that do need to be considered when driving there. Also driving there is only applicable in dry weather. You will bogged down in mud and get stuck in wet weather.
