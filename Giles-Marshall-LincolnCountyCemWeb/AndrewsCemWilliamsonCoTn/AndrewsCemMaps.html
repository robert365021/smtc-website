ANDREWS CEMETERY, Franklin, WILLIAMSON COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Andrews Cemetery as N 35.91973 X W -86.89849.

Location: From downtown Franklin Tennessee go west on Hwy 96 to Downs Blvd and follow that south for about 1/2 mile and turn left into the Williamson County Soccer complex. When that street dead ends into a T go right (south) to the tree lined Andrews Cemetery on your left. It has tall chain link fence including another wooden fence with pad locks on it so better carry a powerful camera with a zoom lens to shoot thru the two fences or the photos will not be readable or locate the caretaker and get permission. I was visiting my grandson's soccer tournament so did not pursue the permission route due to time. I did not want to take anything away from his championship win.

This closer image shows a yellow check mark where the cemetery resides. The cemetery sits above the surrounding terrain which has been modified for the Soccer ball fields.
