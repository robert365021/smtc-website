ABERNATHY CEMETERY, (Amos Hamlet Rd., South side) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Abernathy Cemetery. 

Go south on Hwy 31 from Pulaski and turn east (left) on Amos Hamlet Road. Go 3/4 of a mile and turn right into the driveway of a residence (with permission). Stop on the driveway and look (right) southeast over a rise out into a pasture at the cemetery under a clump of trees. The cemetery may be hard to see because it is behind the rise from the driveway.

This aerial map shows the cemetery under the trees. It is out in a field in a clump of trees on the west side of an old house. This is on Britton Branch Creek which crosses the road shown here as a string of trees going north/south.
