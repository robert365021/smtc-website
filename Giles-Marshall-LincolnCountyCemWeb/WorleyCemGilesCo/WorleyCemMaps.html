WORLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 64 E.) - GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Worley Cemetery. 

Go east on Hwy 64 to Five Points and turn right into a Steel Supply Company driveway and the cemetery is just inside the gate on the left under the trees.

This aerial map shows the cemetery inside the premises of a business. There is a rock wall & an iron fence around some of the graves.
