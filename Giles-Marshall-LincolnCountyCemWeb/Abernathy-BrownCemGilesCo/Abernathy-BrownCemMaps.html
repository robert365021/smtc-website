ABERNATHY (James B.)- BROWN (JIM) CEMETERY, (Tarpley Shop Road, south-side)GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Abernathy-Brown Cemetery. The map is incorrectly labeled as the Birdsong Cemetery. Birdsongs did own the place at one time.

Go south on Hwy 31 from Pulaski and turn east (left) on Tarpley Shop Road. Go about 2 1/2 miles and stop near a thicket on the south side of the road and the cemetery is in that thicket. The cemetery is shown above mis labeled as the Birdsong Cemetery. The Birdsong Cemetery is about 800 yards northeast of this one.

This aerial map shows the Abernathy-Brown Cemetery marked with a red check under the trees next to the south side of the road.
