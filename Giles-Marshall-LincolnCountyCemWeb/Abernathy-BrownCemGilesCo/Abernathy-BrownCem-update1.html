ABERNATHY (James B.)- BROWN (JIM) CEMETERY, (Tarpley Shop Road, south-side) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
THOMAS, Levenia Ophelia Brown, 3 Dec 1870 in Giles County, TN & Died 14 Aug 1954 in Jefferson County, AL.. (twin to Rosana Olevia Brown; buried here in an unmarked grave beside her husband Isaac Bradford Thomas)

Pulaski Citizen August 18, 1954

OPHELIA B. THOMAS
Ophelia B. Thomas, 82, died at 7:00 Saturday night, August 14, 1954, at home of daughter, Mrs. W. C. Wilson, Birmingham, Al., following years of declining health.
Funeral services held at 1:00 Monday afternoon in chapel of Bennett-May by Rev. Ralph Bruce, pastor Bethesda Methodist Church. Burial in family cemetery, Abernathy burying ground on Old Stage Road.
Daughter of late James Davis Brown and Adaline Abernathy Brown. She was born Dec. 3, 1870 in Giles County. Member of Bethesda Methodist Church since childhood. Husband Issac Bradford Thomas died Jan. 1, 1933.
Survived by 3 daughters, Mrs. Henry K. Birdsong, Pulaski, Mrs. Wilson, Birmingham, Al., Mrs. Lena Chambus, Benton, Ky.; one son Raymond Thomas, St. Peterburg, Fl.; 17 grandchildren; and a number of great-grandchildren.
Bennett-May in charge.

Thanks,
Rita Birdsong, Decatur, AL
