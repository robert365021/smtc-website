For those of you interested in the photography aspects of this effort. I was photographing here in the early morning sharp rays of the sun. That created photos with too much contrast in the shade for several instances creating the need to alter the photos so the inscriptions become more readable. I do not use anything but the auto settings on the camera because of the time it takes to otherwise get that right.

Visited here and photographed on the early morning of 28 Sep 2009. Other sources are Giles County Tennessee Cemetery book page 435, 1986 by the Giles County Tennessee Historical Society.
