HOPSON CEMETERY, Knox Street, east side, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Hopson Cemetery as N 35.19145 X W -87.05001.

From downtown Pulaski traveling out west on College St. from the courthouse turn south (left) on Mill St. & go about a mile then turn (west) right on Vales Mill St. Turn on the 1st Street going south (left) which is Knox Street. The Hopson Cemetery is in a clump of trees on the left (east side). There are only a few signs of a cemetery remaining today. I saw one fragment of a carved tombstone - no inscription remaining.
