MOUNT ZION-FRANKEWING CEMETERY, (Frankewing, Near I-65 Exit 14) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Mount Zion aka Frankewing Cemetery. 

From the junction of I-65 & Hwy 64 or Exit 14 go east on Hwy 64 about 1/2 mile and turn left (northeast) on the road that goes to Frankewing (old Hwy 64). Take the first left road and go into Frankewing. When you dead end into Beech Hill Rd turn left and go several hundred yards and the church & cemetery can be seen on the hill on your right. Take the church & cemetery road up the slope to the cemetery.

This aerial view from Google shows the church and cemetery labeled in bright colors.
