SORRELLS (James E.) CEMETERY, S. side Sorrells Rd,  TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the James E. Sorrells Cemetery shown as N 35.35500 X W 86.60577.

Location: From Fayetteville go north many miles on Highway 231 to Chestnut Ridge and turn left on Hwy 129 (Chestnut Ridge/Petersburg Rd). Follow that about 3 miles and turn right (north) on Bledsoe Road and go something under three miles and turn left on Hannah Gap Road. The Hannah Gap Baptist Church is on your right just before you turn left. Follow Hannah Gap Road which becomes Sorrells Road after crossing into Marshall County for 2+ miles and and look to the left where you will see the tall stones of the Sorrells family.

This aerial map shows the cemetery outlined in yellow next to the road on the (left) south side. The cemetery is maintained by the Sorrells family with fencing on three sides.

The Jake Sorrells family and home. May have been across the road from the cemetery.

From this google image we see surviving parts of an old home which shows the foundation rocks and the chimney. This sits back in the field south east of the JE Sorrells Cemetery about 150 yards. There is a problem matching this chimney which looks similar to the one in the home above in that the chimney sites inside the home in this building and the one above for the JE Sorrells home is on the side. This home may have belonged to the Elzey family which has a family graveyard another 200 yards up the hill to the southeast.
