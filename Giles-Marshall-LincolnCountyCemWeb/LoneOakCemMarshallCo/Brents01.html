LONE OAK  CEMETERY (Lewisburg) MARSHALL COUNTY TN

BRENTS, Cora J., 14 Nov 1869 - 15 Feb 1955.
 

 (Mrs. Cora Adams Brents Dies at Age 85; Tenn. Rites Set. Mrs. Cora Brents, died 12:35 p.m. Tuesday at her home, on Hargrover Road. A native of Louisburg Tenn. Mrs. Brents lived there until there death of her husaband in 192 after which she made her home with only son, Tom Brent. They came to Tuscaloosa in 1945. She was a member of the Church of Christ and was active in the work here while her health permitted. Surviving are the son, Tom Brents, l sister, mrs. J. W. Liggett, Nashville, Tenn.; three nieces, Mrs. C. E. Reynold, Panama, Ill, Mrs. W. R. Turles, Corpus Christi, Tx., and Mrs. Clay Orr, Lebonan, Mo. and two nephews, C. W. and L. G. Picker, Sarcoxie, Mo.Funeral will be held at Lousiburg, Tn. Thursday. Jones and Spigener is in charge of local arrangements.
