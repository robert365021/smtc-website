BUNTLEY (New) CEMETERY (Paint Hollow Rd.) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the New Buntley Cemetery as N 35.24329 X W 86.53997.

 Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 and turn right on Clarks Mill Rd and when that road dead ends into Mimosa Rd take that north (left). Continue on Mimosa Rd to Paint Hollow Rd and turn left. Cemetery is on the left just after turning.
