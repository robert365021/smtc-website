FREEMAN (Abner) CEMETERY (Poor Grab-Chestnut Ridge Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
Mapping the Location

Three Graves, Abner Freeman and other members of his family - marked by native fieldstones, no inscriptions. For possible determination of the Freeman family members here see the censuses of 1820 to 1880.

This presentation is based on the photography of C. Wayne Austin, 2 Mar 2011. Added here 14 Nov 2011. Abner Freeman Cem is from the same page in the book Cemetery Records of Lincoln County by Tim & Helen Marsh.
