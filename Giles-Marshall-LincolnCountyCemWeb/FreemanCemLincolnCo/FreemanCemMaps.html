FREEMAN CEMETERY (Poor Grab-Chestnut Ridge Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
Shown as the Freeman Cemetery on USGS mapping. The GPS coordinates are corrects as far as I can tell.

From Fayetteville go north out Hwy 231 into Moore County and upon Chestnut Ridge. While traveling along Chestnut Ridge turn left on Poor Grab Chestnut Ridge Road and go back down the hill. When that road junctions with Pierce Road about a mile along the way turn north (right) into Pierce Rd and the cemetery is right there on the right in a clump of trees beside a house. It is very lightly maintained inside a fence.

 This aerial view shows the trees that are adjacent to the cemetery where the cemetery is located. This is a small family cemetery so may be private.
