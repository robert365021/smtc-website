COCHRAN CEMETERY, Cochran Cemetery Rd, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we are given the Cochran Cemetery GPS location as N 35.39609 X W -86.79969.

Location: From Lewisburg go Southwest on Hwy 31 Alt/Hwy 11 for a couple or three miles and turn left on Cochran Cemetery road and follow that (bearing left) for nearly a mile to the cemetery on the right (west side of the road).

This Aerial Map shows the cemetery more clearly with some visibility of the stones. I have surrounded the area that was photographed with a yellow line.
