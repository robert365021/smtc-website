CRANE CEMETERY, Hwy 82 N. of Flat Creek. BEDFORD COUNTY TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Crane Cemetery GPS location as N 35.40722, W -86.41875.
Location: From Highway 55 go north of Lynchburg to the Hwy 82 Shelbyville Highway. Go several miles and an additional a mile past Flat Creek and look to your right at a clump of brush and a tree in an open field. The cemetery resides under the one tree today.

This Aerial Map shows a yellow check where you will find this cemetery located. It is just one tree surrounded by Privy Hedge.
