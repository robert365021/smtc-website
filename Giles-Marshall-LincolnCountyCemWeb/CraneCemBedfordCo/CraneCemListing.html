CRANE, Nancy, Born Jun 10, 1796 - Died Aug 26, 1816. Believe this is Nancy's footstone no initials found: 3987/3988

Only 2 graves in Cemetery. There is another adult grave beside Nancy Cramer's - no inscription. This is not so visible today due to the infestation of Hedge and the tree.
