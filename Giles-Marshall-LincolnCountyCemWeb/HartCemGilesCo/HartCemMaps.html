HART CEMETERY (Abernathy Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Hart Cemetery. 

Go north on Hwy 31 from Pulaski and turn east (right) on Buford Station Road and after more then a mile turn left on Abernathy Road. Travel about another mile and the road will bend back right. The house in that bend on the left has a tree that has flowers below it and the remaining stones of this cemetery is in that flower bed.

This aerial map shows the cemetery under the tree next to the road.

This Google map shows the tree marked with a red check where the two stones were located. If you visit here please involve the land/home owner in your quest to locate these stones.
