BARNES CEMETERY, Barnes Hollow Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Barnes Cemetery as N 35.19085 X W -86.71383.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 and turn right on Barnes Hollow Road. Follow that for less than two miles and look for the Barnes Cemetery road. Turn right (north) and the cemetery is straight ahead & well kept.

This image tries to capture the borders of the cemetery with a yellow line but in no way reflects the formal legal borders of the cemetery.
