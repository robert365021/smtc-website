BROWN CEMETERY, (Hwy 31, Old Lynnville), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

J.M. Brown memorial on our left and the one on the left is for Malinda Brown. Lying on the ground between the two upright memorials is the below memorial for Martha Ann Keltner.

Martha Ann Keltner.

KELTNER, Martha Ann, wife of B. F. Keltner, 6 Jan 1847 - 6 Sep 1895, 48 yrs, 8 no. (note: B. F. Keltner was buried in Manor, Texas)
Stone is flaking off and becoming very hard to read. Relying on past transcriptions here.
