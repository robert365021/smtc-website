BROWN CEMETERY, (Hwy 31, Old Lynnville), GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
Location: Highway 31 north, two and one half miles north of Waco Cemetery, behind a house now occupied by the Bonds family. The old Brown home was torn down several years ago at this site and a smaller house built on the same site. The yellow arrow shows the place where the cemetery can be found surrounded on all sides by homes.
