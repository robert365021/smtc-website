YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Vick Family lots are under the Hedge Shrub Bushes. Here we see the head & footstones for John H. Vick & his wife Annie Jane Barlar and headstones for three of their sons Kenneth on the south of their dual headstone and Johnnie (our subject here) & Charley on our right (north) side of their parents stone. Several other unmarked fieldstones show graves in line with these and immediately to the north down the north/south row. All this is in the southwest corner of the cemetery.

Johnnie (John Arlule) Vick, 20 Sep 1882 - 20 Sep 1899. (Son of Annie Jane Barlar and John H. Vick.) Photo by Wayne Austin 21 Dec 2009.
