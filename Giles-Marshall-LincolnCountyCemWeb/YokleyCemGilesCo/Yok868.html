WEST, Cora Lee, 17 Apr 1881 - 17 Aug 1881, daughter of J.C. & Delia West.
Her parents are interred in the H.C. Campbell Cemetery aka Cambpell-Rittenberry Cemetery in Campbellsville two miles south of here. Maybe the Wests' moved (or at least started to using the other cemetery) to Cambpellsville about the same time as the Poplar Grove Cumberland Presbyterian Church was moved there? By 1903 J.C. & Delia were burying small children in that cemetery instead of here.
