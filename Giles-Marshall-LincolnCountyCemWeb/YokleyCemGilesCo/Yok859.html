"SACRED to the memory of Franklin A., son of Andrew & Delia Yokley who departed this life June 30th 1852. aged 24 years, 9 mo. & 21 days leaving Father & Mother and numerous relations to mourn their loss. Professed religion at Poplar grove aug 19, lived a consistent member in the Cumberland Presbyterian church died in the triumph of faith. Blessed are the dead which die in the Lord from henceforth yea saith the spirit that they may rest from their labors and their works do follow them." 

"Joined the 1 0 of O. F. oct 51 F.L.T."

YOKLEY, Franklin A., died 30 Jun 1852, Aged 24 years, 9 months & 21 days. son of Andrew & Delia Yokley. (Boxtomb)
The Poplar Grove Cumberland Presbyterian Church was thought to be located near this graveyard at one time. This inscription lends weight to that possibility. The congregation moved to Campbellsville about 2 miles south west of here sometime after the founding of this graveyard in or prior to the 1830s. The little quaint church building is still there today standing as a reminder of the deep faith of these people. See photo on main menu"
