YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Drusilla S. Abernathy Richey. Tall stone on the right is our subject.

"In memory of Drusilla Y. Daughter of J. Y. & M.S. Abernathy & Consort of W. A. Richey born August 1st 1832 died June 24th 1856 age 24 years 1 mo. 7 days. For me to live is christ And to die is gain." (date does not calculate)

RICHEY, Drucilla, 1 Aug 1832 - 24 Jun 1856, Age 24 years 1 month 7 days. (date does not calculate) daughter of John Y. & Mary Sharp Alexander Abernathy, consort of W. A. Richey.
