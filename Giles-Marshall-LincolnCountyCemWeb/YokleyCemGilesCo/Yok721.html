YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

The Andrew Yokley Tomb on the left broken at the top and falling down. Delia Yokley his wife's tomb is just to our right, & falling but still unbroken. Photo by Terina Boyd 10 Oct 2009.

Box tomb on our left. Broken across the center and sunken in at the top for: Andrew Yokley. 

Andrew Yokley, (De)c 28 1791- _ _  Oct  _ _ _ _ (Ag)ed 72 Yrs 11 Months & 29 days. Box tomb broken and sunken into the ground. He is the husband of Delia Yokley whose box tomb is beside his.) Inscription readable: _ _ _ _ (A)NDREW YOKLEY (De)c 28th 1783 Died _ _ _ _ _  _ _ _ _ _ _ Aged 72 Yrs 11 Months & 29 Days. Translation (believe it or not) by Wayne Austin 21 Jan 2010

YOKLEY, Andrew, 28 __ 1791 - _ _ _ _ _ _ _ _ Aged 72 Yrs 11 Months & 29 days. Box tomb broken and sunken into the ground. He is the husband of Delia Yokley whose box tomb is next (north) to his.)
