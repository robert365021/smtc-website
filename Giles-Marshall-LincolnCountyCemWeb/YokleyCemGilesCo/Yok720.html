YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Box tomb in the Middle of the photo. The Box tomb behind hte tall standing slab belongs to Frankin A. Yokley. Beside that is a small box tomb unseen because if is fallen of William C. Yokley. So there are 6 box tombs in this cemetery and I have translated and accounted for all of them. 

Delia Yokley. Inscribed as: " Delia Yokley wife of Andrew Yokley Brn March 25th 1794 or (1791) Died June 22nd 1880 aged 86 Yrs 2 Mos & 27 days.

YOKLEY, Delia, 25 Mar 1794 - 22 Jun 1880, Aged 86 years, 2 months & 27 days; wife of Andrew Yokley; (daughter of Isaac & Susannah Morris.)
