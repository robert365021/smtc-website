YOKLEY, Marcus J., 4 Apr 1862 - 10 Sep 1863, son of J. & A. A. Yokley. 
The Jacob & Ann Yokley family memorials & graves are all lined up north & south in a row with the father & mother in the center and the daughters on the north side of their parents and sons on the south side.
