YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Susannah English, (born 12 Sep 1814) died 25 May 1851. Aged 36 Years 8 Mo & 13 Days. Consort of Thomas G. (Goss) English. Stone is adjacent to that of her two sons Robert & Thomas English both of whom died during the reconstruction era of the Civil Ware when much guerilla warfare fighting still lingered in this area. Were their deaths related to that? Photo by Wayne Austin. 21 Dec 2009.

Inscription: In Memoriam - Died May 25th 1851 SUSANAH ENGLISH  - Consort of Tho's G. - English. Aged 36 Years 8 Mo & 13 Days. Photo & inscription translated by Wayne Austin 21 Dec 2009.
