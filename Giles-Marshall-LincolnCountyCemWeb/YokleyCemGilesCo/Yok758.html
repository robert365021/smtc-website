YOKLEY CEMETERY, (Hwy 245 & Smith Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

James H. Yokley, (Born circa 9 Nov 1866) Died 9 Feb 1888. Age 21 Yrs, 3 Mo's (son of Jacob & Almanza Ann Hayes Yokley.) Photo by Wayne Austin 21 Dec 2009.

"IN Loving Memory of JA'S H. YOKLEY, DIED Feb. 9, 1888. Aged 21 Yrs. 3 Mo's.
Gone, yes gone where a golden crown Now wreathes his lovely brow And in a song to earth unknowing. His voice is mingling in." 
Photo by Wayne Austin 21 Dec 2009.
