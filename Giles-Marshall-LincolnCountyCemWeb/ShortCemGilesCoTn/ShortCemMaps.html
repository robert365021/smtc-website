SHORT CEMETERY, Short Road, north side, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Short Cemetery as N 35.16716 X W -87.08175

From Pulaski go south on Hwy 11 (Minor Hill Hwy) several miles and turn back north (right) on Short Road. Then go 1/2 a mile and turn right into a long driveway  which Crosses the creek and follows the driveway that curves to your right around to a log home of a residence in the distance. The Short Cemetery is east (left0 of you upon a hill out in a clump of trees and cannot be seen without first climbing the hill. With permission veer your vehicle across the field pointing at an angle left away (east) from the log home and climb the steep hill to the crest and bend left (east) toward the tree grove you will see on your left. The grove of trees contains the cemetery.
