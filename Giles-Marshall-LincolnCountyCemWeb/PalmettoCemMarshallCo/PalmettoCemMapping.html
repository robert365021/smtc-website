PALMETTO, CEMETERY, ST 64 & Palmetto Cem. Rd, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates needed to find this cemetery as N 35.49382 X W 86.67207

From Lewisburg go a 6.2 miles northwest on Hwy 31 and turn right on the Shelbyville (State Hwy 64). Follow that for about 2.7 miles and turn left (north) into the Palmetto Cemetery Road. The cemetery is on the right under the trees about three hundred yards up the road.

This map shows a rough outline of the cemetery set in a yellow graphic line. Notice you can see the tops of some of the stones here.
