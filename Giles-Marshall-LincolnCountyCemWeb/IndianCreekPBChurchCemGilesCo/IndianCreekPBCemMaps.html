INDIAN CREEK PRIMITIVE BAPT. CHURCH CEMETERY, Indian Creek Rd., GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Indian Creek Primitive Baptist Church Cemetery. 

Location: Highway 64 E; right into Pisgah Road, then bear right onto Indian Creek Road, at Indian Creek Primitive Baptist Church on left. On this map it is bear right on Braly Hollow Rd then turn back north on Indian Creek rd nand go about a 1/2 mile to the cemetery on the right behind the church inside a chain link fence.

This aerial map shows the cemetery as highlighted with a yellow line showing approximate borders behind the old church building.
