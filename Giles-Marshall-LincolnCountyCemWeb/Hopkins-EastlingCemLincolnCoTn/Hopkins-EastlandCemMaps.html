HOPKINS-EASTLAND (southeastern part of county) CEMETERY LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Hopkins-Eastland Cemetery as N 35.204163 X W -86.48863, behind a modern family home.

 Location: From Hwy 231 S. go east on Lincoln Rd to Corders Crossroads Rd. GO north there to Smith Mill Rd (New Hope) and on past that another 1/4 mile to a new home on the left. With permission, find the cemetery behind the house in a stand of trees. Better approach is to call ahead to this family as one could get a dog bite or some other hazard if not.
 Mapping from  ACME Mapper 2.0 &  MS Streets & Trips mapping sites. Modified and added here 29 Nov 2013 by C. Wayne Austin.
