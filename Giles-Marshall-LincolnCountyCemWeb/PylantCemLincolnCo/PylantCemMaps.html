PYLANT CEMETERY, (Union Hollow Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Pylant Cemetery as N 35.29901 X W -86.57342.

 Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 several miles to Cold Spring Road and turn left or northwest. Travel that to Union Hollow Road and turn right and go about 2 miles to a residence and past that to a fence row and the cemetery is really in the back yard of the home where they have sorta turned it into a part of a large decorative garden.

I have roughed out here about where you will find stones for this cemetery but do not have any idea if this cemetery has a separate lot line. Being on private property and as a family cemetery I doubt there was ever a separate lot line.
