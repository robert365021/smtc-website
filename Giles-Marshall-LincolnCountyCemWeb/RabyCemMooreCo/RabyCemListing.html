This cemetery was visited and the memorials photographed by C. Wayne Austin 23 Feb 2011. The listing was then transcribed by C. Wayne Austin 18 Jan 2013. Preliminary typing of a listing by Faye Bradford, 10 Sep 2011. This cemetery also listed on page 92-95, Moore County Cemetery Records, book by Tim & Helen Marsh abt 1989. This book continues to be an important source of cemetery and genealogy information for Moore County Tennessee. This site is far short of all the cemeteries out there and findagrave.com only covers researchers working on "my family" and folks that only make the easily read tombstone photo finds.  The above record currently has 216 listings. Findagrave.com currently has 191 of those listings and the book was far behind that in listing numbers because this is an active cemetery. In many cases Findagrave.com has great family information including personal photos out there if you have need of that though we show the tombstone photos.
