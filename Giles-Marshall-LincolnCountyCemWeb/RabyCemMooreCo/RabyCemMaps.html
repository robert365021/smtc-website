RABY CEMETERY (Warren Hollow Rd) MOORE COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Raby Cemetery as: N 35.34527 X W 86.49847.

Location: Go north on Hwy 231 from Fayetteville up and down Chestnut Ridge to Mt Hermon Rd. and turn (right) east toward Mt Hermon. Follow that a couple of miles and turn right and go down the hill on Warren Hollow Road to Raby Cemetery Road and turn right on that and the cemetery is 200 yards up the road on the right. You cannot miss it from there.

This aerial map shows the cemetery more clearly. Notice the many names of the Raby Cemetery Road as Gardner Lane or Petty Hollow Rd. Such confusion.
