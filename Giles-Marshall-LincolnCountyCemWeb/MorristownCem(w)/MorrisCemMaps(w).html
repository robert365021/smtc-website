MORRISTOWN CEMETERY, (Dry Creek Road) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of he Morristown Cemetery. Not sure if that is the White, Black or both areas of the cemetery shown above.

 Location: Highway 64 West, right into Campbellsville Road, left on Dry Creek Road. Go 1/4 mile and turn left into the New Hope Cemetery Rd. - former Collins farm.

This map shows the local roads needed to get to this cemetery. I do not know where the New Hope Cemetery is located, but it could be the designate name of the Morristown Cemetery (Black) by the black community.

This is an attempt to show the black & white areas of the Morristown Cemetery. Notice the White area only has five graves marked and is located on the north side of the much larger plot.
