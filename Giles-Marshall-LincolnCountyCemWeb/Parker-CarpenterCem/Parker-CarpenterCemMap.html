PARKER-CARPENTER CEMETERY, (Ball Hollow Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE 

Location: Take Highway 64 West from Pulaski and turn north (right) into Campbellsville Road, after several miles turn right into Minnow Branch Road, then right into Apple Hill Road then left on Ball Hollow Road, cemetery on left side of the front yard of a brick house. If you attempt to visit this cemetery one of the owners will bury you with negative verbiage. Best we can determine the another owner or party acts as a calming agent for the mean one. I cannot guarantee you will be able to visit this cemetery, because of the dense growth and the issues with the owner. So the cemetery perishes from neglect. Is that what the public wants with literally thousands of persons around the country that descends from the interred here?  Evidently prior to this owner it has been well cared for in the past with fencing and the stones (we can see) all still standing, but that won't last long under the present circumstances. 
The cemetery is marked with a small red check on the above map.
