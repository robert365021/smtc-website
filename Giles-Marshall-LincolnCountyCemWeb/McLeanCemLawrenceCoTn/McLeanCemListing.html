This production is based on the on-site photographic session on 6 Feb 2013 of C. Wayne Austin. I was accompanied that day by my wife Linda Black Austin and our friends Nick & Trish Brewer Boone of Nashville TN. Trish is an Irvine descendent and she and I may be third great nephew & niece of Samuel McLean. Other sources for this compilation are Kathy Niedergeses, Dec. 20, 2010, Lawrence County Archives Cemetery listings. Her original sources were  (1) 1936 WPA records; (2) 1955 Lawrence Co. Historical Society listing of names; (3) 1965 listing by Mrs. Lillie Belle McLean Appleton. "At Rest Cemetery Records of Lawrence County Tennessee by Alexander"
