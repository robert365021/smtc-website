Findagrave's Jimbo Stevens says there is two slave children's graves known to be here, buried in a grave marked by a large flat rock. The children were killed in a kitchen fire at the Sharp Family's house. The burial site is approximately 50 feet south of the Sharp Family Cemetery. Findagrave's Jimbo also shows two small footstones, 1 with the initials L.D. & another with the initials T.S.. Perhaps the TS is T. Sharp.
