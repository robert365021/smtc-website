PICKENS CEMETERY, Mooresville Rd. MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map plots GPS Coordinates of the Pickens Cemetery in the southwest corner of Marshall County Tennessee as 35.40992 X -86.90045.

From Exit 27, I-65 exit and go west toward Lynnville less than a mile and 1/2 and turn north (right) on Mooresville Rd. Follow that as it snakes around for abt 3 miles and the cemetery is across the road from a new home upon a hill, in a cane thicket, about 100 yards up the hill, east of the road. Position yourself on the road at the south corner of the new home and climb east up the hill and you will find the cemetery. I caution everyone to go here only with good intentions. Bad intentions will be met with swift justice by the local people who will quietly question anyone's intentions for visiting a cemetery such as this.
