HARRIS CEMETERY (Howell, Daves Hollow Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Harris Cemetery as N 35.23007 X W -86.60719.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 431 to Howell (originally Cane Creek) and turn right on Daves Hollow Rd. Follow that for about 1/4 mile and look to your left at the cemetery next to the road.
If you visit here do so with respect for the heritage of those buried here in this historical burial site.  Otherwise the watchful eyes of nearby neighbors will find you out and you & your deeds will be figuratively strung up to the highest tree for all to pity.
