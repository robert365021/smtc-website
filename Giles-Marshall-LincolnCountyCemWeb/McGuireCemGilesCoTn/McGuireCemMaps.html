McGUIRE CEMETERY, Bee Springs Road, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the McGuire Cemetery as GPS N 35.14194, W -86.83244. 

Location: From Exit 14 of I-65 which is the junction of Hwy 64 & I-65 go east a few hundred yards and turn south (right) on  Bradshaw Creek Road. Follow that south until it crosses into Lincoln County Tennessee and become Dee Solomon Road then back to Bee Springs road after crossing back into Giles County. Just past that turn right (west) into the long driveway of the old Story Farm. The cemetery is past several barns and sheds and requires accompaniment by one of the Story Brothers.

This aerial map shows the cemetery just north of a barn and west of a pond in a clump of trees. A fence line runs on the east side of the cemetery, but actually the whole cemetery is fenced.
