JOHNSON CEMETERY, Bugtussle Rd, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Johnson Cemetery as N 35.18170 X W -86.75931.

Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 and turn left (south) on Swan Creek Road and go less than a mile and turn left on Bugtussle Rd. Follow that for less than a mile and look right after you pass a house and the two little stones can be seen between the driveway and Bugtussle Road.
