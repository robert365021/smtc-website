BOSWELL-CARTER CEMETERY, (Ardmore Ridge Rd) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This cemetery sprang up around the nearby Union Hill Baptist Church. It is still active but moved to Union Hill Road about 1 1/2 miles from its former location. See D. D Johnson listing for possible other church connections.
