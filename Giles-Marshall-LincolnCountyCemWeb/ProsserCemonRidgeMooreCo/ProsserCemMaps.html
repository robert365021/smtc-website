PROSSER CEMETERY, Hwy 231 east side, Chestnut Ridge, MOORE COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Alexander Cemetery as N 35.21394 X W -86.53751.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 and turn right on Clarks Mill Rd then back north (left) on Mimosa Rd and go to Possum Hollow Rd. Turn left there and then right on Gray Road and the cemetery is on the left, into the woods, above a branch, and all around a large Pin Oak tree.

The yellow check marks the spot of this cemetery on the above map.

This is an attempt to outline the perimeter of this cemetery using a yellow graphic line
