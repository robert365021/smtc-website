MT CARMEL CEMETERY, TACKETT BRANCH ROAD, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

Lucy Jane Hardy Allen

ALLEN, Lucy Jane, 13 Jun 1843 - 9 Aug 1866, daughter of Jesse & Sarah M. Hardy; Married P. T. Allen 1 Sep 1864. 
Inscription: LUCY JANE ALLE -- N daughter of JESSE -- & SARAH M. HARDY -- was Born June 13th -- 1843 Professed faith -- in Christ and Joined -- the M. E. Church -- South August 1862. -- Was Married to P. T. -- ALLEN Sept 1st 1864 & Died with -- Full Assurance of a -- Blesed Immortality -- Aug 9th 1866.
