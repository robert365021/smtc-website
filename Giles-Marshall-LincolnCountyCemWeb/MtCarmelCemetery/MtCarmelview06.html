This view looks south over the more densely used portion of the cemetery.  

We are looking down the hill north west.  Further out is the box tombs of William & Nancy Williams & Moses Jones  
Box tombs which are easily picked out from here.  Gracy, Hanna & Warren are some of the surnames that occupy the lower section of the cemetery while the Jones, Paisley & Williams are in the mid section midways down the hill. The upper section is a scattering of most of the other surnames including Farris, Boyd,  Allen & McRee and others.
