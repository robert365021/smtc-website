FOWLER CEMETERY, (Collier Rd) - MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Fowler Cemetery. 

This road map shows the cemetery on the north side of Collier Road from off of Lynnville Hwy (Hwy 129)

This aerial map shows the cemetery marked with a red check., It looks here like it is under the trees but the cemetery is largely cleared. You are following a right of way off of Collier Rd to get here. It is well maintained & fenced. The road to the cemetery also doubles as the road to residential property and also to farm operations so be sure you go here with deliberate upscale intentions and secure permission if needed.
