WHITE (ELISHA) CEMETERY, WACO, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE, Waco to Yokley Rd.

 This map shows the coordinates of the White (Elisha) Cemetery. 

Location: Go Hwy 31 N, left into Yokley Road at Waco (now Old Lynnville), after about 500 yards and with advanced permission turn right into the old historic Elisha White house. The cemetery is behind the house about 400 yards in a concrete & steel cable enclosed fence. The cemetery is back next to the tree line

Here we can see the cemetery just at the tip of a tree line marked above with a red check.

Here we can see the cemetery clearer under the red check in the field but next to the tree line. I believe that alternate field road north of the cemetery leads one to the cemetery.
