HOPKINS CEMETERY, Beech Hill Rd., GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Hopkins Cemetery as GPS N 35.19755, W -86.97238. 

Location: Go east on Hwy 64 to Leatherwood Creek Rd N and turn left (north) and go to Beech Hill Rd. Turn right there and go about 300 yards and cross the bridge over Leatherwood creek and the driveway to the farm and cemetery is on your right. Mr Bart Henson owns the farm where the cemetery is located and can give you permission at 256-881-9389 to get past the locked gate.
