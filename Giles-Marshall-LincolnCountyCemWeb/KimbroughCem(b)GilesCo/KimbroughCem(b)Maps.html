KIMBROUGH CEMETERY (Crooked Hill Rd.), GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Kimbrough Cemetery just south and next to the Anthony Cemetery. 

Go south on Hwy 166 (Bethel Road) to the Crooked Hill Road and turn right. Go 4 tenths of a mile and look to your left upon the bank of the road for the Anthony Cemetery enclosed in a rock wall. Immediately south and across the fence will be the Kimbrough Cemetery for African American citizens. This cemetery still active.
This aerial view shows the Cemetery on the (east) left side of Crooked Hill Road under the trees. A yellow outline has been plotted around the two cemeteries. Merritt Branch Road shown here may be a private road so get here from Crook Hill Road.
