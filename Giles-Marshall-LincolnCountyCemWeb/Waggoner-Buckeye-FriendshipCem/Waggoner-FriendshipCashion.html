WAGGONER-FRIENDSHIP UNITED METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, MOORE COUNTY TENNESSEE

CASHION, James, 1823 - Mar 11, 1908. Son of Archibald Cashion and Letty Shelton.  Married to Mary Wade January 4, 1852.

Son of Archibald Cashion and Letty Shelton. He married Mary Wade January 4, 1852. They had seven known children: Sarah Jane, Johnathan H., Rebecca, Laura C. Jane, Viva G., Calena and Martha Evelyn.
His obituary appeared in the Fayetteville Observer on March 19, 1908, it read:
Mr. James Cashion died of lagrippe at Mulberry March 11, 1908, aged 85 years, was a brave and valiant confederate soldier. He was a member of the Lutheran Church. Funeral by Rev. R.L. Massey, burial at Waggoner graveyard.
