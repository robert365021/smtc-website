SHORES (ON HILL) CEMETERY, (On Hill above old Phillips-Shores Mill site) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

LOCKE, George Madison 9 Sep 1923 29 Sep 1991, (h/o Mary Frances Wilcoxson Locke; s/o Samuel Rollin and Bertha Mae Jones Locke)
Obituary:
Mr. George Madison Locke, 68, of Pulaski, died Sunday, Sept. 29, 1992, at Crockett General Hospital in Lawrenceburg. Funeral services were held Tuesday,
Oct. 1, at Carr and Erwin Chapel with Bernard Konig and Larry Wilcoxson officiating. Burial was in Old Shores Cemetery. A native of Tennessee, Mr. Locke
was the son of the late Sam and Bertha Jones Locke. He was a member of Shores Church of Christ and retired from the State of Tennessee Highway Department.
Survivors include his wife, Mary Frances Locke of Pulaski; one son, Danny Ray Locke of Pulaski; two daughters, Anita James of Pulaski, Sue Johnson o Corbin,
Ky.; five sisters, Mrs. Grady Glover and Mrs. Bob Johnson, both of Goodsprings, Mrs. Ruth Banner of Pulaski, Mrs. Bernice Cates of Lexington, Ala., Mrs. Fount
Webb of Lewisburg; four grandchildren; several nieces and nephews.

LOCKE, Mary Frances Wilcoxson 3 Sep 1925, (w/o George Madison Locke; d/o Newt and Minnie Lou Howard Wilcoxson)
