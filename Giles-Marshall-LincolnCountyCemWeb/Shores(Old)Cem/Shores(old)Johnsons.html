wife
JOHNSON, Eva Louise Locke 31 May 1913 26 Jan 1994, (w/o Bob Taylor Johnson; (d/o Samuel Rollin and Bertha Mae Jones Locke)

Obituary:
Mrs. Eva Louise Johnson, 80, died Jan. 26, 1994, at St. Thomas Hospital in Nashville. The resident of the Goodspring community was the daughter of the
late Sam and Bertha Mae Jones Locke. She was married to the late Bob Johnson.
Survivors include one son, Robert Johnson of Goodspring; two daughters, Mrs. Fred Thornton of Goodspring, Mrs. Bernard Konig of Grassy, Ala.; three sisters,
Cloie Webb of Lewisburg, Ruth Banner of Pulaski and Bernice Cates of Minor Hill; 11 grandchildren, 15 great-grandchildren, and one great-great-grandchild. Services
were held Friday at Carr and Erwin Chapel with Johnny Felker officiating. Burial was in Old Shores Cemetery.
