SHORES (ON HILL) CEMETERY, (On Hill above old Phillips-Shores Mill site) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

PHILLIPS, Lizzie Jones 2 Sep 1887 30 Sep 1980, (w/o Spencer Dixon Phillips; d/o Steve and Maggie Hester Jones)
Obituary:
Mrs. Lizzie Phillips, 93, of Route 6, Pulaski, died Tuesday, September 30 in Fairhaven Nursing Home. Funeral services were held Wednesday at 2:30 p.m.
at Carr and Erwin Chapel with interment in Shores Cemetery. The Rev. Clarence K. Stewart was the officiant. Mrs. Phillips, who was born in Tennessee, was the
daughter of the late Stevie and Maggie Hester Jones. Survivors include four sons, Roy Phillips, Goodsprings, Milus Phillips, Columbia, Da_cus Phillips, Springfield,
Ohio and Jimmy Phillips, Pulaski; four daughters, Mrs. Dorothy Arnold, Pontotoc, Miss., Mrs. Corine Keeter and Nina Cole, Lawrenceburg and Mrs. Marie Smith,
Pulaski; a sister, Mrs. Florence Rosson, Lawrenceburg; 25 grandchildren; 42 great-grandchildren and 12 great-great-grandchildren.
