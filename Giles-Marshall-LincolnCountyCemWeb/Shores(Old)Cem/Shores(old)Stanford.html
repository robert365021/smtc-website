SHORES (ON HILL) CEMETERY, (On Hill above old Phillips-Shores Mill site) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

Husband
STANFORD, James W. 26 May 1911 11 Apr 2000, (h/o Myrtle Leona Stockman Stanford; s/o Samuel Register and Zona Maude McDougal Stanford)
Obituary:
Mr. James William Stanford, 88, of Goodspring died April 11, 2000, at Hillside Hospital in Pulaski. A Giles County native, Mr. Stanford was born May 26, 1911,
and was the son of the late Samuel Register and Zona Maude McDougal Stanford. He was married to the late Myrtle Stanford. He was a retired farmer, a veteran of
the U. S. Army and was a member of Scotts Hill Baptist Church. Funeral services were held April 12 at Pettus-Turnbo Funeral Home Chapel in Lawrenceburg with
Marvin Nelson officiating. Burial was in Old Shores Cemetery in Goodspring.
Survivors include daughter Carolyn Smith of Goodspring; brother Winston Stanford of Pulaski; sister Quilla Tankersley of Pulaski; and two grandchildren.

Wife
STANFORD, Myrtle Leona 1 Sep 1909 8 Apr 1998 (w/o James W. Stanford; d/o Charles Madison and Nora Lee Phillips Stockman)
Obituary:
Mrs. Myrtle Leona Stockman Stanford, 88, of Goodspring died April 8, 1998, at NHC Health Care in Pulaski. Mrs. Stanford was a native of Giles County and the
daughter of the late Charles Madison and Nora Lee Phillips Stockman. She was a member of Scotts Hill Baptist Church. Services were held Friday at Pettus-
Turnbo Funeral Home in Lawrenceburg with Marvin Nelson officiating. Burial was in Old Shores Cemetery in Giles County. Survivors include her husband,
James W. Stanford of Goodspring; daughter Carolyn J. Smith of Goodspring; brother Cecil Stockman of Pulaski; sisters, Belle Knox of Knoxville, Bertie
Adcock of Birmingham, Ala.; and two grandchildren.
