SHORES (ON HILL) CEMETERY, (On Hill above old Phillips-Shores Mill site) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

PHILLIPS, Milus Milton 4 Mar 1919 2 Sep 1991, (s/o Spencer and Lizzie Jones Phillips; h/o Katherine Morris)
Obituary:
Mr. Milus Milton Phillips, 72, of Columbia, died Monday, Sept. 2, 1991, at his residence following several months of illness. Funeral services will be conducted
at 1 p.m. Wednesday, Sept. 4, at Oakes and Nichols Funeral Home with the Rev. James Sewell and the Rev. Richard Roe officiating. The Lawrence County native
was the son of the late Spencer D. and Lizzie Jones Phillips. He was a member of the Craft Memorial United Methodist Church where he had formerly served as
chairman of the Official Board. Serving in the United States Navy, he was a veteran of World War II. Mr. Phillips retired in 1975 from the Cookeville Post
Office and prior to that he was a railway mail clerk. He was also a member of the Chairmanï¿½s Club at First Farmers and Merchants Bank. Survivors include his wife,
Mrs. Katherine Morris Phillips of Columbia; one son, William Larry Phillips of Arlington, Texas; one daughter, Mrs. Joe {Patsy} Hedrick of Columbia; two
sisters, Mrs. Nina Lucille Cole of Charlotte, N. C., Mrs. Corine Keeter of Lawrenceburg; one brother, Jimmy Phillips of North Carolina and three grand-
children.
