wife
LUCURS, Mary Olga Tipper 5 Jul 1886 8 Oct 1982, (w/o Robert Neal Lucurs; (d/o George Washington and Nancy Louisa Tabitha Norwood Tipper)
Obituary:
Mrs. Mary Olga Lucurs, 96, of Rt. 2, Pulaski, died Friday, October 8, 1982, at Giles County Hospital. Funeral services were held at 3 p.m. Sun., Oct. 10 at Carr
& Erwin Chapel with burial in the Shores Cemetery, the Rev. Mack Pinkelton officiating. Mrs. Lucurs was the daughter of the late George Washington and Nannie L.
Norwood Tipper, and widow of the late Robert Neal Lucurs. She is survived by a son, Erskine Lucurs, Pulaski; three daughters, Mrs. Clemmie Liles, Pulaski, Mrs.
Velma Powell, Prospect, and Mrs. Stella Defoe, Fairhope, Alabama; 20 grandchildren; 39 great-grandchildren; and 14 great-great-grandchildren.
