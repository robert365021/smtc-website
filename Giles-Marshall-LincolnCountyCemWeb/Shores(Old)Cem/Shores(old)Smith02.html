SHORES (ON HILL) CEMETERY, (On Hill above old Phillips-Shores Mill site) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE

SMITH, Solomon Lewis 19 Nov 1917 - 24 Feb 1992, (h/o Ruthy Howell; s/o Charlie Daniel and Sarah Elizabeth Childress Smith)
Obituary:
Mr. Solomon Lewis Smith, 74, of Pulaski, died Monday, Feb. 24, 1992, at Maury Regional Hospital in Columbia. Funeral services were held Tuesday,
Feb. 25, at Carr and Erwin Chapel with Mack Pinkelton officiating. Burial was in Old Shores Cemetery. A native of Tennessee, Mr. Smith was the son of
the late Charlie and Sarah Elizabeth Childress Smith. He was a retired farmer.
Survivors include two brothers, Archie Smith of Pulaski, Cecil Smith of Michigan; four sisters, Polly Thurman and Jane Hunt, both of Oklahoma, Kay Shelton and Sue
Kolwaski, both of Oregon.
