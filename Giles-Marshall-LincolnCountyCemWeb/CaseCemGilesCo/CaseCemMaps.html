CASE CEMETERY, (Case Rd) GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS location of the Case Cemetery.  This cemetery is detailed on the USGS records. The GPS Latitude & Longitude is correct.


 Location: Highway 31 South; right into Prospect Road. Pass through Prospect and the road becomes Case Road, but still Hwy 273. After about 2 miles from the center of Prospect the cemetery can be seen in the yard of a house on the left. Inside a chain link fence.  
This Aerial view shows the house and cemetery under the trees. The cemetery is on the left or west side of the house inside a chain link fence. It appears to be well cared for.
