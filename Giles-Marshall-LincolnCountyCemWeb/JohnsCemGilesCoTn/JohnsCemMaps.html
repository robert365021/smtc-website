JOHNS-HAMLETT CEMETERY, Powell Chapel/Johns Cemetery Rd., GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Johns-Hamlett Cemetery as GPS N 35.22723, W -86.95561. 

 Location: Go northeast from Pulaski on Hwy 31/11 Alt to Powell Chapel Road and turn right. Follow that more than a mile and turn left on Johns Cemetery Road. Follow around continuing east until you see the cemetery on your left next to the road.
