WELLS (William) CEMETERY, Crescent view Road, S. side, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the William Wells (a Revolutionary War Soldier) Cemetery as N 35.14751 X W -87.01016.

Go south on Hwy 31 from Pulaski and cross over Hwy 64 bypass. Continue on for nearly two more miles and turn right (west) on Crescent View Road. Proceed about ï¿½ mile. and turn left (south) into the 1st driveway across Britton Branch for permission. From the house go across the pasture west to the clump of trees which contain the cemetery. That is about 250 yards nearly due west from the home. The cemetery is also very near Richard Johnston's house but to the east (but not on) his land.

This aerial & GPS map shows the William Wells Cemetery under the Hackberry tree lined cemetery. It is out in a field in on the west side of a home and actually closer to Mr. Johnston's home on the east side. This is near Britton Branch Creek which I am sure served as a source of water for the Wells Family.
