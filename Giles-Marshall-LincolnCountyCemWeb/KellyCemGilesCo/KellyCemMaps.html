KELLY CEMETERY, (Prospect Rd) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Kelly Cemetery. 

From Pulaski go south on Hwy 31 to Elkton and turn right on Prospect Road (Hwy 273). Go about three miles and cross the bridge and with permission turn right (north) up a private Farm Operation Road. Go past the Silos and the cemetery is on the right side of the metal building complex.

This aerial map shows the cemetery well maintained. The road to the cemetery also doubles as the road to an unused residence and also to the farm operation so besure you go here with deliberate upscale intentions and secure permission as there are expensive assets here that would arouse suspicion without deliberation with the owner.
