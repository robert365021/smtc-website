SOLOMON CEMETERY (Marty Rd, E. of Fayetteville) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Solomon Cemetery as N 35.15746 X W -86.54028.

Location: From the bypass & Hwy 64 to east on Hwy 64 to Marty Rd and follow that for 1/2 mile and the cemetery is behind a house in the woods about 200 yards into the woods Southwest of the house.
