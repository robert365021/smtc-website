HOUSTON CEMETERY, (I-65, Exit 32, Globe Rd) - MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
Mapping the Location

Two Revolutionary War Soldiers, Daniel Bills & Christopher Houston are buried here, including Archibald McConnell who was in the battle of New Orleans 1815. This is one of the oldest graveyards in Marshall County Tennessee.
