The Toad - a rare sighting - hunkered down almost invisible on the shoulder of the Lucinda Rowell memorial. This is a rare sighting of a Tree Toad, much less common than other frogs. This is the only one I have seen in my life time. He climbs like a tree frog and has the color of a ground toad, but is much smaller. Shall I name him the Rock Toad for the way he blends into the background of a rock? - One of those do not disturb moments in photography - CWA. 7 Oct 2010)  5594C,5594
