HOWELL CEMETERY, MAURY/GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE LINE


NORMAN, Seley C.,  Feb 23, 1858 - July 23, 1880. "She was too good, too gentle and fair to dwell in this cold world of care". 
Seley C. (Howell) Norman born Feb. 23, 1858 died July 23, 1880 (Sometimes spelled Celey or Cecelia. She was the daughter of William Perry Howell (buried at McCains Cem.) and his first wife Manerva Foster. William Perry Howell was the son of Major C. Howell. Seley C. Howell married Benjamin Franklin Norman.
