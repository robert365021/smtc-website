RODES-CLIFTON PLACE CEMETERY, Annie Wade Lane, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This is the GPS coordinates of the Rodes-Clifton Place Cemetery at N 35.26965 X -87.07434
Take the Hwy 31 entrance to Annie Wade Lane or the Hwy 166 entrance. Either way you will wind up at the two gates which are usually locked and impassable. Do not go here without advanced permission from the Owens Family.


The gates happened to be open here to the work side of the farm and main gated entrance. It was all locked when I was there.
