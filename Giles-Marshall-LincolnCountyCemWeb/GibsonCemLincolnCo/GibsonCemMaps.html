GIBSON CEMETERY, Bledsoe Road, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Gibson Cemetery as N 35.35757 X W -86.58727.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 431 to Petersburg & turn right (East) on Hwy 129. Follow that several miles and turn north on Bledsoe Road and follow that for about 2 miles and look on the bank of the road after passing Hannah Gap Church on the right and you will see the cemetery. This is just before a residence also on the right.
