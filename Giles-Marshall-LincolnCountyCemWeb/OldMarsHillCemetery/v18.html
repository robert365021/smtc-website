OLD MARS HILL CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE (129) RD, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

Standing just south of the last photo this is the back (north) side of the cemetery looking northwest at the massive but somewhat broken monuments of the Black, Davis, Kincaid and other families who occupied this area in the early to mid 1800s. Someone at one time spent a significant part of their life savings to have these monument erected and we allow them to be trashed like this. We should be ashamed. This is just as much a cemetery as the nice nearby city cemetery.
Photo & info by Wayne Austin 12/29/2005.
