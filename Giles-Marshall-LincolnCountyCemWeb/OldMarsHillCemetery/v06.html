OLD MARS HILL CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE (129) RD, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

This is the south area of the cemetery looking south at the road. For every tall stone there is a smaller footstone usually to the east. Notice in these photos how short footstones without a tall headstone. Naturally this means a lost inscription because the footstone only contains most often only contains the initials.
