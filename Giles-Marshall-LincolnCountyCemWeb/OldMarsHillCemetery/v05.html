OLD MARS HILL CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE (129) RD, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE


Trees are continuously springing up and growing massive and then dying. They then come crashing down on the stones burying them or smashing them. Their roots also push up the stones and cause them to fall. So yes, uncontrolled growth of trees is bad for a cemetery.
