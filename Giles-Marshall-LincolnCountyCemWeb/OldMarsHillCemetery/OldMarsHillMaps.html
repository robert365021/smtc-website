OLD MARS HILL CEMETERY, LYNNVILLE (129) RD, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE

The GPS of this cemetery is 35.3771�N X 86.8903�W as shown on this map.

Find this cemetery by exiting onto Hwy 129 from Interstate 65. Go west toward Lynnville for about 1/4 mile and turn north on Mars Hill Road at the Mars Hill Church of Christ. The Mars Hill Church Cemetery is immediately on the left, but go past Mars Hill Church and cemetery about 1/2 mile. There the road will bend sharply to the left. The cemetery is in the bend on the north (right) side of the road in a grove of trees. Map from MSN Virtual Earth. Modified for this site.
