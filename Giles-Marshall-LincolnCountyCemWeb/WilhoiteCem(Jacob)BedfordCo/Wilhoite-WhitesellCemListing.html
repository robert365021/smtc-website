Jacob Wilhoite, b. 1751, native of Orange Co., NC, built first mill here in Bedford Co known as the German Mill ca 1812. Operated it for several years and it was sold and then the mill washed away in a flood of the Duck River.
 

Photography & compilation by C. Wayne Austin 8 Mar 2003. Publication here 11 Feb 2011 using the most latest & most accurate information available including the research of Jacob Wilhoite deceased about year 2000 and his wife whom we have to thank for much of the accomplishments & knowledge of this cemetery including the monument & survey itself. There is a book that supports this cemetery upkeep called Ancestors and Descendents of Jacob Wilhoite including a revised addition. We also make reference to the book Bedford County Tennessee Cemeteries by the Marsh couple.
