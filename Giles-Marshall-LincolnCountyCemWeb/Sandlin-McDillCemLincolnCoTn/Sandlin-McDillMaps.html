SANDLIN-McDILL CEMETERY (yukon Rd), LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Sandlin-McDill Cemetery as N 35.08635 X W -86.64256.

Location: From Fayetteville go south on Hwy 231 and turn left on Ardmore Hwy (110) and follow that to McMillan Spur (nearly in Kirkland) which become Camargo Rd. Go abt 1 1/2 miles and turn left on Yukon Rd. Travel then about 500 yards and you must negotiate with the neighbors to find this cemetery using a GPS. Basically though Yukon Rd will slightly bend to the right after abt 3 tenths of a mile then a little further on begin to bend back to the left. When the road bends back left stop & walk due north about 2/3 of a mile along what becomes a tree lined hedgerow or fence row. Just before you arrive at the cemetery you will cross another fence row spanning east/west. The cemetery is just north of that fence row still in the fence row you have been following. Don't forget permission and notification of the land owners of your intentions.
