BRADSHAW BAPTIST CHURCH /CENTER POINT CEMETERY, GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
From the above map we see the Bradshaw-Centerpoint Cemetery GPS location. It is across the road from the Bradshaw Baptist Church.

Location: Take Highway 31 Alternate northeast out of Pulaski for about 8 miles and turn right at Hopkins Rd which almost immediately merges into Blackburn Road at Brick Church. Turn south on Blackburn Road. At the Blackburn Road and Clock Creek Road merge right and go about 3 more miles. When the Blackburn Road terminates at Beech Hill Road (aka Old Hwy 15) turn left and after another 2/3 of a mile the Beech Hill Church of Christ will be on your right. Go under Interstate 65 & travel another two miles and the Church will be on your left and the cemetery will be on your right.
This Aerial Map shows the plot where the cemetery resides next to Rail Lines. Beech Hill/Bradshaw/Centerpoint Road goes under Interstate Highway 65 back to the west two miles from here. The cemetery is across the road in front of the Bradshaw Baptist Church as can be seen on the above aerial map. Travel to this place out of respect and reverence for those gone before. Come here for less than honorable reasons and you will be strung up to the nearest tree (figurately speaking, unless I catch you) by the overseers & local law.
