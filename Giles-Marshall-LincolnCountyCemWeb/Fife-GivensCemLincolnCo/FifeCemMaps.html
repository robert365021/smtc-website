FIFE-GIVENS CEMETERY, McDeal Rd., LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Fife Cemetery as N 35.09850 X W 86.63531.

Location: From Taft Tennessee go east toward Fayetteville about 5 miles and turn north on Carmago Road. Follow that until it comes to the Carmago Church of Christ where the road becomes McDeal Road. Follow that another mile and look right upon the bank of the road at a tree and under that is the three fallen & broken box tombs and two upright monuments, one no longer complete enough to be readable. The cemetery is between two driveways about 500 yards apart.

This aerial map shows the cemetery on the right of the road after one passes a private drive.
