COMMONS, Joseph, born about 1786 & died 26 Feb 1858, age about 72.(no marked stones found. This cemetery is considered unmarked, but past records tell us the Joseph Commons family at least some of them who lived here at least until the late 1850s are interred here.)

No marked graves have ever been reported for this cemetery. Boulders cover graves. Piles of Rocks abound. Supposed to be a stone wall here, but if so it has fallen and no longer recognizable. I did not make photos of every plot I saw in this place but examined it to the extent that I am certain there are no visible marked stones.

Photographed on a sunny day 25 Oct 2014. The inscriptions were updated with exceptions found or known by C. Wayne Austin. This publication culminated from various sources. Listed on page 44 of the book Lincoln County County Cemeteries by Tim & Helen Marsh, abt 1978. Added here 14 Mar 2015.
