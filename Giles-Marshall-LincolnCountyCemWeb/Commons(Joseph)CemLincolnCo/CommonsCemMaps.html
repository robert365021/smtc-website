COMMONS (Joseph) CEMETERY (Boonshill Rd.) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Joseph Commons Cemetery as N 35.18733 X W -86.64783.

 Location: From Fayetteville go west on Hwy 64 and turn north (right) on L. Pigg Lane. When this road dead ends on Boonshill Rd turn left adn go past Kidd Road coming in from the left and begin to climb a hill. Look to your right adn there will be two large trees covering the graveyard. One of them if still alive will be a Cedar Tree. Privy Hedge a stubby shrub has taken over this cemetery making it hard to navigate.

In this view the cemetery is seen just to your right on the edge of the road. Supposed to be several unmarked graves here, but without a clearing it is hard to tell.
