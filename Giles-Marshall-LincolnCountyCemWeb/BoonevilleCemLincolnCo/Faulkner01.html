FAULKNER, Hubert W., 1904 - (19 Dec) 1990. (Lincoln Co. native; s/o late Tom & Fannie Groce Falkner h/o and shares stone with Naoma R. Faulkner.) 0800


Your Community Shopper - January 2, 1991

Hubert Faulkner

Hubert Wilson Faulkner, 86, died December 19, 1990, in the Lincoln Regional Hospital, Fayetteville, TN.

Funeral services were held December 21 in the chapel of the Gallant Funeral Home with Rev. Tom Porter officiating. Burial was in the Booneville Cemetery beside his late wife, Noama Faulkner, who died in 1986. Gallant Funeral Home was in charge of arrangements.

A native of Lincoln County, TN, Mr. Faulkner was a member of the Baptist church. He was a retired carpenter and the son of the late Tom Faulkner and Fannie Groce Faulkner.

Survivors include one son, Jay W. Faulkner of Brooklyn, MI; four daughters, Doris Adkison of Lunchburg, TN, Carolyn Bankston of Fayetteville, TN, Shirley Galati of Vero Beach, FL, and Eloise Lynn of Birmingham, AL; one brother, Calvin Faulkner of Fayetteville, TN; four sisters, Mattie Pitts and Carrie Rozar both of Fayetteville, Arcil Phelps and Velma Bradford both of Nashville, TN; 20 grandchildren and 19 great-grandchildren
