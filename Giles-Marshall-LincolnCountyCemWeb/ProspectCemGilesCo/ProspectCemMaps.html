PROSPECT CEMETERY (Prospect, Hwy 273) GILES COUNTY, TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Prospect Cemetery known as the Latitude & Longitude
To get here go south on Hwy 31 to Elkton and turn right into the Elkton into Prospect Rd (Hwy 273) on to Prospect. In Prospect after going down the hill go straight ahead across the intersection and start up the hill. Turn left up the hill into the Cemetery under the oval overhead sign. Do not  go here if your intentions are not honorable. You will be unwelcome. Punishment is meted liberally to violators of the law

This aerial map shows the cemetery upon a high plateau above Prospect - west and north of town in a beautiful setting of Magnolia trees.
