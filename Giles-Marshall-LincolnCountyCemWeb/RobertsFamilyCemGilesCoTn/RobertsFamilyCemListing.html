CASHION, Elizabeth Wilkerson, 1785 -1881 born in Chesterfield Co., Va. Death in Giles County Tn; d/o Mackerness "Mack" Wilkerson and Mary Jackson Wilkerson. w/o Samuel Cashion. Mother of Eliza Cashion Flippen and Harriet Cashion Roberts (Elizabeth is buried here - grave unmarked)

ROBERTS, Harriett Cashion, 25 Oct 1804 - 18 Apr 1850. Member M.E. Church; 23 years, died suddenly, 1st w/o William Bachelor Roberts; d/o Samuel and Elizabeth Cashion. Inscribed as: Here rest the remains of Harriet Roberts, born 25th Oct 1804 and Died (suddenly) April 18th 1850. She was a consistent member of the M.E. Church 23 years. ROBERTS, Mary Ann Moore, (no dates; 2nd wife of William Bachelor Roberts married 27 Oct 1850. (Grave unmarked)

ROBERTS, William Bachelor, (no dates, h/o 1st Harriett Cashion; 2nd wife - Mary Ann Moore, married  27 Oct 1850, in Giles Co., Tenn.)  (Grave unmarked, but evidence exist showing there was a tombstone here at one time.)
