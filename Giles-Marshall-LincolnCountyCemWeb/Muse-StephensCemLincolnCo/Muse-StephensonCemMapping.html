MUSE-STEPHEN CEMETERY, Bledsoe Road, LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Muse-Stephenson Cemetery as N 35.33843 X W -86.57377.

Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 431 to Petersburg & turn right (East) on Hwy 129. Follow that several miles and turn north on Bledsoe Road and follow that for about 1 & 1/2 miles and turn right into Poor Grab/Chestnut Ridge Rd. After a few hundred yards look for the Bledsoe aka Poor Grab Church of Christ building on the left and there is a field road on the left side of the church that takes you to the old house & cemetery but the cemetery is in a clump of trees before you get to the house but on the left. The road takes you thru a gate that must be kept closed. After passing thru relatch the gate. Cemetery is about 4 football fields north but on the left after turning into the church road.
