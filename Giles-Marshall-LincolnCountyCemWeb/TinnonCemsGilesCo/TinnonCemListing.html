TINNON (Robert) CEMETEREY, Hill above African Hollow, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
Highway 31 North about 6 miles & turn right into African Hollow. Cem at the top of the hill. GPS: N 35.27336, W -87.02859on the Chestnut Ridge Farm of Bill and Jami Myers (2015)

TINNON, Robert, born in North Carolina, 20 Jan 1798, moved to Tennessee 1806; married Betsy Abernathy 1818. died 13 March 1862. 7012/7013/7013C

ABERNATHY CEMETERY (Black), African Hollow, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
Location: Highway 31N at Riversburg; turn right into African Hollow, pass A. M. E. Church (not sure it exist today). Could this be the cemetery mapped on NCIS as the Reynolds Cemetery in African Hollow. If the same as Reynolds Cem: GPS: N 35.26551, W -87.02550, but it is not the same as the Reynolds Cemetery.
