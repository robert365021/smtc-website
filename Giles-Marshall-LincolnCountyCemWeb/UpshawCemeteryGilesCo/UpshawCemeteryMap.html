UPSHAW MEMORIALS, INTERSTATE 65 North, REST STOP, Mile Marker 2, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE


This cemetery is on Interstate 65 North, Rest Stop, Mile Marker 2. It is the first rest stop going north after passing the Alabama/Tennessee Line. The yellow arrow depicts the spot you will find these three memorial stones.
