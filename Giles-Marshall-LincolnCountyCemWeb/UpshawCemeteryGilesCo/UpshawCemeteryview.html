UPSHAW MEMORIALS, INTERSTATE 65 North, REST STOP, Mile Marker 2, GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE

This is a area view of the memorials involved. I believe the stone on the right is the base for the Dr. Upshaw marker on the left. The middle stone is a top of a Box tomb with stones that form it into a box, assembled under it when present. The white car in the photo is traveling at least 70 miles per hour north on I-65. Photo made looking northwest at I-65.


This memorial also on the same I-65 Rest Stop.
TENNESSEE AMVETS DEDICATES THIS MEMORIAL TO ALL VETERANS LIVING OR DECEASED WHO HAVE HONORABLY SERVED THEIR COUNTRY THAT FREEDOM MAY REIGN.
APRIL 4, 1998. 
GOVERNOR 
DON SUNDQUIST.
