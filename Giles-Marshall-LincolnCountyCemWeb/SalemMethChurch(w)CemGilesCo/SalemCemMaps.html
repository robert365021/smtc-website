SALEM METHODIST CHURCH CEMETERY, (Big Dry Creek Rd) - GILES COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the coordinates of the Salem Cemetery. 

This road map shows the cemetery on the east side of Big Dry Creek Road. This is about a mile south of Campbellsville, Tennessee on Big Dry Creek Road.

This aerial map shows the cemetery under the trees, Both sections are on the slopes of the hill on the east side of the road. A large Gulley separates the two sections. The south section mentions the Salem Methodist Church Members.
