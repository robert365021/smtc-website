BARRETT CARLISLE CEMETERY, Robinson Rd, BEDFORD COUNTY TENNESSEE

This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Barrett-Carlisle Cemetery. GPS 35,39210 X W -86.51546. Located on Robinson Road. East of Pleasant Grove south of the road in a fence row behind a brick home

Go south from Shelbyville on Hwy 231 pass Center Church Road access on your left and keep on until you get to the Robinson Road access on the right. Turn right (northwest) there and the road will bend back left or (almost due west) and a brick home will be on your left after you travel about 1/4 mile further. Go into the driveway and the owner will direct you to behind their house in a hedge row that spans north/south directly behind their home about 200 yards. There was at one time a dedicated field road to the cemetery but it has faded into the dust.
