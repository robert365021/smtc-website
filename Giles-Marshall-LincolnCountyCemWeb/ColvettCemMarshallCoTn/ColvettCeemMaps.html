COLVETT CEMETERY, Mooresville Road, MARSHALL COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map plots GPS Coordinates of the Colvett (family) Cemetery in the southwest corner of Marshall County Tennessee as N 35.40399 X W -86.90210.

From Exit 27 I-65 take the exit and go west toward Lynnville more than a mile and turn north (right) on Mooresville Rd. Follow that as it snakes around for more than 2 miles and the cemetery is across the road from, and upon a ridge top, in a tree lined fence & hedge row, about 100 yards west of the road.
