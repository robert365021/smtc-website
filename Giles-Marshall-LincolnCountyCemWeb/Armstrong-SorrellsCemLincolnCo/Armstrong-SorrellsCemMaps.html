ARMSTRNG-SORRELLS CEMETERY (Union Hollow Rd) LINCOLN COUNTY TENNESSEE
This map shows the GPS coordinates of the Armstrong-Sorrells Cemetery as N 35.28328 X W -86.58196. It is just north of a giant Beech tree next to a utility easement on the slope of a hill.

 Location: From Fayetteville go north on Hwy 231 several miles to Cold Spring Road and turn left or northwest. Travel that to Union Hollow Road and turn right or northeast. After about 500 yards look to your left at a gate and after going thru the gate with permission turn west and follow the utility easement (smaller power poles and lines) to the giant beech tree and look for the small broken stones and fieldstones marking this cemetery.

In this view the cemetery is just this side of the tree trunk and actually includes the area of the tree where you will find field rocks all around the tree, but mostly to the north. The two marked stones are about 30 feet north of the tree trunk among other field stones marking graves.
